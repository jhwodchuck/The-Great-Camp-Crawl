# Quality Bar

A strong final venue record should have:

- clear overnight or residential evidence
- recent-activity evidence within the last 24 months
- a specific venue or session location
- a canonical source URL
- a basic taxonomy assignment
- at least one evidence URL in front matter
- a short human-readable summary

A great final venue record should also have:

- pricing URL or pricing summary
- age or grade range
- duration or session structure
- contact details
- operator identity
- useful parent notes

When uncertain, preserve the record with explicit gaps rather than hallucinating details.

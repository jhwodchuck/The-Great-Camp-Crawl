Dates and Rates

2026 Summer Camp Dates and Rates

Camp Wekeela consists of 280 campers ages 7-16 each session, a total of around 450 campers for the entire summer. Wekeela is a diverse community with campers and staff from over 37 U.S. States and 33 countries. Wekeela's mission is that every camper has a role model as a counselor. This is an important pillar of our camp.

Campers can attend Wekeela for a variety of session lengths (full session, first session, second session) and first time campers can choose from any of those options or a two week rookie session. The size of our camp was carefully selected to ensure campers experience the maximum benefit from programming and activities while also enjoying an intimate camp setting, where people truly know each other. Camp Wekeela is an environment where campers can foster incredible and worthwhile friendships, no matter the time length or how many summers they have been with us.

We are a nut-aware campus and offer specialty diet meals for campers & staff with dietary restrictions.

Visitors Day (New Format for 2026!)

Visitors Day (First and Full Session Campers) is Friday, July 17th.

We are excited to share some new procedures in 2026 for the end of the first session and Visitors Day, designed to create a more positive experience for both parents and campers.

To help streamline logistics and reduce travel, the final campfire of the first session, as well as the sibling overnight, will now take place on Thursday, July 16th.

  • For first session campers whose parents are unable to join us in Maine for Visitors Day, departures will be on the morning of Friday, July 17th.
  • For full session parents, we invite you to join us for Visitors Day on Friday, July 17th, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. First session parents have the option to join us and depart with their campers that afternoon.

These new procedures are in place to save parents from making two separate trips to camp and from staying two nights in Maine. Although we hope you'll spend a lot of time in Vacationland!

Methods of Payment

MasterCard, Visa and American Express are accepted with an additional fee of 2.65%. We accept payments by checks drawn on American banks only, or by travelers’ cheques. The enrollment form provides space for supplying all necessary charge card information. Please be sure to include the camper's name in all transactions. Wire transfers accepted as well for an additional fee of $50. Please observe the following payment schedule: $2,000 deposit per camper accompanying completed enrollment forms. Subsequent payments due on December 1, March 1, with balance due by May 1.

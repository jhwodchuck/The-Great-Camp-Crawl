Camp UTABA

Our Vision
 

Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Wasatch Mountains, shielded from the harsh clamor of the city by a majestic mantle of pine, is a small retreat area that has been set aside by the Utah Association of American Baptists Churches (UAABC) for Worship, Renewal and Revival.

The cornerstone of UTABA is the words, CARING, RENEWAL, and WHOLENESS. UTABA strives to be a center where you can come for many different activities or simply do nothing at all. Whether through our regular program of camping each Summer or if you want to have your own group camp or time alone, UTABA seeks to meet your needs.

What’s New?

In 2023, Camp UTABA was blessed with multiple donations of money, time, and material to improve camping experiences for our guests. That continued in 2024 with the addition of our new gazebo. We invite you to explore what has changed at Camp UTABA!

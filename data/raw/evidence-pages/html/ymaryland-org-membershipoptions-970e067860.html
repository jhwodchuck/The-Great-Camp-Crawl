Membership Types and Rates

Rates effective as of September 1, 2025 for new memberships

A one-time charge that is applied during sign-up for a new Y membership. New is defined as not having a Y membership in the last 60 days.

$74 - All Family and Two-Person Membership options
$49 - Individual and Senior Adult Membership options
$25 - Y Open Door recipients and Y Community School families

As a leading nonprofit committed to strengthening our community, we are proud to waive the joining fee and offer a 10% discount off of our monthly membership rate for those selflessly serving our community in the professions below. Simply bring in the appropriate proof of employment or service (noted below) to any Y family center.

  • U.S. Armed Forces (active duty)
    Proof of military service
  • First Responders (police, firefighters, EMS)
    Proof of current employment such as a recent pay stub

Up to four adults plus all dependent children age 26 and under residing at the same address. (Ten person limit.)*

Any individual age 13-26.

Any individual age 27-64.

Any two people, at least one age 18 or older, residing at the same address.

Y Member Handbook

By becoming a Y member or participating in Y programs, services and events, you are agreeing to follow all Y in Central Maryland rules, policies and guidelines posted in our online member handbook, all of which are upheld in our Y family centers to help ensure the health, safety and well-being of Y members, volunteers, guests, and associates.

Application Process

List of 3 items.

  • Step 1. Create an account

    In order to apply to Shawnigan Lake School, please complete the following:

    Create an account to complete the online application form

    Upload the following supporting documents:
    • Final report card from previous school year
    • Most recent report card
    • Passport
    • English proficiency test (e.g. IELTS general, TOEFL junior/iBt, Duolingo, etc.) for students whose first language is not English
      *In case a test score is not submitted, we reserve the right to schedule an internal English assessment with the applicant if needed*
    • If applicants take the Duolingo test, they can simply share the result with us online. Please find more information about the Duolingo English Test and how to share the results with us here.
    Email English and Math teacher recommendation forms (link will be provided once application has been submitted)

    Pay $300 application fee

    Once we receive the online application, one of our Admissions Associates will be in touch with the next steps.  
     

  • Step 2. Interview and Campus Visit

    Interviews are conducted when the family arranges to come visit our beautiful campus. It is in the applicant's best interest to visit the School for an in-person interview, but Zoom interviews are considered on a case-by-case basis.

  • Step 3. Decision

    Decisions are made after a thorough assessment of the applicant. We stress that this assessment is based on academic performance and potential, extra-curricular skills and accomplishments, integrity and suitability for the boarding experience. 

    Requests for financial aid (Founder's Awards) will only be considered after a student is accepted.

TRUTH. GOODNESS. BEAUTY.

Legendary Lodge camps seek to engage, equip, and serve the young disciples of Montana and beyond. Located on Salmon Lake near the southern end of the gorgeous Seeley Swan Valley, campers embark on a 5-day summer camp experience filled with prayer, fellowship, and outdoor adventure where they will make memories that will last a lifetime.

Legendary Lodge serves approx. 600 youth per season across six camp weeks, with three different grade levels: High School, Junior High, and 5th and 6th Grade.

Daily camp activities include canoeing, hiking, swimming, archery, hatchet throwing, various field games, and team building obstacle courses.

Young people dive deeper into their Catholic faith through Daily Mass, Reconciliation, evening sessions, prayer experiences, and small group discussion.

Each season is staffed by a team made up of incredible college-aged students from around the country, though many hail from our Montana colleges.

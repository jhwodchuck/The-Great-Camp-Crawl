BioFORCE 2014 Summer Academies Success Thanks to Partners

NCTM’s summer STEM programs for high school students and teachers (“BioFORCE”) were a resounding success this year thanks to our industry and academic partners! The entire NCTM team would like to applaud and extend our deepest appreciation to all the organizations and their staff who took time during their busy schedules to tour and speak to our students and teachers. In alphabetical order:  Baylor College of Medicine – National School of Tropical Medicine; Caliber Biotherapeutics; InGeneron, Inc.; Kalon Biotherapeutics, LLC; Lonza Houston, Inc.; Methodist Hospital Research Institute; National Center for Electron Beam Research; Sacchettini Structural Biology Laboratory; Texas A&M Institute for Genomic Medicine; Texas A&M Institute for Preclinical Studies; Texas Workforce Commission; and Thermo Fisher Scientific/Life Technologies.

Building Artificial Intelligence Camp

In partnership with Byte Camp, this Building Artificial Intelligence Camp will inspire the next generation of problem-solvers. Learn how modern AI and Machine Learning systems are built from the ground up. 

Participants will build code, design and train their own models to solve a host of tricky problems. Discover what lies "under the hood" of Large Language Models like ChatGPT and other generative and classical AI systems that are so rapidly changing the world around us. The final project will be a collaboratively built AI tackling a challenge of the group's choosing. 

Our AI Builders will:

  • Work with large data sets
  • Use Python to analyze data
  • Explore PyTorch machine learning libraries
  • Learn the basics of Machine Learning for prediction and image recognition
  • Understand the fundamentals of Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Practice the full AI development cycle: planning, data collection, data cleaning, training, testing, reinforcement, and improvement
  • Build custom AI models from scratch

Some Python coding experience is required. English level must be Advanced.

Meet the provider, Byte Camp

Byte Camp, we make creative technology education fun for kids! With 20 years of experience offering quality programs in 3D Modelling, Animation, Coding, Music Production, Video Editing, and more, in their after-school and summer camps. They are a frequent partner with SMUS Camps due to their high-quality programming, specialist staff and playful nature. 

 Byte Camp U programs are a step up from other Byte Camp Programs. Specifically designed for learners aged 13-17 to advance their learning. The Byte Camp U specialist team is ready to teach beyond the basics and delve deep into topics.

WHO WE ARE

Experiential education for young people, promoting the natural world and the betterment of human character.

Located in Western North Carolina our residential summer camp and semester boarding school provide transformative educational, wilderness, and cultural adventures. For over 94 years the Foundation has centered on fostering profound individual development, connection to the natural world and responsible action to our local and global communities. Eagle’s Nest Foundation is a 501c3 non-profit educational organization whose programs are designed for young people ages 6-18.

Tamahay Camp for Girls

Fun…with purpose!                                        

For girls ages 7 to 16, Tamahay Camp for Girls is full of adventure: making new friends, appreciating nature, and trying new things. Our program is based on the premise that young people enjoy learning, doing, and being self-sufficient. They do this with the assurance of adult reinforcement.

Building strong, independent women        

Camp life at Tamahay Camp for Girls gives girls the opportunity to live and play in a supportive community. They are away from the pressures of everyday life and the distractions of modern technology and social media. Each camper gains confidence as she learns new skills and makes new friends. She knows that her contribution to the community is important. Campers learn to work together, even as they become fore independent and self-reliant.

Individual attention                                      

Tamahay Camp for Girls accepts only 36 campers per session. Our small size means that every counselor and administrative staff member knows each camper. This allows us to guide each girl into a very satisfying camp experience.

Privately owned and operated since 1969

Tamahay Camp for Girls was founded with the intent of providing a quality camp experience at an affordable price. We believe all young people deserve the opportunity to experience and learn to love nature. Girls need to be able to explore new activities in a nonjudgmental atmosphere that encourages adventure and inspires confidence.

Tamahay Camp for Girls is not a country club for kids. The weeks spent here may be the nearest thing to “roughing it” that some of our campers will ever know. Living in the out-of-doors teaches campers to care for the environment while appreciating and enjoying all that nature has to offer.

Former campers have told us that Tamahay Camp for Girls is more than a place. It is a feeling of accomplishment and satisfaction that goes with each camper. It gives her an inner peace from which she will draw for the rest of her life.

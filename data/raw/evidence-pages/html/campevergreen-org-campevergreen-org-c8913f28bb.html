CAMP EVERGREEN IS A

wilderness-centered Christian camp located in the North Georgia mountains.

WHO WE ARE

God’s creation, unplugged.
Nestled in the North Georgia mountains along a creek, we provide campers with the opportunity to escape to nature, experience adventure and connect with God’s creation.

Feels like family.
Since 1999, family-owned-and-operated Camp Evergreen has welcomed thousands of kids and teens to experience the joy of summer camp.

Everything that you need, nothing that you don’t.
From daily worship and playing in Moccasin Creek to silly, wild evening games—and everything in between—we are intentionally small and wholeheartedly believe in good, old-fashioned, unplugged fun.

Join Us Next Summer

A camp where kids thrive in small groups, connect with Christ and embrace the beauty of the outdoors.

Overnight Camp

Ranging from 4- to 14-day sessions for rising 2nd through 9th graders, these sleepaway camp offerings allow campers to experience 18 wilderness-based daily activities and 50+ unique and silly choice time and “wild time” adventures offered throughout the summer. See For Yourself →

Why Camp Evergreen?

At Camp Evergreen, kids unplug, build confidence and experience hands-on learning in nature. With Christ-centered values and a close-knit community, we create life-changing experiences that your child will cherish forever.

Overview

Camp Hook Spit at Rusty Hook Ranch in Matagorda, Texas, is a week-long overnight fishing camp for 12 to 17-year-olds of all skill levels. Combining adventure, education, and fun, Camp Hook Spit is the ultimate experience for young anglers. Hosted by Hook Spit Junior Anglers, campers fish with professional guides and enjoy other activities like skeet shooting, volleyball, and more in a safe environment. With only 25 spots available, securing your camper’s spot is crucial—so book today!

Specialty camp focus: Fishing

Unique Features

  • Campers participate and compete in a number of activities from like bass fishing, sport shooting, volleyball, and more.
  • Learn from seasoned experts with 50 years of collective experience in hunting, fishing, and guiding.
  • Small enrollment size of 25 campers ensures personalized attention and mentoring.
  • All campers receive a fishing gear package valued at over $750.

ONE-WEEK SESSIONS


ONE-WEEK ROOKIE SESSIONS (June 28th -July 4th and August 9th-August 15th)
Camp Lohikan operates a traditional eight-week summer camp program that extends from June 28th through August 22nd. Campers can enroll for the entire eight-week program or have the option of enrolling for one of our 2, 4 or 6 week sessions. In addition to these sessions, we have two One-Week Rookie Sessions which operate during the first week and seventh week of our traditional summer camp season (June 28th-July 4th and August 9th-August 15th). Children ages 6 through 15 are welcome to attend one of the rookie sessions.

The program operated during Rookie Week is our traditional camp program. Our 65+ Arts, Sports and Adventure Activities are in operation on a daily basis and available to our rookie campers. Essentially, rookie campers experience our traditional camp program for 7 days. It’s designed for campers who want to experience the "feel" of the traditional camp but are hesitant about making more than a week-long commitment. Campers who want to extend their stay for a second week after their rookie session are welcome to do so.

The One-Week Rookie Sessions are limited in terms of numbers. Campers are housed in cabins with counselors and other campers who are enrolled for a regular camp session. Campers participate fully in our summer camp program which features 65+ arts, sports and adventure activities! It’s a great introduction to the camp experience!


ONE-WEEK MINI-CAMP PROGRAMS (June 21st-June 28th and August 22nd-August 28th)
The week before and the week after the operation of our eight week traditional program we operate a special One-Week Mini-Camp Program. The Mini-Camp Program differs from our eight-week program in that it’s a smaller camper population (approximately 160 children) and the activity offerings are not as diverse. Mini-Camp does not offer gymnastics, drama, cheerleading, circus activities like flying trapeze and the inter-camp games program is not in operation. However, because of the smaller camp population, activities like quad riding, jet skiing and horseback riding (more than one period a week) are activities that are offered at no additional charge.

  • Mini-Camp is specifically designed to give first-time campers a taste of the ultimate sleep-away camp experience! While it is a great way to start sleep-away camp, we have campers that return to mini-camp summer after summer. We also have campers of all ages join us during mini-camp, so don't think you're too old to join in all of the fun!

  • Mini-campers sleep in cabins with counselors (just like regular camp), participate in a full day of camp activities and join in some of our best evening activities!

  • Mini-Camp activities include: Horseback Riding (no extra charge), Swimming, Tennis, Waterfront Activities, Jet Skiing (no extra charge), Arts & Crafts, Basketball, Archery, Volleyball, Hiking, Ropes Course, Quad-ATV Riding (no extra charge), Zip Line and a super cabin overnight in the woods!

  • Campers entering 4th grade and younger travel together with their cabin counselors. Campers entering 5th grade and older participate in Lohikan's very popular ELECTIVE PROGRAM. They get to choose all of their activities every day!

  • First-time campers receive the ultimate in care and attention during Mini-Camp which is possible with our 1 to 3 staff to camper ratio! There is no better staff to camper ratio and no more experienced camp staff available. Mini-Camp is the ultimate for first-timers!

SESSIONS - PROGRAM - TUITION

Camp Lohikan’s program is designed to accommodate all of the enrollment sessions listed above. While our evening activities and special events do not repeat during the summer, we design our calendar of events, trips, intercamp games and clinics so as to equalize the fun and excitement of camp throughout the 56 day season.

Campers are assigned to cabins according to their age, grade and session enrolled. Our goal is to have cabin groups start and end their session together. Bunk requests are honored as long as the campers are entering the same grade and enrolled for the same session.

There is a price difference between the first and second half sessions in order to equalize demand for the half season session. In no way does it reflect the content of the program or the quality of the camp experience. Many families schedule vacations in August and, if the 8 week enrollment period is not a possibility, a July enrollment is the usual choice. July enrollments are also popular with campers who attend schools that start earlier in August.

TUITION REFUND GUARANTEE

We are so confident that the summer camp experience will be a positive, fun experience for children attending Camp Lohikan for the first time that we'll guarantee it. If for any reason a camper decides that camp is not for him or her and returns home, we’ll refund the camp tuition for the days the camper leaves early. We know of no other camp that offers the same.

SIBLING DISCOUNTS

Families enrolling 2 or more children are credited $400.00 for each additional sibling enrolled for the full season. A 6 week session, half session and a 14 day session enrollment of a sibling earns a $300.00, $200.00 and $100.00 credit respectively.

ADDITIONAL FEES

Canteen Fund

The canteen fund is the only mandatory expense charged in addition to the camp tuition. The canteen fund covers: (1) the camper’s snack bar allowance (2 items: 6 days a week), (2) a camp t-shirt, and (3) loyalty rewards camper program (6-year camp blanket, 5-year camp jacket, 4-year camp backpack, 3 year camp t-shirt), optional visiting day trip. The canteen charge is $390 for the full season, $300 for both 6 week sessions, $210 for the first, middle, and second 4 week session, $120 for each 2 week session, and $80 for the rookie week session.

OPTIONAL FEES

Baggage Transportation

Camp Lohikan's Bus & Baggage Service is optional. Bus & Baggage Service is available 4 days during the camp season: (1) June 28th, (2) July 25th, (3) August 22nd, (4) August 8th (only Manhasset, NY and Union County, NJ). We service the New York (Manhasset, NY and West Nyack, NY) and New Jersey (Union County, NJ) areas. The charge for the Bus & Baggage Service is $350.00 round trip or $175.00 one-way. There is a 1 duffel and 1 trunk limit.

Camp Lohikan provides transportation to and from the Newark, New Jersey Airport and JFK and LaGuardia Airports in New York. The transportation from the Newark Airport (Newark, New Jersey – Liberty Airport) is $225.00 one-way. The charge is reduced to $170.00 one-way when two or more siblings travel together. Transportation from the JFK or LaGuardia Airport in New York is $325.00 one-way ($250.00 when 2 or more campers are traveling together).

Extra Program Fees

All of the camp's daily activities are included in the camp's tuition except for: Extra Horseback Riding, Jet Skiing, Quads (4 wheeler ATV's) and Paintball. Horseback Riding is included in the tuition at no extra charge for 1 period (1 hour) a week. Extra Horseback Riding is available in units of 2 periods a week at the rate of $40.00 a period. Jet Skiing, Quads, and Dirt Bike Riding are scheduled in units of 2 periods a week and are charged at the rate of $25.00 a period. Paintball sessions are offered as a special activity at a cost of $45.00 per session (3 hours).

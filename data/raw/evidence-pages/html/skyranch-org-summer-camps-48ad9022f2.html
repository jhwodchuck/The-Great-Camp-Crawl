Engaging Summer Camps in Texas and Colorado

Whether they are 6 or 16, your child needs a week that is designed just for them – free of screens, focused on friendships, and rooted in fun.

Best Week Ever

When you look at your child, you see who they could become, and wonder how to help get them there. So do we.

RAISING HUMAN BEINGS IS AWESOME... AND TOUGH.

 

As parents, we want to provide opportunities for our children to gain confidence, try new things, form lasting relationships and grow spiritually. But in a world full of noise and fear it can be hard to know who to trust.

That’s why we’re here. Sky Ranch has been partnering with families for over 70 years investing in the emotional, physical, mental, and spiritual development of children and teens. Our intentional plan inspires, challenges, and encourages your child to tune out the noise and become who they were created to be.

Summer Camp

WHAT EVERY KID NEEDS

 

At Sky Ranch Christian Camps, your child will experience: 

  • A digital detox and mental reset surrounded by nature and friends
  • Real face-to-face community inspired by godly role models
  • Emotional resilience in trying new things and building confidence and courage 
  • Healthy adventure which strengthens problem-solving skills and reduces fear-based thinking patterns

 

RegisterCamp Options

An incredible week of Sky Ranch Summer Camp includes:

Small Camper to Staff Ratio

Our camper to staff ratio ranges from 6:1 in the cabin, to even 3:1 depending on the activity. Cabin sizes vary from 11 to 22 campers based on age. There are 3 -5 counselors in every cabin – ensuring campers are known, cared for and encouraged.

Relational Counselors

Your child will spend every moment with their cabin group led by our highly trained, college aged staff. Counselors are with their campers 24/7 – present during every meal, activity, and night event. Building trust, encouraging community, and modeling what it looks like to live out the Gospel.

Bible Study

Curriculum guided by the intentional plan of the Life Map. Our rotating world view questions meet campers right where they are with Gospel truths that reinforce their unique value, their identity in Christ, and dependence on Him.

New Friends

Cabin groups quickly feel like family – creating community, a sense of belonging, and developing friendships that last long after their week of camp is over. Campers learn empathy, how to encourage others, and how to invest in meaningful relationships.

Parties Every Day

Dance parties, costume parties, messy parties - each night there is a different themed event tailored for your kid’s age and gender! These shared memories of fun and craziness will become some of their favorite stories to tell.

On-Site Superheroes

We have trained staff for every situation. From a 24-hour health center utilizing nurses, EMTs, and doctors, to certified activity specialists, and experienced leadership ready to address homesickness and dietary concerns – we are well-trained and prepared to respond.

An Unplugged Experience

Freedom from screens and social pressures reduces comparisons, overstimulation, and constant notification-driven stress. Being outside, engaging in physical activity, and connecting with nature restores normal sleep rhythms. Your child will come home refreshed with a greater sense of hope and purpose.

Camper Photos and Parent Emails

We provide a glimpse into camp life through the Bunk1 app with daily photos, updates, and one-way Bunk Notes to your camper.

Feel Secure in Your Decision

Safety

We evaluate everything we do through the lens of safety. It isn’t just in the big things, but also in the small things. We are accredited by the American Camp Association, but more importantly, safety is a part of our culture.

Safety

Fun

Feeling safe frees up the space to have FUN - silly fun for our youngest campers in Texas, all the way to adventurous fun for teenagers in Colorado. Engaging counselors help turn every activity, night event, impromptu dance-off, and mealtime into a fun experience that unites cabins.

Activities

Faith

We know that when a camper feels secure and is having a blast, they are more likely to ask questions and grow in their faith. The intentional plan of the Life Map is woven into camp in relevant, age-appropriate, and fun ways: Bible study, summer theme, and teachable moments with counselors.

Life Map

It's not just a week. It's a plan.
Fostering spiritual growth and relationships, year after year.

Each program is guided by an intentional plan where we seek to grow a camper from who they are at 6 to who they can be at 18. This plan is called the Life Map.

Our yearly curriculum answers one of four worldview questions:

Who is God? What is our need? What is truth? Who are you?

Developing Character & Faith
PHASE 1: LEARN

During this phase, character development takes place. Through fun and relationships guiding them, campers can come to saving faith in Jesus.

Putting Faith into Action
PHASE 2: EXPERIENCE

Campers are challenged to live out what they believe, by putting their faith into action. They are ready and desire to learn more about who God is and who He created them to be. Phase 2 is what we call Leadership Camp.

Contributing as a Servant Leader
PHASE 3: BECOME

As developed leaders, former campers are now contributors - serving in their community, church, family, and camp.

 

 

Activities

There's never a dull moment at Sky Ranch.
*Not everyone does everything. Activities are age & program specific.

Bible Study

Sky Ranch’s biblically-based study curriculum answers one of the annually rotating worldview questions:

  • Who is God? (The character and nature of God)
  • What is our need? (The person and work of Jesus)
  • Who are you? (The body of Christ)
  • What is Truth? (The Word of God)

Each program will tackle these 4 questions from the Life Map meant to intentionally challenge and encourage our campers where they are at developmentally. By moving through them every four years, campers work through these questions in the different stages of childhood – developing and deepening in their understanding as they mature.

Cracker Barrel

Cracker Barrel is a time for campers to visit the camp store during the day and have structured free time to play games and hang out with friends and counselors, all while enjoying their favorite snack or purchasing a camp keepsake from the store to take home.

Crazy Pool

Cool down at the Crazy Pool, complete with a Wet Willie Superslide, basketball goal, 9-Square in the Air, and an Aqua Zip’n!

Frontier Town

Frontier Town is a fun-packed activity zone featuring the Adventure Playground, T-swing, and a netted arena used for Dodgeball.

The Pier

The Pier at Sky Ranch is a waterfront activity area. With AquaTrikes, Canoes, Corcls, and Stand Up Paddle Boards to provide the opportunity for a remarkable experience.

Nature Center

The nature center at Sky Ranch provides a hands-on, interactive experience to observe and learn about various mammals, reptiles, and arthropods. Facilitated by our trained staff, guests have the chance to encounter unique animals like the red-tailed boa, chinchillas, and hedgehogs.

Party Pool

Kick your feet in the shallow end, play water polo, or challenge yourself on the trapeze!

Skit Time

This is a mid-day time to get out of the heat and rest indoors, while singing songs and watching funny skits and videos created by our Camp Life team.

Skywaii

Skywaii features swimming and lakefront activities such as the Blob, Wet Willie Slide, and Water Inflatables!

Torpedo

The most popular summer camp activity, campers get the thrill of racing over the waters of our 90-acre Sky Lake as they are pulled on an inflatable behind a boat.

Mat Racers & Water Slides

Have a thrilling race down the four-lane mat racers, and then go cruise through the three body slides.

Ziplines

Speed down our multi-line ziplines with your friends. A high-flying adventure you'll never forget!

Paintball

Combat or target practice, Sky Ranch has something to offer for participants of varying skill levels. Develop unity with your team as you strategize how to cross into the opponent’s territory or become an expert at shooting on target. Additional cost applies.

3-Person Giant Swing

Our thrilling, 3-person swing offers a beautiful bird's-eye view of the lake.

The Block - Activity Center

The Block is our air-conditioned Activity Center, which provides a way for guests to beat the heat by playing games indoors like Basketball, Gaga Ball, Volleyball, and Ping Pong.

The Sandlot

The Sandlot offers a fun time of kickball.

The Yard

The Yard has a whole host of fun activities: Mini Golf, Volleyball, Basketball, Gaga Ball, the Maze, and Tetherball.

Laser Tag

Set in an outdoor course, Laser Tag is an exciting game of strategy and teamwork.

Horsemanship

An add-on activity for campers, horsemanship is an opportunity to become more comfortable on horseback through various activities and games, all led by our Sky Ranch wranglers. Learn More! Additional cost applies.

Alpine Swing

This is a swing unlike any other! Get ready for a 360-degree view.

Rec Center

The Horn Creek Rec Center is packed full of entertainment so, no matter the weather, there is always something to do: Bowling, Rock Climbing, Basketball, Racquetball, the Trading Post store, Peaks Treats, and more!

Tubing Hill

Glide down our tubing hill in an inflatable tube. Fun for all ages! Available all seasons. 

Water Slides

Choose from three different slides. Race a friend down or enjoy a refreshing cool down.

Kayaks

Take an excursion to Lake San Cristobal for an afternoon of kayaking.

Skeet Shooting

Enjoy this recreational and competitive activity where participants attempt to break clay targets.

Nights Events

Each night of the week is packed with an awesome theme night created with your camper's age in mind. From the unforgettable Carnival & Rodeo for Sky 1 & 2, to the tradition of Mission Impossible for Sky 3, or Zion's Cup for Leadership camps, campers are sure to have a blast.

Challenge Course​

An adventure with a fantastic view, the challenge course gives you the opportunity to challenge yourself on various elements and finish with a zipline!

Tree Top Towers

Interactive adventure playground with a dozen different elements: climbing walls, jumping segments, a zipline, and more!

Jumping Pillow

The Jumping Pillow is an activity you probably haven't seen before. Jumping on a giant inflated air pillow provides a fun and new experience.

Launch Pad

The Launch Pad brings together the thrill of bouncing and the excitement of soft landings. With multiple lanes, it’s designed to engage visitors of all ages and skill levels, allowing them to try new tricks and enjoy high-energy fun.

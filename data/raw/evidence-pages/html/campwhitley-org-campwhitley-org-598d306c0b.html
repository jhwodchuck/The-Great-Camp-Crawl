About Camp Whitley

Camp Whitley is a non-profit residential summer camp located on Troy Cedar Lake in Columbia City, IN. Camp Whitley provides week-long summer experiences to youth ages 7-14. In addition, there are special events and rentals for weddings, reunions, retreats and more. Camp Whitley has a special place in the hearts of many local residents, and families across the country! Since 1928, Camp Whitley has been making memories to last a lifetime!


Camp Whitley is a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

KEEP CAMP ALIVE BY DONATING

Camp Whitley is a non-profit and our sponsors keep Camp Whitley alive and thriving! Sponsorships help send kids to camp despite financial need, provide a pizza & movie night, contribute to repairs to our facilities, and more. Without our sponsors and volunteers, Camp Whitley couldn't exist!

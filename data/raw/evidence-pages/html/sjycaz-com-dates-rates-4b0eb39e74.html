2026 Rates & Dates

Dear Camp Families and Friends,

As we get ready for our 2026 summer season, we are thrilled to share our Rates and Dates and officially kick off the countdown to camp!

Our incredible staff is already looking forward to filling camp with laughter, adventure, and the kind of moments that can only happen under the pines of camp. Whether your child is joining us for the first time or returning for another summer, this year promises to be unforgettable.

We encourage you to plan ahead and save your child’s spot early, as many sessions tend to fill quickly. We cannot wait to welcome your family to SJYC for another season of camp magic.

2026 Rates & Dates

Discounts & Savings FOR OVERNIGHT CAMP (AGES 6-14)

  • Launch Days Discount: Save $75 on Overnight camp on our Launch Days: October 1st and 2nd, 2025!

  • Early Bird Discount: Save $50 on Overnight Camp through October 3rd, 2025 through January 5th, 2026!

  • Multi-Session Discount: Sign up for a second (or additional) week of overnight camp and save $50 off each additional week.

  • Sibling Discount: Save $75 per additional child.

  • Camper Referral Program: Refer a new camper and receive $50 off your registration. Learn more here!

Additional Information

  • These discounts apply to overnight camp programs (ages 6–14).

  • Note that all the above discounts stack, giving you the opportunity to maximize your savings!

  • Payment plans are available during registration.

  • We are ESA-friendly.

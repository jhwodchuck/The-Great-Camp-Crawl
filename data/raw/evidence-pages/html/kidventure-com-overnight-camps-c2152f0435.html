THREE OPTIONS FOR THE ADVENTURE OF A LIFETIME

Kidventure provides three overnight camp programs. Select the camp for your child below and learn more!

SAFARI

Ages 8-12 / 6 Days

Named ‘Texas Best Overnight Camp’, Kidventure’s Safari Camp mission is simple: provide kids with opportunities to learn important life lessons through adventure, teamwork, and just pure fun! There simply isn’t a more rewarding Texas overnight camp experience.

Located in Rocksprings, TX (transportation provided)

Safari Camp Details

Quest

Ages 8-12 / 9 Days

Like our traditional Safari Camp, we only take 50 kids, campers operate in one of our four tribes, and activities are a bit more ‘high adventure’. In addition to the activities, the session is also longer at 9 days affording our campers additional opportunities not experienced at Safari.

Located in Rocksprings, TX (transportation provided)

Quest Camp Details

Echo

Ages 13-16 / 9 Days

With a focus on outdoor high adventure, teamwork, and community service, Echo offers a life-changing experience that your teen will remember forever. Our 9-day camp provides opportunities for teens to learn, grow, and be a part of a 30-year tradition of excellence.

Located in Rocksprings, TX (transportation provided)

Overnight Camp FAQs

Safety is always our first priority. All of our Overnight Camp facilities are located on high ground and outside of floodplains, and our staff is trained in emergency preparedness. For parents who want full details, please see our Severe Weather Preparedness Guide.

We communicate with parents on a daily basis. In the event of any significant weather or schedule change, updates are provided promptly through email and our camp app.

At Kidventure Overnight Camp, we believe camp isn’t just an opportunity to have fun - it’s a chance to grow strong in knowing you can do anything, be anything, and do so with great character. The activities we do, from zip lines to kayaking, are simply a vessel to teach teamwork, leadership, and resilience.

  • Fun, Social & Friendship: Evening parties, social events, and lasting friendships.
  • Adventure: Zip lines, high ropes, kayaking, paddle boarding, hiking, rock climbing, and more.
  • Team & Leadership: Small group “Tribes” and character-driven activities help kids build teamwork and leadership skills.

Safari is a shorter term consisting of six days. Quest is longer at nine days. Both are designed for campers ages 8-12 and both have a similar format and objective. Quest involves a little more ‘high adventure’ than Safari and takes place at our camp in Rocksprings. Safari takes place at Mo-Ranch.

For Safari, campers stay in boy’s or girl’s dorms. These dorms are air-conditioned, bunk bed style lodging. Restroom and Shower facilities are located within the dorms. In addition, all bedding and bath towels are provided at Safari. For Quest and Echo, campers stay in separate boys and girls’ air-conditioned cabins with restroom and shower facilities. Campers must bring their own bedding for Quest and Echo.

Tribes are coed. For Safari and Quest, we divide our campers into four tribes, (Bear, Eagle, Buffalo, and Turtle). These tribes are by age and consist of boys and girls. Tribes are a function of our activities during the day and are separate from cabin assignments. Echo has two tribes, Donoma: Boys and Girls Ages 13-14, and Miakoda: Boys and Girls Ages 15-16.

Camp Facility 

Our flagship camp since 1949, is nestled on 219 acres along the shores of Fort Gibson Lake, 45 minutes east of Tulsa.  Overnight accommodations for groups of 60-150 are available in 11 air-conditioned cabins, complete with 8-10 bunk beds, indoor restroom, table, and a central fireplace.  Outside most cabins is a covered porch, picnic table, fire ring, and nearby shower house.  Common areas include a spacious Dining Hall with commercial kitchen that can seat up to 150 people, a tree house, and multiple rustic, screened-in meeting rooms.  Our camp is available April through November.

 

Rental Rates

Rental rates are priced per day and may be customized to fit unique programs and user group needs. Non-Profit rates are available. For more information, contact the Camp Fire office at 918-592-2267 or complete the below information form.

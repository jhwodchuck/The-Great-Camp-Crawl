        Payment Plan

        Payment Options for NYLF Medicine & Health Care

         

        When you are within the enrollment application, you may choose to pay in full or use the Envision payment plan, which allows you to secure your spot for just $499 and then spread the remaining cost of tuition with equal monthly payments. Monthly payments will vary based on the selection of session, optional items, and date in which you enroll.

         

        Installment payment plans are available from October 1st through April 30th. Past this date, applicants are expected to pay the full tuition in one installment.  

         

        By enrolling this April, you’ll be eligible for a 1-month payment plan of $4,059 a month. 

         

        Sample payment plan:
         

         *$450 deposit plus one-time $49 deposit fee
        **Includes a $10 installment fee per payment; amounts may vary based on the selection of session, optional items, and date in which you enroll

         

        All credit card payments will incur a processing fee (where allowed by state law). While you’ll still have the option to pay via credit card, you can switch to our ACH payment method to avoid credit card processing fees. With ACH, funds are securely and automatically withdrawn directly from your checking or savings account and can be easily set up within your MyEnvisionExperience.com page.

         

        Additional payment options may be available. Please contact the office of Admissions at (866) 858-5323 to speak with one of our enrollment specialists.

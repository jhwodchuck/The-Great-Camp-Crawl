Registration & Workshop Rates

2025 Registration

Registration for the 2025 Oklahoma Fall Arts Institute is now closed.

SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION

Oklahoma public school teachers and administrators may be eligible for a full scholarship. Scholarships will be awarded by August 29. For scholarship consideration, applicants must complete an online registration form, which includes a brief scholarship application. Scholarship evaluation will begin on August 25, so it is recommended to submit an application prior to that date for full consideration. Please see the Educator Scholarships page for more information.

Workshop Rates

IN-STATE & OUT-OF-STATE RESIDENTS

Rate includes tuition, meals (Thursday dinner through Sunday breakfast), and 3-nights lodging in a private, single-occupancy room. See below for guest fees.

In-State: $750.00 

Out-of-State: $975.00 

FRIENDS OF OAI

A $100 DISCOUNT is available to Oklahoma college/university students and faculty, OSAI alumni and counselors, retired Oklahoma educators, OAI advisory panel members, and OAI board members. Rate includes tuition, meals (Thursday dinner through Sunday breakfast), and 3-nights lodging in a private, single-occupancy room.

$650.00

OKLAHOMA PK-12 PUBLIC & PRIVATE SCHOOL EDUCATORS

Rate includes tuition, meals (Thursday dinner through Sunday breakfast), and 3-nights lodging in a shared, double-occupancy room. If you are an educator who wishes to enroll immediately with no scholarship, see the rates below.*

*If you are an Oklahoma public school educator or administrator seeking a scholarship, please see the Educator Scholarships page.

EDUCATORS 
$450.00

UPGRADE TO SINGLE-OCCUPANCY ROOM (as space allows)
+ $200

Upon completion of each workshop, participants receive a certificate for 18 hours of professional development.

ADDITIONAL GUESTS

Rate includes all overnight stay and meals. Because OAI is charged per person, per night, the following fees apply. OFAI participants reserving a private room may bring a spouse, guest and/or a child for an additional fee.

$125 (ages 6+)
$Free (ages 5 and under)

Guests may not attend classes and no unattended children are allowed at Quartz Mountain.

Terms of Registration

PAYMENTS
A $100 deposit is due within three days of registration. The remaining balance is due no later than two weeks prior to the chosen OFAI workshop.

REFUNDS/CANCELLATIONS 
If you are unable to attend a workshop for any reason, please notify the Oklahoma Arts Institute immediately so that your place may be filled by an applicant from the waiting list.

If you cancel more than 14 days before the first day of the workshop, your tuition and $100 deposit will be refunded. If cancellation notice is received after the 14 day deadline, and the workshop place cannot be filled, participants will forfeit the $100 deposit. If you cancel within 72 hours of the beginning of your workshop, you will forfeit the entire cost of your workshop. In the instance that the Oklahoma Arts Institute is forced to cancel a class, your tuition and $100 deposit will be refunded.

The Oklahoma Arts Institute reserves the right to substitute faculty members. In all such cases, an attempt will be made to inform participants of a faculty change as time constraints will allow.

APPEARANCE/WRITTEN COMMENTS RELEASE
Participants agree and understand that, unless the Oklahoma Arts Institute is otherwise instructed in writing, enrollment constitutes a release to use their name, written comments, and likeness, including any audio recordings, video footage, or still photographs of them taken during OFAI, in publicity for the Institute.

LIABILITY RELEASE
With online registration, participants hereby waive any and all claims for the participant and the participant's heirs and personal representatives against the Oklahoma Arts Institute and any of their agents or employees for injury, illness, or loss of personal property, by theft or otherwise, which may result from participation in such workshop.

The Oklahoma Arts Institute reserves the right to refuse registration to anyone for any reason.

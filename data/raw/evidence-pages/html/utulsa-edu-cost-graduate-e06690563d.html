Tuition & Costs

Cost of Attendance: 2025-26 Graduate

Tuition
Graduate Tuition per semester hour, all programs if not otherwise noted below $1,548
• Doctor of Nursing Practice per semester hour, except for Nurse Anesthesia $1,266
• Doctor or Nursing Practice Nurse Anesthesia, per semester hour $1,478
• Master of Athletic Training, per semester hour $974
• Master of Business Administration – Online, per semester hour $850
• Master of Cybersecurity – Online, per semester hour $850
• Master of Science Nursing – Online, per semester hour $633
• Graduate AG Certification – Online, per semester hour $725
Fees*
Fees are in addition to the tuition shown above.
University Fee, full-time students (9 hours or more), per semester** $633
University Fee, part-time students (8 hours or less), per semester** $108
One-time Student Services Fee for first-time enrollees $108
International Student Services Fee, per semester $202
Graduate School application fee, nonrefundable $60
Thesis and Dissertation Fees (3 required copies) $110
Estimated Living Expenses
Room and Board (on-campus), per semester*** $8,350
*See bulletin for program-specific fees.
**University Fee covers the following amenities for UTulsa students: fitness center, intramurals, counseling center, Hurricane Health Center, computer labs, campus-wide wireless internet, printing, career services, 24-hour campus security, and other student focused initiatives.
***Double occupancy in a residence hall with All Access Plan with $400 Dining Dollars each semester. For additional housing and dining tiers, visit our website.

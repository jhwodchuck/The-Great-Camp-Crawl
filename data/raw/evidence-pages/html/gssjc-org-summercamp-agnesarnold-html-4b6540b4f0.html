Summer Camp at Agnes Arnold

 

Located near Conroe, Camp Agnes Arnold offers a wide variety of classic camp activities, including archery, nature hikes, arts and crafts, and much more. At Shadow Lake, with its 35 acres of cool, refreshing fun, girls can canoe, swim, and explore nature around the lake. Girls can also explore Adventure Park and learn camping skills, such as how to build a campfire and how to cook over one. 

This summer, we have camp sessions to match every girls' outdoor interests. Take a look at the overnight camp schedule below, or review our Summer Camp Guide for more details. Next, register for a session through UltraCamp. Act now because spots fill fast!

Accommodations

Campers will stay in tents, tree houses, or cabin units that each accommodate 12 to 24 girls. Girls are assigned units based on their session, and units are assigned based on the number of girls in a session.

With a view of the lake, girls eat most of their meals in the air-conditioned Ann Temple Allen Lodge. Near the lodge, the climate-controlled craft rooms and library/memorabilia room also offer an escape from the summer heat.

The treehouses are not climate-controlled, but they have ceiling fans that keep campers cool. Cabin units have window air conditioning units. The camp session descriptions in the camp guide will have information about the type of unit campers will stay in.

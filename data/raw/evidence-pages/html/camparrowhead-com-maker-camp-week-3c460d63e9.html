Maker Camp Week

Maker Camp Week is where Camp Arrowhead transforms into a creative workshop in the mountains. During this special week, campers dive deep into the Maker Spirit — forging, carving, sewing, and building side-by-side with guest artists, blacksmiths, woodworkers, and craftspeople from across the country. It’s a celebration of imagination and craftsmanship, where every project tells a story and every camper gets to take home something real they made themselves.

From blacksmithing and woodworking to bushcraft, leatherwork, and creative design challenges, Maker Camp Week is packed with opportunities to learn new skills, collaborate, and experiment. Campers gain confidence through doing — turning raw materials into finished creations while discovering the satisfaction of true craftsmanship.

One week. Infinite creativity. Maker Camp Week at Arrowhead

Love Camp Arrowhead but have a packed summer schedule? Maker Camp Week is the perfect way to experience the best of Arrowhead in just one action-filled week.

This special session can be added as a fourth week for campers enrolled in our three-week session, or attended on its own as a stand-alone week for boys ages 12 and up who can’t stay for a full session.

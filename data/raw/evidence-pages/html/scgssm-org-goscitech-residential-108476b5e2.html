GoSciTech - Residential Camp

Everything you love about STEM in a fun-filled week on campus.

READY FOR THE BEST SUMMER YET? SUMMER CAMP APPLICATIONS ARE NOW OPEN!

Apply Now

Explore STEM. Make Friends. Have Fun.

GoSciTech Residential is a week-long, on-campus summer camp for rising 8th, 9th, and 10th graders who are ready to dive deep into science and technology. Students live in our residence halls, learn in real labs, and experience daily life on the GSSM campus—just like our residential students.

Courses are led by GSSM faculty, college and university professors, and industry experts who make STEM learning hands-on, challenging, and fun.

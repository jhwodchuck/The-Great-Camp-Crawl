About Us
Est. 1946 in Bemidji, MN

Two Camps, One Tradition

Four generations. 600 acres. Three miles of shoreline. Brother-sister camps, just miles apart. 30+ Activities. 3:1 camper to staff ratio. We are proud to be the only program in the country that offers a traditional summer camp experience that evolves into a Teen Adventure Tour Program. We build confidence and friendships that last a lifetime.

Boys Camp

Define your days. Embrace adventure. Find your people. At Camp Thunderbird for Boys, campers step outside their comfort zones and discover unknown strengths. We cultivate kindness and empathy, while encouraging grit. This is a place where boys can play hard, create freely and find moments of stillness and reflection. Who will your son become when you give him the space to grow?

Girls Camp

Be bold. Be loved. Be you. At Camp Thunderbird for Girls, we give our campers the space to discover their full potential while surrounding them with friends who uplift and support them. Girls leave stronger, more confident, and part of a community that feels like family. Each day invites them to stretch beyond their horizons and see what they’re truly capable of. What could your daughter achieve when she is given the space to grow?

More Than Just a Program

Camp is a stepping stone for your child's lifelong success and happiness. Since 1946, we've been one of the leading camps in the industry. By the time your child is done with our program, they'll believe they can do anything they set their minds to. Backed by generations, we've seen this impact time and time again. Whether you come as a camper or a staff member, you are known, supported, and pulled into a community that truly cares. This is why you choose Thunderbird.

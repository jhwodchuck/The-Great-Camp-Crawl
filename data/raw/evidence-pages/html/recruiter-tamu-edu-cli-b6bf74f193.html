    Corps Leadership Invitational: An overnight program with the Texas A&M Corps of Cadets

    The Corps Leadership Invitational is for academically eligible high school juniors and seniors in student leadership organizations like JROTC, FFA, 4-H, Girls State, Boys State, National Honor Society, Student Council and Girl Scouts, Scouting and Eagle Scouts. Students must be at least 16 years old to participate.

    During this immersive experience program, participants will:

    • Stay with a leader in the Corps
    • Experience first-hand what it is like to be a cadet in the Corps
    • Meet the Fish Drill Team - national drill meet winners
    • Attend a leadership panel with cadet hosts and the Hollingsworth Center for Applied Leadership Studies
    • Learn about scholarships, key dates, and recommendations for submitting your Texas A&M application

    Dates and Schedule

    • October – Juniors, and Seniors with a Texas A&M application on file
    • February – Juniors, and Admitted Seniors to Texas A&M

    For more information, please email us at aggiecorps@tamu.edu or call the Corps Recruiting Office at 979-845-9232

    Your CLI Application will be reviewed for qualifications and space is limited, not all submissions will be approved. 


    If your CLI Application is approved then you will receive an email with a link to complete the form for the event and a non-refundable $45.00 fee per student is required to attend which includes dinner and breakfast at Duncan Dining Center for the student. Your registration is NOT complete until payment is submitted via PayFlow, a 3rd party payment portal used by Texas A&M University.

    If approved you are required to complete and upload the TAMU Camps Forms during the registration process.     You are responsible for your travel arrangements to and from Texas A&M University, College Station, TX, and the Sanders Corps Center.

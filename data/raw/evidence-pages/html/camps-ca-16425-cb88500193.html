Virtual Trumpet, Trombone, French Horn, Baritone & Tuba Lessons

By: The Music Studio


About this Program:

  • Virtual Program
  • Age 8 - 18+ (Coed)
  • Specialized in Musical instrument training, offers 1 activity
  • 0 session dates to choose from
  • Cost starting from $41
  • Special needs support not available

Contact:

Visit Website
Request Info

Through your child’s fun and engaging results oriented trumpet, trombone, French Horn, baritone or tuba lessons with their dedicated and nurturing teacher at The Music Studio, your child will develop confidence, commitment and problem solving skills while experiencing the joy of making music.  Your child's lessons will focus on developing essential trumpet, trombone, French Horn, baritone or tuba, and musical skills while your child plays music they enjoy and explore their creativity.

Your child has the opportunity to take centre stage and be the star as they share their music with family and friends at The Music Studio’s monthly concerts. 

This program is also offered as an in-person option. Choose from The Music Studio’s in-person lessons, or live and interactive online lessons taught in a welcoming and professional learning environment. Please contact us today.
Request more info


Character Development: This program helps build the following traits

  • Resilience: Through trumpet, trombone, French Horn, baritone or tuba lessons your child learns how to persevere and overcome challenges when learning a piece of music so that they can perform the music well and feel a sense of accomplishment.
  • Creativity: Your child will explore their creativity when playing the trumpet, trombone, French Horn, baritone or tuba through their interpretation of musical phrasing and dynamics, and expressing themselves through their music.

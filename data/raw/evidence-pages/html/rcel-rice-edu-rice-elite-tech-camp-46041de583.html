About Rice ELITE Tech Camp

What Makes Rice ELITE Tech Extraordinary

Faculty-Designed, Student-Taught Excellence

Each course is designed by a head instructor who is a Rice engineering faculty member, PhD student, or engineering professional. Day-to-day instruction comes from exceptional Rice engineering students who achieved outstanding academic credentials (averaging 3.96 GPA and SAT scores exceeding 1500) during their high school years. Head instructors periodically visit classes to ensure quality and provide direct access to curriculum designers.

Professional-Grade Technology Skills

Master cutting-edge technologies and tools including robotics, AI and machine learning, Python and C++ programming, IoT applications, bioengineering, and 3D modeling. These aren't basic coding exercises—you'll build real engineering projects for your portfolio.

Engineering Leadership Development

Beyond technical skills, daily Engineering Leadership Workshops develop communication, project management, and leadership capabilities that distinguish true engineering innovators. Learn what it takes to be ELITE—an Emerging Leader in Technology and Engineering.

Mentorship and College Readiness

Through daily conversations and structured student panels, discover what it takes to follow a similar path to engineering excellence. Learn from the engineering students you aspire to become while experiencing life at one of America's most prestigious engineering institutions.

Engineering Skills in Action

Engage directly in the engineering design process through multi-day, student-led projects where you'll practice hands-on activities that employ technical, scientific, and analytical concepts to solve real-world problems.

Rice ELITE Tech - Houston (@Rice University)

Residential Camps

Rice ELITE Tech Residential Track takes place at Rice University in Houston, Texas. Campers attending the Residential Track will stay on Rice University campus in the Residential Colleges (dormitories) for the duration of camp. Your tuition includes housing, on-site meals, supervisory and instructional personnel, program materials, all program activities, a Certificate of Completion, and local transportation during the program. However, your transportation between home and Rice University Campus in Houston, TX is NOT included in tuition. Sunday and Saturday check-in/check-out activities are required.

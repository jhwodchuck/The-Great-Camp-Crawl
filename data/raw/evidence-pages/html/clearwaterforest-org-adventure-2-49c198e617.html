Adventure Experiences

The 3rd letter in our FLAME acronym stands for Adventure Experiences!  Adventure is all round us at Clearwater Forest!  From land to lake, there is something for everyone!  Mountain bike, hiking, and snowshoe trails, archery, low ropes challenge course, canoeing, kayaking, stand up paddleboards, fishing, and so much more!

With the addition of the new Programming Building, we hope to offer even more Adventure Experiences year-round!  It will also be helpful on those rainy days or when the air quality keeps us indoors.

REGISTRATION FOR SUMMER CAMP
Summer camp programs at Clearwater Forest will include Grands Camp, Sailing Camp, and 10-Day Camp, as well as the traditional Discovery, Adventure, and Senior High Camps. Campers can also participate in Camp Create, a new camp hosted by an award winning author/artist along with other art and drama opportunities. Come join us!!

Summer Camp Pricing

At Clearwater, we believe every youth should get to experience summer camp, regardless of their family’s financial situation. For this reason, we have Scholarships. Please apply here. 2025 Summer Camp is $625 per camper for one week. *Longer camps cost more and shorter camps cost less. Family and Grands camp rates can be found on the Family Programs page of our website.

Registration FAQs

Fees are due paid in full 10 days before any camp session begins. If registration occurs within 10 days prior to the start of the session, all fees are due with registration.

We want everyone to experience Clearwater Forest! Please check with your local Presbyterian Church first, and then apply for Clearwater’s Scholarship.

Each camp fee includes a minimum non-refundable deposit of $100. If registering within ten days of the start date for the camp, all fees are due at the time of registration.

*Check our cancellation policy to find the most up to date information.

Camp fees may be transferred to another session of camp or refunded (minus the $100 deposit) up to 10 days prior to the start of any session. Any amount of financial assistance received for a camper that cancels will be refunded only to the provider of the financial assistance. Cancellations due to sudden illness, injury or extreme weather will be refunded the full amount at Clearwater Forest’s discretion.

*Check our cancellation policy to find the most up to date information.

Clearwater Forest does not prorate the fees for any programs. If a camper or guest is to arrive late or depart earlier than the scheduled program times, please notify the camp office.

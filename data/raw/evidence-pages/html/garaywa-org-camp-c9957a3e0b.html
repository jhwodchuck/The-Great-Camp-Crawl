Garaywa Camp and Conference Center

Camp

Experience the summer of a lifetime through Bible Study, worship, morning devotions, missions education, singing, swimming, zipline, ropes course, crafts, drama, recreation, and other exciting activities!

 

 

               Garaywa Camp & Conference Center is a missions camp for children and youth. The cost includes all meals, a snack everyday and a t-shirt at the end of the week. Campers participate in a variety of activities including swimming, morning devotion, ropes course, Bible study, crafts, worship, missionary visits, recreation and much more. We emphasize missions throughout the week by providing the campers with opportunities to hear from missionaries as well as equipping them to be missionaries.

 

Registration is open!

If you have any questions about summer camp please call 601-924-7034.

A TEXAS TRADITION

Camp OTX is the Premier Texas Hill Country Summer Camp Experience. Camper activities expand as the camper ages, ensuring a fresh and unique experience each and every summer. OTX customizes each individual camper’s schedule so that individual interests, abilities and goals are met while still providing plenty of opportunity for shared experiences that create lifetime friends and lifetime memories. Whether it’s our one of our many themed special events or our one of a kind team competition, every day at OTX encourages individual exploration and growth while bringing the entire camp community closer together, all in a wholesome Christian environment.

HAND CRAFTED

We believe that one of our most important responsibilities is to recognize that every child is unique. Every child has different gifts, different talents and different interests. During our morning activity periods, we allow every child to select the activities which he or she might most enjoy creating their own unique schedule. We also leave plenty of time for exploring and attempting new activities during our afternoon structured free time. We carefully craft the makeup of the cabin to allow for a spectacular cabin experience, while keeping the entire camp as part of the bigger OTX family. We intentionally structure our program to create the opportunity for new friendships to form outside of the cabin as well. Every camper matters. And that is what Camp OTX is all about.

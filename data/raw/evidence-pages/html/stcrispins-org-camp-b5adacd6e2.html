ST. CRISPIN’S SUMMER CAMP

As the saying goes, St. Crispin's is a thin place. A special place, where the distance between heaven and earth gets just a bit smaller. At St. Crispin's we ground ourselves in our Rule of Life, which teaches us how to live in Christian community. St. Crispin's is an inclusive camp where we strive to meet the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of all. Campers leave St. Crispin's forever changed by the beauty, joy, and friendships found here.

FUN

Games, arts and crafts, archery, kayaking, paddle-boarding, a climbing wall, music, and more - St. Crispin's has it all!

FAITH

St. Crispin's is a community of daily worship and prayer in the Episcopal tradition. We welcome all people and all faith traditions in our midst.

FRIENDS

When you ask people what they love most about St. Crispin's it's always the people. Form lifelong friendships and create connections that go beyond the boundaries of camp.

ADDITIONAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

St. Crispin’s Summer Camp and the Episcopal Diocese of Oklahoma is committed to never turning away a camper due to inability to pay. The Diocese of Oklahoma offers scholarships starting at 1/3 of camp tuition and work with individual camp families based on financial need. If you are interested in receiving a scholarship from the Episcopal Diocese of Oklahoma please fill out the Diocesan Scholarship Form. If you are receiving assistance from a congregational or another entity, please fill out the Congregational Assistance Form. The Congregational Assistance is required by the Diocese so we can apply your assistance to your account. Please note, if you are wanting to receive congregational assistance and apply for a scholarship from the Episcopal Diocese only fill out the Diocesan Scholarship form.

Contact Teresa Phares if you have questions tphares@epiok.org

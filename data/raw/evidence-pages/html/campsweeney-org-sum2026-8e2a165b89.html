BEFORE YOU START THIS APPLICATION

Make sure you have all of your information:
* Guardian contact name(s), number(s), email(s), address(es), and occupation/employer

* Camper's food allergies, if any

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Financial Aid: provided by Camp Sweeney with support from donors, the United Way, and private foundations. Aid is based on the camper's household annual income and the number of people living in the household. Any camper who has diabetes may apply for financial aid.
Scholarships: provided via donor-funded scholarships. There are a limited number of scholarships available. Criteria such as single-parent families and other chronic illness may apply.
PLEASE NOTE: Amount of aid awarded cannot exceed the remaining tuition balance after other sponsorships or credits have been applied to your session account.

Email proof of income to registrar@campsweeney.org
Would you like to apply for financial assistance and/or scholarship opportunities?*
DOCUMENTATION PROOF OF INCOME REQUIRED:
1. Earnings/Wages: First and second pages of 2025 tax return, 2025 Form 1099(s), 2025 W-2(s) from each employer, or 2 most recent paycheck stubs from each worker or current unemployment paystub. If application is made prior to 04/15/2026, information from 2024 tax return, 1099, or W4 may be used if a current paycheck stub is not available.
2. If you are self-employed, own your own business, and have NOT filed taxes yet, you may send a current Profit and Loss Statement.
3. Child Support/Alimony: Court order papers.
4. Social Security/Retirement: Letter from US government or 2026 retirement income tax document.
5. Government Support: Letter from US government.

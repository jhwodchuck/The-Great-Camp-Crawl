With over 75 years of camp history, Camp Shaw loves to celebrate what makes it special. There is so much to experience here; we continue many of our camp traditions while bringing in new adventures and activities!

Our campers have the opportunity to experience all that we have to offer when they sign up for camp. In trying these new activities, each camper is able to gain skills and experiences to keep with them for life! 

Here at Camp Shaw…

 

We are ADVENTUROUS!

 

Did you know that we have 3 zip-lines that all cross over Rock Creek? Those that choose to try zip lining here get a nice view on their adventure!

 

We are CREATIVE!

 

We love to highlight creativity in each camper through our different programs that we offer. Campers can express themselves in camptivities like arts & crafts, theatre, music, and much more!

 

We are BRAVE!

 

It takes a lot of bravery to partake in archery! Those that try out this activity will get the opportunity to practice improving on important archery skills that will increase their target accuracy.

 

And We LOVE OUR CREEK!

 

Camp Shaw is fortunate to have access to Rock Creek, which runs through the middle of our property! We love to cool off and play in the creek, do rock building, and we have a waterfall that is perfect for you to slide on!

More Information About Us

Camp Shaw is an ACA-accredited, overnight non-profit summer camp and rental facility located in northeastern Illinois, just an hour south of Chicago. We have 75 years of experience which combines time-honored camp traditions with new adventures to create the perfect mix of programming for both our campers and rental groups.  While at camp, youth will have the opportunity to make new friends, try new things, and gain independence.

At Camp Shaw, children will further develop valuable social skills when they put technology down and proactively problem solve, play, and bond with their peers. Join us in summer as we continue to offer fun and safe overnight summer camp experiences.

Additionally, we’re the perfect venue for your beautifully rustic, laid-back event, including corporate retreats or outings, family reunions, sporting events, and more! We’ve hosted band camps, running camps, church groups, youth clubs, and more. Not sure if we’re the right fit? Just ask and we’ll let you know if we can accommodate you!

Camp Shaw Mission

Rooted in nature and tradition, Camp Shaw-Waw-Nas-See inspires connection and growth by bringing people together through joyful play, purposeful learning, and peaceful interaction.

Camp Shaw Statement of Inclusion

At Camp Shaw, we believe Camp is A Magical Place. We are committed to providing transformative outdoor life experiences in our safe and inclusive camp environment. We celebrate the differences of our campers, staff, and volunteers as part of our beautiful natural setting.

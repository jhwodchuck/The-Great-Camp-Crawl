Summer Camp Should Be Awesome

Choosing the right summer camp is one of the most impactful decisions you’ll make for your child.

At SJYC, campers and counselors-in-training build lifelong friendships, discover independence, and gain real-world skills like communication and problem-solving — all in a safe, supportive, and seriously fun environment.

They’ll come home saying, “I can’t wait to go back!”

Why Choose SJYC?

Camp isn’t just about what your child does — it’s about who they become. At SJYC, we’ve created camp experiences that are deeply rooted in tradition, guided by caring staff, and packed with opportunities for growth, fun, and connection.

More Than Just a Camp

At SJYC, we’re more than a place to ride horses, hike trails, or roast s’mores — we’re a summer family. Campers and counselors-in-training return year after year not just for the adventure, but for the people who make it feel like home.

The heart of camp is our staff. Over 95% of them grew up here themselves, and now they return to give back the same love and support they once received. That’s what makes SJYC special — it’s a home away from home, built on shared traditions, lifelong friendships, and memories that last well beyond summer.

Nestled in the pines of the Coconino National Forest just 25 minutes from Flagstaff, we’ve been welcoming campers since 1949 — and we can’t wait to welcome yours.

Bring Your Gathering to Camp

While SJYC is best known for our summer camp programs, camp isn’t just for campers — and it doesn’t end when a session does.

Throughout the season, families, churches, and community groups gather here for reunions, retreats, weddings, and special events. There’s something different about bringing your people together in the pines, sharing meals in the dining hall, laughing around the campfire, and waking up in cabins that feel like they’ve been part of your story for years.

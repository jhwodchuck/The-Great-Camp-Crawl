Where Jewish Kids Become Themselves

An overnight summer camp in the Texas Hill Country for ages 6–14

Since 1952…

CYJ Texas has given thousands of Jewish children life-changing summers. We offer a fun, challenging, action-packed, safe environment where campers can play, grow, and make lifelong friendships.

We Can’t Wait to See You

Year after year our campers go home with a love of Judaism and a strong connection to Israel. We foster personal growth by encouraging all of our kids to try new things and build new skills each and every summer.

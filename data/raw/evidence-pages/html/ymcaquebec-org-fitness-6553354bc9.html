YMCA Fitness Certifications

Are you looking for an accredited, reliable, and comprehensive fitness certification program? The YMCA offers a range of training options easily adapted to your needs and schedule. You can take the training online or in person with a qualified instructor.

Our Fitness Instructor Training (Fit) Programs

  • Become a group fitness instructor
  • Become a private trainer
  • Become a functional trainer for older adults
  • Become an aquafitness instructor

Why Choose the YMCA for Your Certification?

The YMCA was the first organization to offer fitness instructor training in Canada. The FIT program is a rigorous and comprehensive program that is recognized throughout the country. Choosing the YMCA FIT certification means choosing high-quality and highly flexible training with various specializations.

A Training Program With High-Quality Standards

  • Mandatory continuing education every 2 years
  • Recertification period
  • Workshops led by industry experts

An optimal learning experience

  • Theoretical content combined with hands-on training
  • Online or in-person courses
  • Easier access to employment opportunities in the YMCA network

Certification Eligibility and Requirements

  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • Must understand English and French

*A minumum of 10 participants is required for a course to take place.n.

  • Must attend 80% of theory courses and 100% of certification courses
  • Must score at least 80% on the theoretical and practical exams
  • Must complete the CPR/AED - Level A training
  • Must fulfill certification requirements within a year following completion of the theory course

You must have an intermediary understanding of English and French as the instructor may teach in both languages or alternate between the two depending on the group dynamics. Course materials are available in both languages and you can use the language of your choice.

Recertification and Continuing Education

If you do not meet the criteria for recertification, your certification will be temporarily suspended and charges will apply to re-activate your file.

Recertification criteria

  • Fill in the online form
  • Earn 16 continuing education credits every 2 years
  • Have taught 50 hours (1 or 2 specializations) or 75 hours (between 3 and 5 specializations) of courses in the last 2 years
  • Up-to-date CPR/AED certification - Level A

*The individual conditioning certification is no longer renewable.

If your certification was issued 2 to 5 years ago:

  • Provide proof of your certification
  • Pass the qualification, basic theory exam with a score of 80% or higher ($50 fee applies)
  • Pass the private trainer qualification exam (if applicable) with a score of 85% or higher ($50 fee applies)
  • Pass the practical exam supervised by a YMCA trainer ($50 fee applies)
  • Provide valid CPR/AED certification - Level A

If your certification was issued more than 5 years ago:

Retake all the courses required for certification and pay applicable fees.

To maintain a valid certification, you must attend at least 16 hours of continuing education workshops over a 2-year period. Six hours must be taken from the YMCAs of Québec’s Continuing Education Workshop Calendar. Workshops, certifications, or fitness conferences exceeding 6 hours will be recognized for 6 hours maximum toward the 16 continuing education credits required for recertification.

Continuing education credit equivalency program

The content of the continuing education program contributes to further developing the knowledge, skills, and abilities of private trainers and group course instructors.

Terms and Conditions of Registration

Registration and Payment

  • You can pay by credit card either online or by telephone.
  • You can pay by credit card, debit card, or cash in person at a YMCA.

Members Get a Discount

If you are a YMCAs of Québec member, you get 15% off your fitness certification.

Register for continuing education workshops

Workshop rates vary by type of workshop and duration. To know the language of the workshop, please consult the calendar.

Course withdrawal and refund

All course cancellation requests must be submitted using our contact form. The refund is calculated from the time the written request is received and not from the date the student withdrew from the course. In addition, refunds only cover the remaining courses.

Summer Camps

Registration opens at 8:00 a.m. CST February 9, 2026.

Please know that our overnight programs fill quickly. Camp registrations are processed in the order in which they are received as indicated by the date and time stamp on the registration.

The Discovery Hall Programs offer a variety of programs for all ages during the summer, ranging from single-day programs to residential camps and academic courses. Our highly qualified educators guide campers and students through all activities, and residential students are well-supervised by our exceptional counselors.

More details available in the Summer Camp Guide.

Summer Course

Residential Marine Science Class

Rising Grades: 9th-12th Cost: $3,000
The Residential Marine Science Class is an immersive 4-week academic class that introduces high school students to the field of marine science. The course gives students a better understanding and appreciation of the various disciplines in marine biology and oceanography.
 

Day Camps

Oceans Alive!

Ages: 5-8 Cost: $45

A fun half-day camp combining arts and crafts with a beach adventure. A parent/guardian should accompany the child.

Ocean STEAMers

Ages: 8-11 Cost: $75

A full day camp allowing campers to investigate ocean science, technology, engineering, art, and math (STEAM) through hands-on activities both indoors and outside.

BIO Blitz

Ages: 10-13 Cost: $60

A full-day camp in which campers become a Barrier Island Explorer. Campers will set out on an expedition across the diverse habitats of Dauphin Island, including the beach, salt marsh, and maritime forest.

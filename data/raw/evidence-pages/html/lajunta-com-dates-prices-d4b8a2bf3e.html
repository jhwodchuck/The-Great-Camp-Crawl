News About

Registration

All new camper applications will be time and date stamped upon their arrival in the office and processed according to their receipt date. The sooner your application is received, the greater your chance of acceptance. Waitlists begin forming for most age groups and terms fill well before Christmas. Waitlist applications will be processed as soon as an opening occurs, but campers will not be officially accepted to a term until an application and $500 deposit is received.

 

La Junta is proud to offer the best in traditional recreational camping.  Our tuition is all inclusive – this means no additional fees for laundry, activity supplies, special trips or snacks and no required camp uniform.  Brother discounts are also available.

 

Deposits are refundable until February 1st of the registered year; and tuition payment in full is due by April 1st, prior to the registered summer.

Selecting the Right Session

for your Son

Camp La Junta’s terms are designed to provide young boys with a positive, rewarding and eventually lengthy stay in an outdoor environment promoting self-confidence and independence. It has long been established that the real successes of resident camp come after the initial two weeks – after the fears of separation have been conquered.

We feel strongly that every camper’s goal should be a four-week term, and a vast majority of our current parents echo these feelings. The challenges of four weeks are greater, but so are the opportunities and rewards. However, anxious first timers (campers and parents) often are more comfortable with just a two-week option for first time summer. Thus, we offered shorter, introductory programs to allow younger campers the opportunity to get a “taste” of camp life without jumping straight into four weeks away from home. After this short, positive experience, campers usually return for longer sessions.

Future Summer Waitlists

We recommend beginning the registration process at least five summers in advance of completed first grade school year, or as soon as you decide La Junta is in your future. We will accept early registrations at birth.  The fee for a future summer waiting list is $100 and is non-refundable, but credited toward your camper’s tuition once enrolled.  Just apply below to select your preferred summer/season, and we will take it from there!

Championing Girls Since 1939

This is not your ordinary summer camp.

Camp Birchwood is a place where girls are embraced with love and support as they engage their whole selves in fun, meaningful activities in the Great Outdoors. The tremendous kind of “know-how” that girls gain at Birchwood ignites their self-esteem and fuels their confidence.

Come Into Your Own

With kindness and respect as the hallmarks for daily life, Birchwood campers are steeped in a rich community and encouraged to be their truest, most authentic selves.

At Camp Birchwood, she’ll meet her forever friends.

The Birchwood Way

Six Core Values Center Our Work

  • Spend Time Outdoors

    Children are meant to breathe fresh air, walk through tall grasses, and explore the woods.

    At Birchwood, campers ditch the gadgets and run until their cheeks are rosy.

  • Discover Self-Confidence

    Girls need a safe place to grow.

    Our counselors teach girls to root themselves in their own strengths rather than compare themselves to others. With every success, girls bloom.

  • Build Lasting Friendships

    The genuine connections forged at camp teach girls that they deserve that same level of kindness and respect everywhere they go in life.

  • Learn From Real Role Models

    At Birchwood, a vibrant team of women of every shape, size, and disposition run every aspect of our operation so girls see strong ladies taking charge and making it happen!

  • Acquire Essential Soft Skills

    Real life asks more out of our girls than academic know-how, success in sports, or hard-core talents.

    Girls develop a multitude of “soft skills” as they live and learn at camp, emphasizing collaboration.

  • Celebrate Accomplishments

    When girls choose to challenge themselves at Birchwood, we are there to support and celebrate that brilliance. Whether she hit a tennis ball over the net for the first time or emerged from a week-long trip in the remote wilderness, they are invited to take a minute and say, “I did it!”

Welcome to your “home away from home”.

At Birchwood, campers get comfortable, fast.

The very first day is filled to the brim with amazing outdoor activities. Our activity instructors individually welcome every child to the group, and connections quickly start to form. Girls learn each other’s names and find things in common. Smiles spread. Heads nod. Shoulders relax.

Before you know it, fits of giggles erupt, and that quintessential Birchwood magic starts to happen.

Birchwood girls are active, soulful participants in their own lives. They are immersed in a kind of memory-making that simply can’t happen back at home or in school. Nah, this is special.

Yep, once you’re part of the Birchwood “web” of friendship, you’re tied to people who care about the REAL you, throughout your life.

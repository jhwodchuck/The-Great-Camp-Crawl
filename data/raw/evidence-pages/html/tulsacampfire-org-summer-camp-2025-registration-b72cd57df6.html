Welcome to the 2025 Camp Registration Process

We are so excited for an awesome summer. This year, we have simplified registration, splitting it into an Initial Registration and Final Registration. Upon completing each piece, you will receive an email – we ask that you save these emails so that you can always return to them. They will act as your hub for your camper’s registration. 

This is the Initial Registration – completing this begins the full process of registering and requires an initial $50 deposit for each session. You will be invoiced to pay the remaining balance for your camper’s sessions. Additional forms and payments are required (see pricing information below). We will send you all necessary materials and links to secure your camper’s spot at camp and finalize the registration process. We are here to assist you throughout the whole process! If you have any questions or concerns don’t be afraid to reach us. Email: info@tulsacampfire.org | Phone: 918-592-2267

Camp Scholarships are available for families who request them. To request a Camp Scholarship (a “Campership”), please fully fill out the camp registration forms. There is an area to request a Campership on the registration forms.

Already begun registration but need to submit final documents? Click here.

Pricing and Payments:

This year, you will be able to pay as a guest via PayPal or be invoiced for deposits and final payments. Deposits will be applied to your total payment and are only used as a way to secure your camper’s spot. We accept card over the phone and in person and checks mailed to our office. More information is available to you once you’ve completed the form below. Full Sessions are priced at $475 and Half Sessions are priced at $295.

Discounts and Camperships:

To make Summer Camp as accessible as possible, we offer unique discounts and Camperships so everyone can experience Camp. Camperships, and camp scholarships, are offered each year to youth who want to attend Camp and need financial assistance. See our offering of unique discounts below:

Club Participant: Any current, registered, Camp Fire Green Country club members will receive 50% off of their camp cost.

Additional Sessions: All additional sessions added/attended will be 10% off.

Siblings: Each sibling registered after the first will receive $35 off of their cost of camp.

Referrals: For every registration that lists your name as “how they heard about Camp”, you will receive $35 off of your oldest child’s registration (up to 10 referrals).

*The $50 deposit is due at the same time of registration and any discounts will be applied to the remaining amount. Discounts may NOT be combined. Instead, the discount of the highest value will be applied if you are eligible to receive multiple. Campership eligibility is not altered by any discounts you receive.

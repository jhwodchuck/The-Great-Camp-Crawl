      About North Carolina Overnight Camps

      Overnight camp is fun for kids of all ages who want to sleep away from home. They usually take place in the summer, and they can feature a range of activities, from hiking and swimming to rock wall climbing anSee More »

      Overnight camp is fun for kids of all ages who want to sleep away from home. They usually take place in the summer, and they can feature a range of activities, from hiking and swimming to rock wall climbing and competitions. Choosing which overnight summer camp is best for your child depends on a number of factors, such as maturity levels, personal interests, age and ability.

      Highlights: Some camps have specialties that focus on a particular academic area, such as math or computers, while another may focus on the performing arts, for example. Other overnight camps offer a traditional camp setting, with a variety of campfire games, sports and water activities to try out.

      Counselor Certifications: Depending on the type of overnight summer camp, each counselor should have the proper licensing and certifications to work with children. They should be fully background checked, plus have plenty of experience in their field. Some are volunteers, usually college age kids, who help out the instructors with activities and games.

      What to Ask the Camp Counselor: What if I have to cancel for any reason? You may be able to get your money back, less the deposit, if you cancel far enough in advance. If you wait till the last minute, though, you may only get a credit towards a future camp.

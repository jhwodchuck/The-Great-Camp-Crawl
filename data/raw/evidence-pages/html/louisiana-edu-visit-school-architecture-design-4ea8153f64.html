There’s no better way to explore the School of Architecture and Design than by experiencing it for yourself.

From guided tours to immersive camps and open houses, we offer several opportunities for you to see our spaces, meet our faculty, and feel the energy of our design culture.

Schedule a Tour

Interested in seeing where your creative journey could begin?

Through the UL Lafayette Office of Admissions, prospective students and families can schedule a full campus tour including dorms, classrooms, and, with at least two weeks’ notice, the design studios and facilities in Fletcher Hall.

Your design program tour will be led by a knowledgeable faculty member or our program director, giving you an inside look at what it’s like to learn and create at School of Architecture and Design.

Schedule Your Tour Today (Pre-registration required)

Summer Design Camp 2024
June 16–22, 2024
Hosted on the UL Lafayette campus

Are you thinking about a career in architecture, interior design, or industrial design?
This is your chance to explore all three in one immersive, hands-on week.

The School of Architecture & Design’s Summer Design Discovery Camp is a one-week pre-college experience for high school students who are curious about the world of design. You’ll stay in campus dorms (or commute daily), visit real design firms, attend design lectures, and complete your own projects — just like a college design student.

Whether you dream of designing buildings, products, or interiors, this camp will give you the tools, insight, and inspiration to take the next step.

What You’ll Experience

  • Live and learn on a college campus
  • Work in real design studios with faculty and current students
  • Tour professional architecture and design firms
  • Explore architecture, interior design, and industrial design
  • Complete hands-on studio projects
  • Present your work at a final exhibition and reception

Two Program Options

Track 01: One-Week Residential Experience

Live like a college student.

Stay in university residence halls, eat all meals on campus, and immerse yourself fully in the college experience.

  • Dates: June 16–22, 2024
  • Cost: $850 (includes tuition, housing, meals, field trips, and materials)
  • Check-in & Orientation: Sunday, June 16
  • Class Schedule: Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
  • Final Exhibition & Reception: Saturday, June 22

Track 02: One-Week Non-Residential Experience

Commute daily, dive into design.

Experience the same projects, lectures, and trips—without the overnight stay.

  • Dates: June 16–22, 2024
  • Cost: $550 (includes tuition, lunch, field trips, and materials)
  • Orientation: Sunday, June 16
  • Class Schedule: Monday–Friday, 8 a.m.–5 p.m.
  • Final Exhibition & Reception: Saturday, June 22
  • Register for Summer Camp

Why Join the Summer Design Discovery Camp?

Because design isn’t just something you do—it’s a way of thinking.

You’ll leave this program with:

  • A better understanding of the design professions
  • Confidence in your creative potential
  • A clearer vision for your future college and career path
  • A portfolio of your work and new skills to build on

What Is Design, Anyway?

Here’s a quick snapshot of the design disciplines you’ll explore:

Architecture
Design buildings and spaces that shape how people live, learn, and connect. Blend creativity and structure to imagine better communities.

Interior Design
Create beautiful and functional spaces that promote health, safety, and well-being. Interior design transforms how people experience environments.

Industrial Design
Design the products people use every day — from tools to furniture to technology. Industrial design is about innovation, form, and human-centered solutions.

Experience a Day in the Life of a Design Student

Design Day is your chance to explore what it’s really like to study architecture, interior design, or industrial design at UL Lafayette. Spend the morning touring our studios, meeting faculty and students, and even tackling a mini design project — no experience required.

You’ll:

  • Tour Fletcher Hall, our design studios, and facilities
  • See examples of student work and projects
  • Participate in a hands-on design activity led by faculty
  • Learn directly from professors and graduate students about our programs and studio culture

Parents Welcome

While students dive into their mini-project, parents will take a guided tour and have an in-depth conversation with our director about why the School of Architecture & Design is a great place for your student’s future.

Preview Day offers a broader introduction to campus life and student opportunities, including:

  • Academic and student life fair
  • Info sessions on scholarships, TOPS, financial aid, and housing
  • Walking and bus tours of campus
  • Meet-and-greets with faculty and current students from SOAD

the creative arts camp of new england

for kind & curious kids

A joyful & inclusive sleepaway camp where all kids can shine!
Performing & Visual Arts. Waterfront, Music, Cooking & Circus.
No screens. All heart. Ages 7-16.

We’re an independently-run, arts-focused camp where kids ages 7–16 design their own summer — choosing from music, theater, fashion design, candle-making and more.

Our welcoming community attracts kind, curious, and creative campers who are encouraged to explore what makes them shine.

Can We Share More Info With You?

Since 1967, we’ve been committed to ensuring kids are their happiest selves at camp. Please leave your info below and we’ll reach out for a quick 15 minute call to learn about your child and see if we are a genuine match.

The Medolark Difference

Medolark is a place where we celebrate each camper’s unique strengths and guide them to discover and deepen passions.

That’s why we’ve been nicknamed ‘Camp Kindness.’

Here, kids explore their interests without pressure to perform or compete.

Since 1967, families from over 50 countries have chosen Medolark for our inclusive environment and our reputation as a haven for creative kids.

Don’t Wait! Cabin Space is limited!

As summer approaches, cabins are filling quickly. Drop your info below and we’ll reach out for a 15 min call to learn more about your camper.

A Camp for visual & performing Arts

We offer all the fun of a traditional sleepaway camp—plus so much more than basic arts and crafts.

Whether your child is passionate about the arts or simply looking for something beyond nonstop sports, they’ll find a one-of-a-kind experience here, with options like stained glass, music, fashion design, theater, dance, candle making, circus, and more.

Each activity is led by passionate artisans who are there to help foster the collaborative spirit we pride ourselves on.

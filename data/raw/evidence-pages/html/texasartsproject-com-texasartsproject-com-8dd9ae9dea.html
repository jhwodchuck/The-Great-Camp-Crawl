Summer Stock Austin

Summer Stock Austin has become an annual tradition in Austin, where local high school students combined with college students from all over the United States come together to train, perform, and engage in all aspects of production and performance in a fast-paced professional atmosphere. With two fully-staged musical productions, typically one traditional and one contemporary, along with an original musical for family audiences, Summer Stock Austin is uniquely a theatre production company and an educational training program in one. There is no cost to participate, and college students are provided opportunities for stipends. 

In 2021, we partnered with Texas Performing Arts with a live production of Bring It On on the McCullough Stage and a première of our original movie Scout in Bass Concert Hall. Scout is playing the film festival circuit this year and we are creating a digital educational series to partner with the film for elementary and middle school classrooms around the country.

Audition Studio

We want to help you. We know preparing for auditions can be stressful. We know preparing for college auditions can be overwhelming. We can help you get ready for whatever is coming up.

Our studio is divided into two types: College Auditions and General Auditions.

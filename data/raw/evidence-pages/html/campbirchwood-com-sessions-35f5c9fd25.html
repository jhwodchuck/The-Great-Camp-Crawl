Payment Information

Tuition is all-inclusive!

Tuition: All of our activities are included in our stated tuition rates. Unlike other camps, there are no added fees for horseback riding, wilderness trips, specialty arts and crafts projects, or other fun at Camp Birchwood. So once you get to Camp, campers are free to choose what they like to do without any financial pressure. That said - the only costs your camper may incur at camp are purchases at the Camp Store (snacks and merchandise) and Travel Fees are at Camp Families expense. Please see our FAQ page for more details.

There is a $1,500 deposit due at the time of enrollment for two and four-week sessions. There is a $2,000 deposit due at the time of enrollment for eight-week sessions.

You may pay in full at the time of enrollment or select an automatic monthly payment plan, in which case the credit/debit card or eChecking account you have on file will be auto-charged monthly with tuition divided into equal payments until May 1st. Alternatively, you may elect to pay by paper check, but this option ends on March 1st. If you elect to pay by check, please mail your monthly payment in a timely fashion and ensure your account remains current.

Tuition is due in full by May 1st.

Payment Terms

Payment Methods

We accept payment via ACH/echeck, debit, and all major credit cards. For your convenience, we auto-bill on the tuition due dates (the first of each month, but leave some wiggle room for processing days falling on holidays, etc.!). We also accept paper check for payment until March 1st, after which time all payments must be electronic.

Payments made by credit card incur a 3% processing fee, and payments made by ACH/echeck incur a 1% processing fee. There is no fee to pay by debit card or paper check. The option to pay via paper check ends on March 1st. Tuition is due in full by May 1st.

Cancellation Policy

There is a $250 administrative fee for cancellations after October 1st. We offer a full refund (less $250 fee) for cancellations prior to March 1st, a 50% refund of tuition from March 1st - March 31st, a 25% refund from April 1st - April 30th, and NO REFUNDS beginning on May 1st. This No Refund Policy after May 1st is industry standard. Cancellation insurance is available at the time of enrollment via CampinTouch - a third party, which we have no affiliation with nor profit from. Cancellation policies cost an average of $370 (standard coverage, 4-week session) to $550 (“cancel for any reason”, 4-week session). Coverage is completely optional and is typically less expensive for 2-week sessions and more expensive for 8-week sessions. Rates are set by the insurance provider and not Camp Birchwood. To protect our families’ ability to purchase cancellation insurance we simply cannot make exceptions to our Refund/Cancellation Policy. Please note: a reduction from a longer session to a shorter session constitutes a “cancellation”. For example: If you’ve enrolled in a 4-week session but after May 1st can only attend a 2-week session, even if we have room to change your camper’s enrollment to a 2-week session, you would still owe tuition at the 4-week rate! This is true for 8-Week sessions reducing to 2 or 4-week sessions as well. Thank you for your understanding!

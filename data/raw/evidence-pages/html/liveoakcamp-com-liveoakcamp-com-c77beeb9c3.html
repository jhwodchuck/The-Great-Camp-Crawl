Our Mission

At Live Oak, we are reimagining leadership for our people and our community. We connect diverse, values-aligned youth from New Orleans at sleep-away camp and then invest in their success all year-round.

The Live Oak Journey

From ages 7+, Live Oak grows with our community members through five interconnected branches.

Summer Camp

Two weeks of transformative experiences where campers build lifelong friendships and leadership skills.

Wilderness Adventure

Challenging outdoor expeditions that push boundaries and build resilience through LOWA trips.

Gatherings

Year-round events bringing the Live Oak community together for connection and celebration.

Year-Round Leadership

Leadership seminars, mentorship, and growth opportunities throughout the year.

Beyond Camp

College advising, internships, financial coaching, and continued support into adulthood.

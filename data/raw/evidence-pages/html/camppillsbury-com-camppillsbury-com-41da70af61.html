Try Every Thing!

Camp Pillsbury in Owatonna, Minnesota offers kids and teens the chance to grow and shine through fun, high-quality summer camp programs designed to build skills and confidence.

Coed Sleepaway Camp for Ages 6–17 | Fun Activities for Kids & Teens

At Camp Pillsbury, campers design their own unique schedule by choosing 3 majors to focus on daily throughout the session—perfect for developing skills and growing confidence. Our premier programs are led by experienced professionals in each field. In addition, campers pick 3 fun minors each day, exploring new activities or enjoying their favorites—like music, theater, circus, sports, cooking, robotics, and watersports. There’s something for every kid and teen to love!

Meet Camp Pillsbury

Your Camp Questions Answered

Where You’ll Find Camp Pillsbury

Camp Pillsbury is located in Owatonna, Minnesota—just 45 minutes south of Minneapolis—offering a perfect summer camp setting in the heart of southern Minnesota.

Is Pillsbury a Camp or a School?

We’re both! In summer, we’re a fun-filled camp, and in winter, we become a year-round boarding school with strong academics for campers who want the camp experience all year.

What is the weather like?

The weather is very comfortable. During June, July and August, the daily temperatures are in the low to mid 80’s and evening/overnight temperatures in the low to mid 60’s.

About Our Campus

Nestled in the center of Owatonna on 20 beautiful acres, Pillsbury is a summer camp in Minnesota located near Minneapolis and Rochester. The Campus is less than an hour south of the Minneapolis Airport and 45 minutes west of the Mayo Clinic in Rochester.

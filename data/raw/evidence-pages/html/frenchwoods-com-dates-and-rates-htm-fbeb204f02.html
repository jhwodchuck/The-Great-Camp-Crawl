C.I.T. Rates

The teenage years are the critical period for developing leadership skills. French Woods offers a Counselor-In-Training program for campers who minimally have reached their 15th birthday by August 1st of the summer of attendance. 

CIT’s attend a minimum of two sessions during the summer at discounted rates. 

Please take note of our Counselor in Training (CIT) requirements. As of 2026, 1st year CITs must have passed their 15th birthday by August 1st; 2nd year CITs must have passed their 16th birthday by August 1st; Super CITs their 17th birthday by August 1st. Please note that service credits are available to all CITs upon request.

Incredible Teachers & Staff

The YMCA teachers and staff are the kindest people you’ll ever meet. Your child is important to every single one of them. We’re committed to giving your child an enriching experience that sets them up for success for years to come.

Developmental Focus

We partner with you when it comes to helping your child thrive. Your observations, concerns and ideas help us provide developmental opportunities your child needs to:

  • Build pre-writing and reading skills
  • Develop math skills with counting, shape sorting and matching patterns
  • Explore solutions to conflicts, recognize feelings and relationships
  • Develop large and fine motor skills
  • Develop social-emotional skills needed for self-regulation

Arrival, Drop-Off and Sign In

Ease into the day with free play while socializing with friends and staff

Breakfast

Morning Meeting

Lunch

Rest Time

Snack

Active Learning/Outside Exploration

Core Learning Areas

We work every day to make each interaction a learning moment for your child. Our core curriculum areas for preschoolers include:

  • Literacy Pre-Writing Skills: Expand vocabulary, letter formation, interactive writing and name recognition
  • Literacy Pre-Reading Skills: Participate in shared reading, explore letter recognition, rhyming and alliteration
  • Mathematics: Understand number concepts, counting, shape sorting, matching patterns and relationships
  • Science: Understand physical, life and environments
  • Social Studies: Explore people, places and environments
  • Arts: Learn dance moves, music, drama and visual arts
  • Technology: Begin the basics of tools and technology
  • Social and Emotional Development: Learn to recognize feelings, build peer relationships and explore solutions to conflicts
  • Physical Development: Practice large and fine motor skills by running, jumping and playing
  • Healthy Habits: Understand healthy eating and personal safety, while building lifelong enjoyment of physical activities
  • Body, Mind and Spirit: Develop social skills, core values and a foundation for social responsibility

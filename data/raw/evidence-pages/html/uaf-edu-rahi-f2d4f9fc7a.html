What We Do

Empowering rural Alaskan students to succeed in higher education since 1983.

Rural Alaska Honors Institute

RAHI is a six-week summer program at the University of Alaska Fairbanks that helps high school students get a head start on college.

With RAHI, students live on campus, take real college courses, and build the confidence and skills they need to succeed.

RAHI is offered at no cost to students and their families.

YOUTH CAMPS

covey rise youth camps

EVERYONE LOOKS FORWARD TO

Summer Camp

Our Youth Camps result in a lifetime of enjoying outdoor sporting activities. Our professional staff teaches basic hunting and camping skills within an environment that promotes education and fun. Campers are housed in the Main Lodge. Every bedroom contains three or four beds and has a private bath. As always, safety is of utmost importance and will be reinforced in all daily activities, with each camper being informed of the camp’s rules, regulations, and responsibilities beforehand.

fun in the sun

What Your Kiddo

Can Expect

Whether you decide upon Basic or Advanced Summer Camp, your child can expect a summer full of newly acquired skills, non-stop outdoor recreation, and formidable bonds with their fellow peers. Our Youth Camp selections differ from many options offered within our area during the summertime. In addition to several hundred acres of pristine woodlands with nature trails and a running creek, our facilities boast a well-equipped skeet field and sporting clay course, a swimming pool, and a fifteen-acre beautiful lake completely stocked with fish for more outdoor adventure.

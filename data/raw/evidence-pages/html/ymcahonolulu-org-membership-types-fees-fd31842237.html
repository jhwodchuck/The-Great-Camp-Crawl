Membership types 

A non-refundable $99 joiner’s fee is due the day you enroll. In addition, you pay a monthly membership fee. Learn more about membership benefits.

  • Limited-Time Special purchase a family membership by May 31 and we’ll waive the joiner’s fee—that’s a $99 value! Use coupon code “HKD” when you join online.
    • Offer valid for new members who have not had a YMCA membership within the last 6 months.
    • Your Health Insurance may include a FREE or reduced-cost membership.
  • Military Promotion: In appreciation for your service, active duty and retired military, your dependents, and veterans are eligible to get your first month free, plus, save $99. Use coupon codes “Military” and “MilitaryJF.” You will need to present a military ID.
    • Offer valid for new members who have not had a YMCA membership within the last 12 months.

Need-based financial aid

Can’t afford the full cost of membership? Use our calculator to determine your potential aid and learn what’s needed to apply. YMCA membership financial aid is available for individuals earning less than $65,000 per household per year to families of four earning less than $110,000 per household per year.

Corporate Membership

The Y can work with your organization to establish a corporate health and wellness program that benefits employers and employees and includes waived joiners fee for new members ($99 savings), 10% off monthly membership fees and personal training, and 10% off YMCA Camp Erdman Company Retreats, and more! Learn more about Corporate Membership or email Jenny Ostlind at [email protected].

Learn More

Health Plan Fitness Benefits: Active&Fit Enterprise™, Silver&Fit™, One Pass™, and Renew Active™

Many Y members enjoy free or reduced-cost memberships provided by their Medicare, Medicaid, "Employer-Provided Health Insurance Plans," or other insurance providers, including HMSA, Kaiser, United Healthcare (UHC), UHA Health Insurance,  Moda Health, Wellcare by Ohana, HMAA, Aloha Care, and others.  

The Y participates in fitness benefit plans, including Optum's One Pass, Aaptiv, and Renew Active programs and ASH's Active&Fit Enterprise, Kaiser Fit Rewards, and Silver&Fit programs. Please check with your insurance provider to determine your eligibility.

Facility Rental

Howard H. Cherry Scout Reservation is open year-round for Scouting units and other organizations. We offer nonprofit pricing for registered 501(c)(3) organizations.

New facility and programming rates apply for new reservations made for after July 1, 2026. If would like to schedule programming at Camp Wakonda, please fill out the Wakonda Facility Rental Application and submit it to Sarah Dawson at Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org. Inquiries about packaging may also be directed to
Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org.

If you have any questions regarding camp grounds, please contact Josh Yoder at joshua.yoder@scouting.org.

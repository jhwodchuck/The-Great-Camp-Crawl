2026 Overnight Camps

Here you can find registration information, including dates and prices, for the upcoming residential camps and specialty camps.

These US dollar rates are the complete cost, covering all of the activities at our residential summer camps, including off-camp trips and horseback riding.

Each session accommodates a limited number of campers in each grade. When a grade fills, we will maintain a waiting list and contact parents if an opening occurs.

Scroll down for information about how to enroll, and the link to our online camp application.

How to Enroll

Completing an application is the way to request a spot at camp, and while it does not guarantee we will have an opening in the session you choose, it does begin the application process.

There is no financial obligation to complete this application. After processing and confirmation of availability, a refundable (prior to February 1) $1000 deposit will be due to enroll and hold a spot at camp.

Before applying, please read this detailed information about enrollment.

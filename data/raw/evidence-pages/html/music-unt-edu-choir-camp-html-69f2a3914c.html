All-State Choir Camp

June 10-13, 2026

 
About the Camp

This camp is appropriate for choir students in 1A-6A Texas high schools who wish to prepare repertoire for the LSC (Large School) Texas All-State choral auditions.

Tuition & Fees

Fees vary for campers who will reside on campus and commute as well as “all-state” status.

  • Overnight Camper - $414 which includes:
    • Registration fee
    • Tuition*
    • Housing
    • Three meals per day (Wednesday lunch-Saturday lunch)
    • Parking
  • Day Camper - $304 which includes:
    • Registration fee
    • Tuition*
    • Lunch & dinner daily (Wednesday-Friday & Saturday lunch)
    • Parking
  • All-State Music Packet - $30 (optional)
    • Having music in rehearsals is required, however the place of purchase is your choice.
    • Unfortunately, we are not able to sell part CDs due to the timing of when CDs will be available.

*Current All-State Members can apply for a $75 tuition discount code

  • Members are students who auditioned and were selected to perform with an all-state choir during the TMEA convention in San Antonio in February 2026.
  • Email music.camps@unt.edu with the student's full name and voice part PRIOR to completing registration.

Overnight campers must provide their own bedding (extra-long twin size), pillows, towels, and toiletries.

Registration Information

To register, visit https://app.getacceptd.com/untmusicworkshops. Begin by selecting your Area (Choir Camp) and Program (Texas (Large High School) All-State Choir Camp - Voice Part). For technical questions, please visit the Acceptd Help Desk.

Registration Deadline

  • May 22, 2026

Registration Policy

  • Payment is due in full at the time of registration.
  • We are unable to provide partial refunds for unused services including meals and parking.
  • Participants must be current high school students (rising freshman-rising seniors).
Refund Policy
  • Requested by April 15, 2026 – Full refund less $125 administrative fee
  • Requested by May 15, 2026 – Full refund less $175 administrative fee
  • We are unable to provide partial refunds for unused services including meals and parking. 
    • We are unable to process refunds requested after May 15, 2026 for any reason, including illness or injury.
    • No refunds will be made should a participant be dismissed for violating UNT policy or program rules provided on the first day.
Camp Schedule

Campers must attend the whole of every rehearsal and the final performance with no exception. In addition to monitoring student safety, our faculty has an incredible amount of music to get through in a very short time. While commuters will be returning home each day, our camp is not a “come and go as you please” camp.

*The schedule is subject to change, and any changes will be communicated to participants in a timely manner.

Why do we exist?

Networks really matter
in New Orleans.

Relationships and connections power our world. They open doors, provide opportunities, and change lives. Networks really matter in a place like New Orleans.

In New Orleans,
networks don't connect.

Unfortunately, our city's networks are often disconnected along racial and socioeconomic lines. This is particularly true for our city's children, whose primary institutions -- schools, sports, churches, the arts -- rarely reflect our city's diversity.

Live Oak is building a
rich new network.

Live Oak develops young people to become stronger leaders and teammates. We start with summer camp and go far beyond that in order to increase social capital and connect our community of talented campers, staff, and alumni.

Our Facilities

Located near Foxworth, Mississippi on 1,200 acres of beautiful woodland, our camp features world-class facilities designed for adventure and connection.

  • 3 lakes for swimming, boating, and fishing
  • Dining hall
  • Boathouse
  • Nature center
  • Arts Village for arts and crafts, dancing, cooking, and woodworking
  • Miles of hiking trails

A Non-Profit Overnight Summer Camp For Teens Ages 13-18

Located in the Berkshire Mountains of Massachusetts.

Be Exactly Who You Are

OTC welcomes every kind of teen—LGBTQ+ youth, gamers, goths, geeks, artists, activists, neurodivergent campers, and anyone who doesn’t fit into a box. At OTC, everyone is celebrated for who they are, and every camper belongs.

2026 Tuition

Timber Lake Camp’s All-Inclusive Tuition Includes:

  • All canteen costs ($150 value)
  • All linens & towels ($175 value)
  • Tipping staff is STRICTLY FORBIDDEN at Timber Lake

Choose From The Following Payment Options:

Option #1: Early Payment
$16,775 ($1,000 off tuition + $500 early enrollment credit)
$5,500 deposit on enrollment, balance paid in full by September 1st.
Option #2: Automatic Payment
$17,275 ($500 off tuition + $500 early enrollment credit)
$5,500 deposit on enrollment, $1,400 automatic credit card charge the 1st of each month (September - March), with the final balance charged on April 1st.

Early Enrollment Credit

A $500 early enrollment credit is available for new campers that enroll within 72 hours of visiting camp or by Visiting Day for current campers

Note, there is a 3% processing fee on credit card payments

When you enroll your child at Timber Lake Camp, a portion of your tuition goes directly to the TLC STARFISH Foundation which provides underserved children with summer camp, educational, community and wellness opportunities. To learn more, click HERE.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I enroll my child?

Since space is limited in each age group and our re-enrollment is between 90-95%, we suggest the earlier the better.

How do I get more information?

We’d like to hear from you. Contact us between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM Eastern Standard Time any weekday at 1-516-656-4200 or 1-800-828-CAMP.

How do we get to camp?

Take a look at our driving directions page.

Can I get some references?

Sure, just contact us and we’ll be happy to provide them for you.

Where are most of the children from?

All over the U.S. and internationally.

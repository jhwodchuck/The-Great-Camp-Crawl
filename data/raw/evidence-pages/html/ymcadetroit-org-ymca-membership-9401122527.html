Membership Amenities

The YMCA is more than a fitness center—it’s a nonprofit community dedicated to helping everyone thrive, no matter their stage of life or background. Here’s what your membership includes:

A Great Fitness Center and More

Our facilities include exercise machines, free weights, stretching areas, and weight rooms. Beyond equipment, the Y offers a safe, welcoming space to strengthen your spirit, mind, and body.

Access to 7 Metro Detroit Locations and YMCAs Across the Country

Membership unlocks access to all 7 Metro Detroit Ys and participating nationwide YMCAs, helping you connect with your goals and community wherever you are.

Lap and Family Swimming

Dive into our pools for exercise or family fun, with lifeguards and instructors ensuring a safe experience. Check our online pool schedules to plan your visit.

Discounts on Programs

Members enjoy savings on swim lessons, youth sports, day camps, and more, along with priority registration for paid programs open to non-members.

Free Group & Water Exercise Classes

Stay active and make friends with unlimited access to classes like Zumba, Yoga, HIIT, LesMills, and more. Create a fitness routine that fits your lifestyle.

Free Child Care While You Work Out

Take advantage of our Child Watch and Kids Club programs, offering engaging care for your little ones while you focus on your health. Parents must stay on-site, and hours vary by location.

Personalized Wellness Consultation

Every member can schedule a 45-minute session with a nationally certified personal trainer to set goals, develop a workout plan, or get an equipment orientation.

Family Activities and Events

Bring the whole family to enjoy our pools, gyms, and tracks during open times. Plus, participate in free family events held throughout the year.

The YMCA Detroit App

Manage your family’s schedule, sign up for classes, participate in wellness challenges, and receive updates—all through our app, available in the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Join the YMCA and discover how membership means more than just fitness—it’s about belonging to a community that supports you every step of the way.

Financial Assistance

The Y is dedicated to providing affordable programs and services to benefit people of all incomes and backgrounds in Metropolitan Detroit. Financial assistance is available if you meet income requirements—everyone is welcome to apply.

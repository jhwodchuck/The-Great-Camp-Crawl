2026 Arts Summer Camp Sessions & Dates

Interlochen offers a variety of art summer camp experiences—with lengths and dates for kids and youth in multiple age ranges (grades 3-12). Two and four-week sessions are available for grades 3-6, six and three-week sessions are available for grades 6-12, and one-week "Intensive" programs are available for grades 9-12.

Programs by Age/Grade

At Interlochen Arts Camp, students in grades 3–6, 6–8, and 9–12 come together from around the world to learn, create, and connect. Campers enjoy world-class instruction, meaningful friendships, and the joy of summer in a vibrant, artistic community. Choose your desired division below to learn more.

A student's age division is based on their current grade level, not the grade they will be entering in the fall following Camp.

Grades 3-6

Explore your creativity, make new friends, and enjoy the magic of summer in northern Michigan.

Grades 6-8

Grow as an artist, connect with peers, and be inspired by world-class instruction in a vibrant camp community.

Grades 9-12

Immerse yourself in your craft and challenge yourself alongside passionate young artists from around the world.

Create Art Alongside Adventure

Bring your curiosity and excitement—and leave with new techniques, lots of life experience, and lasting art camp memories. You’ll find counselors, faculty, and mentors who will provide exceptional instruction and help your talent grow—and you'll feel energized and inspired by young fellow artists from around the world.

Learn, Create, and Perform

Spend your days at art camp practicing your art or stretching your creativity by trying new opportunities at one of the best summer art camp experiences available. Explore your interests and experience adventures outside of your arts discipline in an amazing Michigan lakeside environment. And do it all surrounded by exceptional instructors and other talented kids who will cheer you on.

The Great Michigan Outdoors

Experience the wonders of nature in environmental studies classes. Enjoy our scenic campus during your free time. Go swimming, hiking, canoeing, kayaking, or sailing on one of our two freshwater lakes—or take on some friendly competition in individual and team sports.

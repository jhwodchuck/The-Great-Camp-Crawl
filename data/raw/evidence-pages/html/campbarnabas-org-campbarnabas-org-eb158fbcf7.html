We Create Experiences Where Everyone Belongs


Camp Barnabas is a place of hope, acceptance, and limitless possibilities. We understand the challenges individuals with disabilities and chronic illnesses face:


  • The struggle to fit into a world that's not always designed for their unique abilities.
  • The longing for an environment where they can thrive, not just survive.
  • The desire to be recognized for their abilities, not defined by their disabilities.


We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to participate in a camp experience that enables them to discover new possibilities and feel a deep sense of belonging and purpose.

You Can Change A Life!

Our donors, Missionaries, and Staff serve as guides on a journey towards helping our Campers navigate the path to self-discovery and empowerment. You can play an important role in providing campers with the opportunity to enjoy all the wonderful and life-changing experiences that a summer camp has to offer.


Because of people like you, our Campers have the opportunity to embrace their uniqueness, feel included, and be understood. They can look forward to the rush of a waterslide, the tranquility of fishing at the lake, the thrill of ziplining, and the joy of connecting with camp friends at nightly parties. It's a summer full of possibilities waiting to be discovered.


Make a donation, serve at Camp Barnabas, or spend a summer working with us. Together we can ensure that no Camper misses out on the life-changing experiences we offer. We can make a difference - one Camper at a time.

unique and first-class attractions

Have fun like never before!

Professional karting track

Perfectly equipped with an impact protection system, traffic lights throughout the track, professional Mylaps timing equipment, and more than 50 Gokarts where you can cover the probable 30 circuits on our track.

Off Road Track

Mud, ups, downs and curves are just some of the features of this track, to ride on fun tubular “coyotes”. Equipped with 5-point seat belts. Surely a fun adventure!

Off-road cycling circuit:

Throughout the Camp. If you are an expert or are just starting out in the wonderful world of cycling, this circuit is ideal, since it has a length of 2000 meters with tests and challenges for all levels. You'll always find a bike and helmet that's right for your size.

Climbing and Rappel:

The closest thing to climbing on natural rock... We have it on the cliff! Always insured by our certified instructors, you can also climb on two walls, in addition to bouldering and if you like abseiling, get ready for the descent in tower 1 and free rappel in tower 2.

Canopies:

155 meters of adventure among the treetops; Canopies is a double circuit through which you will previously be assured you will go through some tests that will make you get the best out of you. You can compete or just enjoy the views that this activity offers.

Cloud:

Have you ever imagined flying? Venture out to try. The cloud is a giant air pocket, patented for this practice; overcome your previous jump and level up from 6 to an impressive height of 12.60 meters. The best jump wins points!

Equestrian paddock:

There is no better opportunity to start in the magical world of horses, in a controlled environment and with the safety elements for this practice. Enjoy the experience of horseback riding but above all get to know these friendly animals.

River:

By kayak, canoe or pedal boat! After putting on your life jacket you can compete for points, or simply discover the magic of the aquatic world and contemplate the different species of animals such as mythical axolotls, prawns, carps, ducks, turtles, etc.

Pools:

Immerse yourself in the adventure of our four pools, always supervised by our staff and each one with different activities and attractions.

Kamikaze and yellow ramp:

You can't miss them! Glide at high speed on these water slides, inflatable, sitting or lying down you won't stop laughing... guaranteed!

Water flight:

After a fast-paced descent, get ready to fly. Double slip to get you farther than everyone else.

Pendulum:

Do you want to get on a swing? We invite you to take your emotions to their maximum expression... On the 15-meter-high pendulum, you don't want to get off.

Skill and Dexterity Circuit:

Here you will discover your inner athlete. Test yourself and overcome every challenge on this circuit such as ropes, balance, slides, climbing, as well as the always fun “leagues”. Compete and earn points for your team!

Zipline:

Travel from tower one to tower two. Perfectly equipped with a helmet, harness and gloves, enjoy the magic of traveling through the trees.

Sports fields:

And if you like team sports, we have them all! Baseball, soccer, mini tennis, gagabal, beach court, basketball, volleyball, team-ball. Exciting games and various activities you can do in whichever one you like the most.

Golfito:

Practice your best shots on the 10 holes of the completely renovated “Golfitos”. Choose the club that you like best according to your height and sharpen your shots on the 11 holes of the “putting green”.

Frequently Asked Questions about the camp

We answer all your questions!

What is the age range for children to sign up for camp?

In the Camps we only welcome boys and girls from 7 to 15 years of age.

Who takes care of the children?

The children are cared for by an instructor and an assistant, together with instructors specialized in various activities and camp managers who are on the lookout for all campers.

If my child goes with a friend can they be together?

Yes, as long as the children are the same age and both they and the parents agree. Although we can't commit to more than 3 campers on the same team.

Do the rooms or cabins have their own bathrooms?

The vast majority of rooms or cabins have their own bathroom.

Do you help young children bathe and dress?

No, we ask that you consider that children should be independent in activities such as bathing, dressing, combing their hair, going to the bathroom and taking care of their things. Instructors are waiting, but cannot take care of each child individually.

How is communication with children? Can we talk to them?

Calls are not allowed. The communication is through messages on the camp page, delivered daily at breakfast time or the first activity in the morning. Children respond to these messages in letters that are taken photos and sent to the parents' emails.

If my child is carrying medications, how do they manage them?

We have medical staff who administer medications. Parents should write down the information in the registry and hand in medications when leaving their children at the camp.

What do you do in case of an accident? Do they warn us?

The protocols are followed. In case of minor injuries, there is no warning so as not to worry parents. There's a paramedic in the camp. In more serious cases, parents are contacted before taking the child to the hospital.

Do you have any health insurance?

Yes, we have accident insurance that covers up to $150,000.00.

How safe is the camp? Do you have any systems in place?

The camp is safe, with measures such as electric fences, closed circuit television, among others.

What are the foods like? Can you briefly describe what they eat?

  • Breakfast: Fruits, juices, smoothies, milk, cereal, eggs, beans, chilaquiles, waffles, hot cakes, sweet bread.
  • Food: Salads, vegetables, soup, cream, chicken, fish, beef or pork, flavored waters and dessert.
  • Dinner: Salad, cereal, sweet bread, pizzas, club sandwiches, muffins, milk, flavored water, tacos.
  • Refreshments (mid-morning and mid-afternoon): Flavoured water, fruit, vegetables, whole wheat crackers, sandwiches, wraps, popsicles, jellies, and so on.

7 Week Summer Camp

Session Info
Raquette Lake Camps is a traditional brother/sister camp with full summer & four-week sessions (for first time campers only). Girls Camp and Boys Camp operate separately with their own sites, programs, and facilities, but frequently get together for co-ed events.
Location
We are located in the beautiful Adirondack Mountains in Upstate New York, each camp situated on private and serene lakefront property.
History
Family owned and operated since 1916, we value our rich history and traditions.
Facilities
Our state-of-the-art facilities and equipment are kept in top-notch condition.
Staffing
We maintain a 2:1 camper to staff ratio with the highest quality professional staff, many of whom return for as long as five to twenty five years (or more!)
Activities & Events
Each camp has a full program of activities, from sports to waterfront programming to special events and more!
Accommodations
Campers live in a bunk with six-to-eight other campers and at least three counselors. Each cabin has private bathrooms and showers.
Medical
We operate fully-equipped health centers on each camp.

Camp Tuition & Enrollment

$16,950 + canteen & transportation

Becoming a Raquette Lake family is like enrolling your child in a new school. You want the very best for your child, and so do we. It’s important to know that we’re a good fit for your child, and that your child will enjoy being part of the Raquette Lake Camp community. We get to know all new families. We will meet with you in your home, via video chat, or when you come to tour camp.

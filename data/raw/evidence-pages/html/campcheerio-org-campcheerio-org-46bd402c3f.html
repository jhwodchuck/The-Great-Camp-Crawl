Camp Cheerio

Camp Cheerio offers traditional summer camps, high adventure camps, and year-round retreats for everyone in the family. Located in the Blue Ridge Mountains of North Carolina and Virginia, Cheerio invites you to come and learn, grow, and have fun in a relaxed Christian atmosphere.

Camp Cheerio

Camp Cheerio is a traditional summer camp for boys and girls who are rising 2nd-10th graders located in the Blue Ridge Mountains of North Carolina.

  • Overnight summer camp
  • One & two week sessions
  • 135 acres of gorgeous mountain land
  • Over 35 fun traditional camp activities including archery, canoeing, fishing, arts & crafts, basketball, golf, lacrosse, zumba, riflery, LEGOs, free swim, climbing, & horseback riding
Learn More

Cheerio on the New River

Cheerio on the New River is an adventure camp for boys and girls who are rising 4th-10th graders located on the New River in Virginia.

  • Overnight summer camp
  • One week sessions
  • 150 acres in the valley of the New River
  • Over 20 adventure-based outdoor activities including mountain biking, tree climbing, boating, orienteering, fishing, backpacking and more!
Learn More

Faith + Fun

Here the Nueces River creates a natural playground on hundreds of acres of Hill Country terrain. Limestone cliff faces, hidden trails and caves, spring-fed crystal waters, and wide-open skies. It’s raw. It’s beautiful. It’s fun! And it’s the perfect place to meet Jesus without all the everyday distractions.

Outdoor Adventure

In God’s creation, faith gets real. Every climb, zip, and challenge turns into a moment to trust Him and put faith into action.

Performance Day at Camp Pillsbury is a celebration of talent, creativity, and hard work!
Join us for a full day of exciting shows and exhibitions proudly presented by our amazing campers. The detailed schedule will be emailed to each family before the big day.

Bring a blanket, enjoy a picnic, and spend the day with us cheering on your camper as they showcase everything they’ve been working on. It’s a special moment you won’t want to miss!

Performance Days begin at 10:00 am and last throughout the day.

Day Camp

Camp Pillsbury Day Camp is 5 days per week (Monday – Friday) for Full Day Campers. Full-day campers also attend Adventure Day on Sunday Performance Day happens on the last Saturday of the session

Full Day Camp begins at 9:30am and pick up is at 5:30pm.

Half Day AM Camp begins at 9:30 and pick up is at 1:00 pm

Half Day PM Camp begins at 2:00 and pick up is at 5:30 pm

Cost is $500 per week – Full Day Campers. $250 per week – Half Day Campers

About Our Campus

Nestled in the center of Owatonna on 20 beautiful acres, Pillsbury is a summer camp in Minnesota located near Minneapolis and Rochester. The Campus is less than an hour south of the Minneapolis Airport and 45 minutes west of the Mayo Clinic in Rochester.

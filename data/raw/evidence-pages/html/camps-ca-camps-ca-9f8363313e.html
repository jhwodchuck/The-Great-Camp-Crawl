Find camps by focus

Education camps

Camps offer a fun avenue for children to enhance their learning, communication skills and general knowledge. Academic camps teach students how to read more reflectively, think critically and engage with the ideas of others.

Sports camps

Summer sports camps offer the opportunity for your child to indulge his or her love of sports (or a particular sport) while enjoying the company of other kids. These also encourage health and fitness, and kids may even meet pro athletes.

Arts camps

Arts summer camps help your child grow in their love and appreciation of arts such as dance, music or fine arts. Encourage your child to extend him or herself and make friends with other children with similar interests. Find the arts camp focusing on the discipline you love.

Technology camps

Technology camps give kids a chance to learn more about their favourite gaming platform, learn coding, or build things with technology. Camps encourage collaboration and learning in a freeform setting. Find your favourite tech interest in the list below.

Specialty camps

Specialty camps offer families the opportunity to get special attention for children or to spend summer camp with kids from like-minded families. Kids and teens share camp with others who have a similar background. Explore specialized camps, listed below!

Camp guides

Our camp guide looks at how to choose a camp, the benefits for kids and families, financial concerns, and more. We believe that camp is an important part of kids' growth, and we give you all the information you need to make the right choice.

celebrating 125 Summers of sleep away camp in Connecticut

For 125 years, Camp Jewell YMCA in Colebrook, CT, has served as one of the premier overnight camps in New England, less than 2 hours from New York and Fairfield County, and 2 1/2 hours from Boston. As part of the YMCA of Greater Hartford, our mission fuses tradition with innovation: helping campers grow in confidence, connect through meaningful relationships, and belong in a diverse, supportive community.

Set on 560 acres of woodlands, trails, lakefront, and our own Hide-A-Way Ranch, Camp Jewell offers campers a balance of choice and structure: from horseback riding and wilderness skills, to daily swim and creative arts. Our leadership, CIT, and LIT programs amplify campers’ voices and growth beyond just fun and games.

Whether your child is new to camp or returning, they’ll find a home here — free from devices, full of laughter, and rich with opportunity.

ABOUT US

A Tradition of Growth Since 1901
For 125 years, Camp Jewell YMCA has been a place where children grow, connect, and belong. Our camp blends timeless traditions with innovative programs that help campers thrive—on the trail, in the cabin, and long after summer ends.

A Community for Everyone
As a branch of the YMCA of Greater Hartford, we welcome campers from Connecticut, Westchester County, and New York City, and beyond, creating a diverse, inclusive environment where every child feels at home. Our flexible tiered pricing system ensures that camp is accessible to all families.

What kinds of camp and retreat experiences does Eagle Sky provide?

KIDS CAMP

Our Kids Camp, West Camp, is designed for ages 8-12 and will provide a fun and rewarding atmosphere while teaching campers about God.

TEEN CAMP

Eagle Sky Teen Camp is designed for students in 6th (at least 12 years old) through 12th grade. It’s the perfect place for teens to grow in their faith while enjoying unforgettable adventures!

EAGLE SKY RETREATS

Eagle Sky provides multiple retreat options that focus on deepening relationships with God for a variety of groups.

GUEST RETREATS

We are proud to offer our facilities to churches and other organizations looking for a great retreat experience!

At Eagle Sky Christian Challenge Camps, our mission is to share the love and message of Jesus Christ with children, youth, and adults from all around the world.

We have 5,000 acres nestled in the beautiful Ozarks of Missouri that serve as a breathtaking backdrop for an experience unlike any other.

Our dedicated staff is committed to offering each guest who passes through our gates a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to learn, grow, and experience the power of God’s love.

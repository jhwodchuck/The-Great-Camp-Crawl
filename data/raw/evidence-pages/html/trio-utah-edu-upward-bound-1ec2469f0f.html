About Upward Bound

Upward Bound is a college preparation program for high school students who come from low-income households, are potential first generation college students, or have a disability. We help students succeed in high school and prepare for college to set them up for a successful future.

  3. UB Newsletter February 2024

    On February 24, 1986, a congressional resolution declared that Saturday, February 28, 1986 will be designated as “National TRIO Day.” Every year since then, our community has reserved the fourth Saturday in February as National TRIO Day—an opportunity to focus the nation’s attention on disadvantaged young people and the support they need for success.

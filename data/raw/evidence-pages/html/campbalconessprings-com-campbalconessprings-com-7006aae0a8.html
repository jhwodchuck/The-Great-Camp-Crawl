A summer camp in the heart of the Texas Hill Country for boys and girls, ages 6-16

A secret, small paradise

Camp Balcones Springs is located in the beautiful Texas Hill Country, just 40 miles northwest of Austin. Established in 1993, founded on Christian principles, CBS is among Texas’ most progressive camps, redefining the overnight summer camp experience for boys and girls, ages 6 to 16.

A camp mom, as well as the camp owner.

If you’re wondering if your child will be safe at camp, if the experience will be uplifting, if they will have fun, or be successful making new friends — I get it. There’s a lot to think about.

Why send your child to our summer camp?

Because this is a HUGE opportunity for growth. As a parent, I know you want your child to mature, learn to persevere, develop interpersonal skills, take a break from technology, unwind from the stress of negative peer pressure - the same things I wanted for my three children. You know what? It takes mountains of courage, joy, and love to grow a great kid.

Summer camp is an amazing, exciting, non-stop, unplugged, outdoors, stay-up-late, try-new-things, grow-an-inch experience! Kids don’t know the half of what’s waiting for them, but they can’t wait to get to camp!

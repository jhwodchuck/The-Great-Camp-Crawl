    Timpoochee Overnight Summer Camp

    Looking for a week away where you can be yourself, make lasting friendships, explore coastal Florida, and have so much fun you’ll be wishing you stayed all summer? 4-H Camp Timpoochee creates all those experiences and so much more!

    A week at Camp Timpoochee begins with arrival on Monday morning and ends with departure Friday morning. Each week, campers will engage in various activities and programs tailored to specific weekly themes. Some activities that occur every week, no matter the theme, include swimming, crafts, campfires, outdoor learning, and recreational games. Nutritious and delicious meals are provided from Monday lunch through Friday breakfast, and lodging is provided in air-conditioned cabins with bunk beds. 

    Each camper will be a part of a cabin group that is always supervised by at least two camp staff. Campers’ safety is of utmost importance for all our camp staff, which is why we take so much care in training our camp staff and in managing our facilities and activities appropriately. In the case of illness or injury, parents will be notified and may be required to pick up their child from camp early.

    Registration is open to any youth aged 8-16 years, as of September 1stof the previous fall. For more information about specific weeks at camp and on how to register, please see below. For any questions regarding overnight summer camp programs or to schedule a tour, please email us at timpoochee4h@ifas.ufl.edu or give us a call at (850) 897-2224. Please note we will not be able to offer tours during our staff training or summer season.

    JR Counselor Program

    Tuition $425/week

    Teens 4-H age 14-16 are able to apply to be a Jr. Counselor at Camp Timpoochee for all the weeks of summer camp below, except Just for Teens Camp.

    Camp Timpoochee will accept a maximum of eight Jr. Counselors per week. Jr. Counselors will pay the early bird fee of $450/week for each week of camp they attend as a Jr. Counselor. If accepted into the Jr. Counselors Program for the summer, you will receive detailed registration instructions in your acceptance letter.

    We highly encourage you to read through the description for each week of camp below and pick a week(s) with a theme you are passionate about and are available to attend this summer! 

    Resources:

    • Timpoochee Summer Camp Handbook
    • Timpoochee Residential Packing List

    Registration for all summer camp programs will be open 9:00AM EST on February 2, 2026 and will be available on a first-come-first serve basis. Individual camper registrations cannot be made, or positions held, over the phone or via email.

    All registrations must be reserved using 4-H Online or by using the links in the description. Full payment of registration fees must also be received to secure a place at camp.

    Upon receipt of the camper’s completed forms in 4-H online and payment, a confirmation email will be sent to confirm registration is complete and their space is confirmed.

    No better way to kick off summer 2026 than in the great outdoors!

    Join us for a week fully immersed in the wild beauty of Camp Timpoochee as we explore the amazing world of plants and wildlife.

    From identifying native species to digging in the dirt and discovering the fascinating world of bugs, Wild Outdoors Camp gives youth the chance to connect with nature like never before. Campers will enjoy guided nature walks, hands-on learning, and adventures through the diverse ecosystems found at Timpoochee—all while making summer memories that will last a lifetime.

    Time to crank up those imaginations and discover where magic meets science!

    Join us for our 6th summer of exploring the fascinating space where the magic of the world meets real-life science.

    Through hands-on experiments and creative art projects, campers will dive into the wonders of chemistry, physics, wildlife ecology, and robotics—discovering how curiosity and creativity bring science to life. Throughout the week, campers will conduct exciting experiments, craft beautiful works of art, and collect specimens from the incredible natural world around them. It’s a week designed to inspire wonder, spark innovation, and celebrate the joy of learning in the great outdoors!

    This camp is sure to make a splash in both marine and freshwater ecosystems!

    From beaches and springs to estuaries, lakes, and rivers—Florida’s waters have it all, and we’re diving into the science behind them.

    Campers will explore the habitats that make our state so unique, learning how to monitor water quality like real scientists, identify important wetlands, and discover the wonders of the Choctawhatchee Bay up close. With hands-on activities, outdoor exploration, and plenty of time in (and around) the water, it’s the perfect blend of science, adventure, and fun.

    Join us for an exclusive, teen-only week at Camp Timpoochee!

    This exciting session is designed just for older campers who are ready to take their summer experience to the next level.

    Teens will enjoy all their favorite parts of camp while building leadership, teamwork, and essential “adulting” skills through fun, hands-on activities. And that’s not all—campers will once again travel off-site for an unforgettable adventure, making memories that will last a lifetime!

    Must NOT have attended an overnight camp at Timpoochee before.

    Do you have a camper who’s a little too young—or just not quite ready—for a full week of overnight camp? Then Try-A-Night is the perfect introduction to the Camp Timpoochee experience!

    This 24-hour minicamp is designed to help campers build confidence being away from home, experience staying in a cabin, and join in on some of our most exciting evening programs. It’s a fun, gentle first step into the world of overnight camp—full of adventure, friendship, and discovery!

    Do you have what it takes to thrive in the great outdoors?

    Join us for a week of hands-on adventure as we explore the essential skills for surviving in the wild!

    Campers will learn the basics of fire and shelter building, compass navigation, and even how to cook over a campfire. Throughout the week, participants will put their new knowledge to the test in an action-packed survival challenge, building confidence, teamwork, and a true appreciation for the power of nature.

    Get ready, young scientists, for a week full of laughs, creativity, and explosive S.T.E.A.M. fun! Campers will dive into hands-on experiments that connect science, technology, engineering, art, and math in exciting and unexpected ways. From colorful chemical reactions to creative art projects, every activity is designed to spark curiosity and imagination.

    A Camp Timpoochee classic continues!

    Campers will dive into a week full of ocean exploration, building on beloved Marine Camp traditions that make this program so special. From netting in the bay and conducting scientific dissections with local experts to caring for marine creatures in our on-site laboratory, each day is packed with discovery and excitement. Throughout the week, campers will learn why studying the oceans matters and how these vast ecosystems help our world on land thrive. And who knows—a special guest might even make a splash during the week!

Main Camp

Our classic, choice-based program for 6-16 year-old campers

The Experience:
In this choice-based program, campers express their unique interests to create their own schedules for 3-day blocks. Campers are provided with high-quality instruction and the support and structure to build skills and pass levels over the session and from year to year.

A structured, shorter experience for younger, or middle-grade new campers or campers who need a shorter session.

Harmony Land Camp

Our HLC programs allow campers to try out camp life with extra support. HLC campers live with staff in one of our newest cabins, purposely built for HLC right in the heart of camp with their own bathrooms with showers. Campers live and play together, making new friends and experiencing lots of activities – some with main camp and some that are special just for HLC.

Counselor-in-Training (CIT)

Counselor-in-Training (CIT)

A leadership-building program for 16-year-old campers


The Counselor in Training program spans between being a Runoia camper and learning how to teach and counsel youth. As a CIT you help counselors in residential life and activity programs to teach and support children to have a successful time learning. CITs will have the opportunity to earn certifications like Red Cross Lifeguarding and identify their program areas in camp. CITs will also perform community service and leadership work.

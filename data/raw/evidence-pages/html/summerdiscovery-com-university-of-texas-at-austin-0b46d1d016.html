Summer at University of Texas at Austin

The University of Texas at Austin is distinguished by its illustrious history, dynamic culture, and exceptional academic standing, particularly in the arts, business, and STEM disciplines. Every Summer Discovery course at UT Austin is developed by the university and taught by university faculty and instructors, and thanks to our ten-year relationship with the school, our students enjoy unprecedented and exclusive access to campus facilities not available through other summer programs. 


The University of Texas at Austin program allows students to engage with esteemed UT Austin faculty members and participate in immersive summer courses or full-day academies covering a multiple fields of study, such as medicine, engineering, fashion, music business, neuroscience, psychology, and sports management. 


The #1 Pre-College Enrichment Program Recommended by High School College Counselors


Students who excel at UT Austin are characterized by their ambition, curiosity, and enthusiasm for new experiences. They possess a deep love for learning and readily embrace academic challenges, making the most of the university's outstanding faculty and wide range of courses. These students are also socially active, and they value being part of a vibrant, diverse community. They love exploring, engaging in activities and adventures as they soak up the energetic campus vibe. 


With full access to UT Austin’s 431-acre campus, Summer Discovery students can enjoy incredible pool parties, rock climbing events, golf simulators, as well as a various courts for basketball, tennis and pickleball. 


Situated in the heart of Austin, the university benefits from the city's vibrant cultural scene, which includes live music, festivals, and a plethora of outdoor activities. This prime location provides students with easy access to an array of entertainment options, restaurants, cafes, shops, gardens, museums, and more, all within a thriving local community.

University of Texas at Austin Programs

At Summer Discovery's UT Austin program, high school students have the opportunity to engage in immersive courses or full-day academies taught by official UT Austin instructors and experts in their respective fields, providing students with hands-on experience and a genuine taste of college-level education. Academics span an array of different fields, such as medicine, civic engagement, neuroscience, engineering, fashion, psychology, sports management, music business, real estate & property management, and more. 


Students also have the opportunity to participate in engaging site visits. For example, medicine academy students visit the Regional Simulation Center to get up close and personal with the medical field, even earning their first aid and CPR certification if they choose during the course of the program. 


Likewise, students in neuroscience visit the acclaimed Anatomy Lab at the University of Texas as part of their studies, and students studying fashion, product development and merchandising visit Stitch Texas, an innovative apparel and product design and manufacturing company. 


At the end of the program, students receive a branded Certificate of Completion from the University of Texas, Austin, a detailed letter highlighting their experience in the program, and a personal transcript.

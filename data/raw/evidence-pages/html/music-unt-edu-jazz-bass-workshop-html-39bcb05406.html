Lynn Seaton Jazz Double Bass Workshop at UNT

June 22-26, 2026

Overnight campers will arrive and check into the residence hall on Sunday, June 21. Residence halls open at 3:00pm. The workshop concludes after the Friday evening Bass Bash Concert.
About the Workshop

Professor Lynn Seaton has established himself as one of the leading performers and teachers of the jazz bass in the U.S. today. He brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to his annual Jazz Double Bass Workshop.  This intimate workshop is limited to 11 upright bass players. These spots are first-come/first-served, thus your spot is not fully secured until you have completed registration and made your final payment. The workshop is open to advanced high school (14+), college, professional, and serious amateur upright bassists wishing to expand their capabilities. Beginner-level players should register for the Jeff Bradetich Workshop.

The Lynn Seaton Jazz Double Bass Workshop offers an intensive week of study and performance opportunities for the upright jazz bassist.  

Topics to be covered include:

  • Upright technique
  • Developing walking bass lines
  • Small group playing with a rhythm section
  • Music theory
  • Jazz bass history and literature
  • Bass ensembles

Participants also receive coachings from Professor Seaton and attend faculty concerts throughout the week. 

The workshop concludes with the annual Bass Bash Concert featuring all participants performing in bass ensembles on Friday evening. Family and friends are encouraged to attend this free concert!

Tuition & Fees

  • Overnight camper - $1,200 which includes

    • Registration fee
    • Tuition
    • Housing
    • Three meals per day (Sunday dinner-Friday dinner)
    • Parking
  • Day Camper - $925 which includes:
    • Registration fee
    • Tuition
    • Lunch & dinner daily (Monday-Friday)
    • Parking
  • Overnight campers will arrive on Sunday, June 21 and depart on Friday, June 26.
  • Overnight campers have the option to depart on Saturday, June 27 due to travel needs for an additional fee of $100 per night.
  • Overnight campers must provide their own bedding (extra-long twin size), pillows, towels, and toiletries.
  • Day campers will arrive at 9:00am on Monday, June 22 for camp registration.
Registration Information

To register, visit https://app.getacceptd.com/untmusicworkshops. Begin by selecting your Area (Jazz) and Program (6/22-6/26 Lynn Seaton Jazz Double Bass Workshop). For technical questions, please visit the Acceptd Help Desk.

Registration Deadline

  • June 1, 2026 or until 11 spots are filled

Registration Policy

  • Payment is due in full at the time of registration.
  • We are unable to provide partial refunds for unused services including meals and parking.
  • Participants must be at least 14 years of age by the first day of the workshop to participate.
  • Enrollment is limited to 11 participants.
    • Email to be placed on the waitlist if workshop is full - music.camps@unt.edu. 
Refund Policy

  • Requested by May 15, 2026 – Full refund less $80 administrative fee

  • Requested by June 1, 2026 – Full refund less $230 administrative fee

  • We are unable to provide partial refunds for unused services including meals and parking.

    • We are unable to process refunds requested after June 1, 2026.

    • No refunds will be given should a participant be dismissed for violating UNT policy or program rules provided on the first day. 

Sample Schedule

Overnight campers will arrive on Sunday afternoon to check into the residence hall. Official camp registration will be 9:00-10:00am on Monday.

*A more detailed schedule will be provided approximately one week before the workshop begins. The schedule is subject to change, and any changes will be communicatd to participants in a timely  manner.

Sample Schedule

9:30am - 11:00am Technique
11:15am - 12:00pm Listening Session
12:00pm - 1:30pm Lunch
1:30pm - 3:30pm Rhythm Section (students alternate getting coached while playing with a rhythm section)
3:40pm - 4:00pm Technique Review
4:00pm - 5:00pm Bass Ensembles
5:00pm - 7:30pm Practice Time and Dinner
7:30pm Faculty Concert (Student Concert on Friday)

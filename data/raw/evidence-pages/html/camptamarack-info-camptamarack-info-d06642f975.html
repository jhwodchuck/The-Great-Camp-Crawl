Welcome to Camp Tamarack

Ontario’s Best Overnight Camp For Kids

Camp Tamarack has earned the confidence and loyalty of thousands of campers, parents and staff since 1981. Tamarack’s large private property, surrounding a pristine lake in the heart of Muskoka, is the magical setting for the building of skills, confidence, friendships and memories that will last a lifetime. With an incredible array of land, water, arts and other special and unique programs and opportunities, including a significant amount of individual choice, there’s something for everyone! As our camp song says “Tamarack, I wanna go back!”

Explore Camp Activities

  • Land
  • Water
  • Arts
  • Extreme

Tamarack’s variety of facilities on its private 1,100 acre property are ideal for hosting a wide range of awesome land activities, including court and field sports such as tennis, baseball and hockey, as well as archery, outdoor skills, martial arts and more!

Learn More

At Camp Tamarack, we love having fun on the water! With exclusive use of our very own private Duck Lake, we’re able to offer a huge array of water activities, including a high quality, individualized instructional swim program, sailing, canoeing, kayaking, flyboarding, waterskiing, wakeboarding and more!

Learn More

Creative and inspiring arts programs are at the very core of everything that defines Camp Tamarack. Our various arts-oriented opportunities include popular activities like dance, film/video, music, visual arts, theatre, cooking and more which allow campers to use their imaginations and release their inner artist!

Learn More

We take the camper experience to an “extreme” level with activities such as rock climbing, mountain biking, high and low ropes courses and more, that will test your strength, spirit and sense of adventure!

What are Retreats?

New Life Ranch is one camp, across two sites, with one mission. Regardless of which camp you are staying for your retreat, you can expect the same level of service.

Biblically Based

Our primary focus is making sure we serve you well so you can focus on proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ and equipping believers for ministry.

Focused on Relationships

We see our retreats ministry as a partnership with like-minded organizations. Our goal is not just to host your weekend well, but to build a lasting relationship with leaders and guests.

A Retreat Just for You

No two retreats are alike, and we make sure to address the individual needs of your group. We'll work with you before, during, and after to make sure your retreat runs smoothly!

Cabins

With western charm and modern amenities, our cabins serve as the perfect space for relaxation and community. We have 36 separate bunk-style cabins, along with 17 motel style units, to accommodate groups of sizes 25-600.

Learn More

Meeting Spaces

We have a variety of meeting spaces across camp that can accommodate up to 600 people. All of our meeting spaces are equipped with audio and video capabilities.

Learn More

Facilities

All of our facilities are designed to create a distraction-free experience for your retreat. From our all-you-can-eat dining hall to tons of activities, you and your retreat will feel comfortable, taken care of, and refreshed.

What’s included in the cost?

Retreat cost includes cabin-style lodging, all-you-can-eat meals, meeting spaces, and access to basic recreational activities. A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your dates. The remaining balance is due after your event once final numbers are secured. Additional costs include premium activities and private motel-style lodging.

Will someone help us plan our retreat schedule? Do you offer retreat planning resources or templates?

Yes! Our Retreats Team is here to support you every step of the way. We can provide sample schedules, planning templates, and personalized help to craft a retreat experience that fits your goals and flow.

How do I book a retreat?

Booking a retreat is easy! Simply fill out our retreat inquiry form on the website or give us a call. Our reservations manager will contact you to confirm availability, answer your questions, and guide you through the next steps.

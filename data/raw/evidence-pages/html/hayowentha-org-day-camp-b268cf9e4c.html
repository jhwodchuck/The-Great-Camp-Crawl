Camp starts here

Unplugged - On Torch Lake - Explore - connect

At Sparks Day Camp, kids are invited to experience the magic of a classic summer camp. Nestled along the beautiful shores of Torch Lake, campers dive into nature and adventure through thoughtfully designed weekly themes and a wide variety of exciting activities. From rock climbing and ziplining to arts & crafts, gaga ball, and so much more — every day is filled with fun, friendships, and unforgettable memories.

Curated for ages 5 through 10.

Staffing

Our staff bring the hype! We are both fun and safety focused. We intentionally keep our staff-to-camper ratios at a maximum of six campers to one staff. Our staff love camp! As a result, so do our campers! 

PHILOSOPHY

At Sparks we embody our core YMCA values of Caring, Honesty, Respect, Inclusion, Environmental Stewardship, Teamwork, and Responsibility. It's our mission to get kids engaging with the natural world without the distraction of modern technology. We are fully unplugged!

Weekly PROGRAM

Sparks Day Camp is located on the beautiful shores of Torch Lake and campers are able to fully immerse themselves in the natural world around them. Each week we have an exciting theme of fun, and everyday there is a highlight!

Mondays and Tuesdays: Best of our on camp activities! Rock climbing, ziplining, sport courts, archery, swimming, arts & crafts, camp games and more!

Wilderness Wednesdays: Frog catching, nature hikes, fort building, outdoor cooking, rock hunting... on Wednesdays we are explorers of the great outdoors!

Theme Thursdays: It’s the party of the week! We celebrate our weekly theme with all-camp games, special crafts, and a big, exciting activity that brings everyone together.

Family Fridays: After another action-packed day at camp, families are invited to join the fun! During the final hour, campers will share skits, sing their favorite camp songs, and enjoy a slideshow of photos from the week’s adventures.

2023 YMCA MEMBERSHIP & RATE INFORMATION

BUILDING A HEALTHY SPIRIT, MIND AND BODY FOR ALL

As a member of Sherman Lake YMCA Outdoor Center, you are part of so much more than a place to work out. You are part of a community - a place where people of all backgrounds, races, genders, and faiths come together to share in programs that build healthy spirit, mind, and body for all. We accomplish this through campfires and swim lessons, coffee, rock climbing, yoga, and wedding receptions. The Y is a place and a purpose where your membership is meaningful.

2023 MEMBERSHIP RATES

Financial Assistance:  Everyone belongs at the Y, regardless of their ability to pay. We are committed to ensuring every community member has access to the Y. Our income-based financial assistance program enables all individuals and families to become active members and participants at a rate that fits their budgets.

MEMBER BENEFIT HIGHLIGHTS…THESE AND SO MUCH MORE

  • Access to a wide variety of group fitness classes including yoga, strength training, aqua exercise, Les Mills, and spinning, all included in your membership

  • Child Watch is available at no additional cost. We provide care for your children ages 8 weeks to 8 years while you work out.

  • Two complimentary wellness sessions with our Healthy Lifestyle Coach annually.

  • Special rates for our day and overnight camps.

  • Each membership receives five (5) guest passes per calendar year to use when friends or family are visiting.

THIS YEAR YOUR Y…

  • Hosted multiple blood drives, collecting almost 700 units of blood to give the gift of life-saving blood to those in need.

  • Awarded more than $47,000 of financial assistance for individuals of all ages in need of support

  • Taught hundreds of children how to swim, a critical life-saving skill that builds confidence and makes it possible for them to safely enjoy water experiences.

  • Distributed fresh food boxes at three distribution sites around our community.

  • Added a Music Park and Kangaroo Jumper, provided by our capital campaign “Building Character Building Communities”, offering families new outdoor activities at Sherman Lake YMCA.

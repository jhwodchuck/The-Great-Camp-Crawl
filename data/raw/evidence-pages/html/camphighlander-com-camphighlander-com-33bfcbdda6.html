Building boys and girls of courage, honesty, integrity and faith

Camp Highlander is a residential summer camp in Mills River, North Carolina, devoted to the growing and nurturing of boys and girls, ages 6-16, in a safe, fun-filled, and electronics-free environment. Since 1957, we have partnered with parents and families to build character and confidence in the lives of children as we provide lifelong memories, authentic friendships, and a camp family to come home to year after year.

  • Girls' Camp fosters a community in which each girl is a valued member of an irreplaceable sisterhood.Girls' Camp
  • Boys' Camp exists to foster and promote a firm sense of brotherhood and belonging.Boys' Camp

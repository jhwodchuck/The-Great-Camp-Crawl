Start Making Your Plans, Now!

"I am happy to announce the Summer Schedule for the 2026 camping season," said President & CEO, Stephen Mabry. Pictured at right is a snap-shot of the schedule.

  1. Our summer begins with three sessions of "regular", residential youth camps for children with physical disabilities, ages 7-16 will be held. (No families at camp these weeks).
  2. We will then host Camp David and a session for children who have Down Syndrome.
  3. The next two weeks will see the return Dr. Ponder and the medical team and are set aside for our campers who have type-1 diabetes.
  4. Camp Discovery will then be back in the house. A one-of-a-kind experience for children who have cancer or are in remission.
  5. Finally, we will end the summer with our innovative program for families.

If your child has never attended TLC, please choose the "Open New Account" option. If you already have an account from a prior year, please choose that option to login and register.

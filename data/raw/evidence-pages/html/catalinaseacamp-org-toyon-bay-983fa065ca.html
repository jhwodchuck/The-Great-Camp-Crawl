Welcome to

Toyon Bay

Home to Catalina Sea Camp’s 1-Week and 2.5-Week sessions, Toyon Bay is tucked into a private cove just a few miles from Avalon—an unforgettable setting where campers dive into ocean adventures, exploration, and lifelong friendships.

Our

Facility

Toyon Bay is the perfect setting for a life-changing summer adventure. From 1-week sessions to 2.5-week stays, campers enjoy a large dining hall, comfortable dorms, and activity spaces designed for unforgettable memories with friends old and new.

Explore Our Program Overview

Features

Dorm-Style Housing

Campers are housed in comfortable dorms with bunk beds.

Features

Bathrooms

Campers share dorm-style bathrooms with sinks, toilets, and showers.

Features

Dining Hall

Buffet-style meals are served in our dining hall.

Features

Marine Science Labs

Aquariums and touch tanks allow campers to get up-close to ocean animals.

Features

Dive Deck

Campers get outfitted for snorkeling and scuba diving on our Dive Deck.

Features

Yacht Club

Sailing classes are based out of our Yacht Club with a wacky clubhouse vibe.

Features

Seafood Cookery

Our outdoor kitchen is the perfect place for campers to whip up fun new recipes.

Features

Climbing Wall

Our climbing wall with auto-belay system provides a safe space for campers to challenge themselves.

Features

Rec Areas

Campers can spend their free time in our recreation areas, including Gaga ball, nine-square, basketball courts, and volleyball court.

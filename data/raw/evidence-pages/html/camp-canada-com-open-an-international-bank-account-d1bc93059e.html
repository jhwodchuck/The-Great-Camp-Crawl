How to Open an International Bank Account: Guide for Students & Travellers

Here at Camp Canada, we know that preparing for an international adventure can be overwhelming especially if you’re living, working or travelling abroad. That's why we've partnered with Wise to make international banking hassle-free not just for our summer camp staff, but for students and travellers too as we know many of our participants stay in Canada after a summer at camp! This guide will walk you through the simple steps of opening an international bank account, so you can easily manage your money on your adventure abroad.
Camp Canada
20/11/2024

Why Choose an International Bank Account?

‍

Opening an international bank can have many benefits. They offer the opportunity for seamless global transactions, competitive exchange rates and fees, and the ability to hold multiple currencies in one account. Wise allows you to transfer and receive money from over 160 countries and 40 currencies. So, whether you're joining us at camp or embarking on another adventure, here's why opening an account with Wise is your best choice.

‍

If you're working at summer camp in Canada, you'll NEED a Canadian bank account to receive your salary. Opening an international bank account with Wise is a convenient way to do this.

‍

With Camp Canada, you can get a free card or fee-free transfer (for new users only) when you sign up through our link. This exclusive offer allows you to make one transfer of up to £500 (or equivalent currency) fee-free.

‍

Benefits Of Opening An International Bank Account With Wise

‍

  • Pre-Arrival Setup - You can open your account before leaving for your trip
  • Smooth Transition - There are no complex banking procedures upon arrival at your destination
  • Competitive exchange rates - Wise uses the real exchange rate, avoiding high bank fees
  • Fast, reliable transfers - Transfers are often completed within 1-2 business days
  • Transparent fees - You can see all fees upfront before making a transfer
  • Multicurrency accounts - Hold and manage money in over 40 currencies
  • Immediate Access via the mobile App - You can use your account as soon as you arrive and manage your money conveniently on the go

How to Set Up Your Wise Account

‍

  • Sign up and create an account through our link.
  • Verify your identity and follow the simple verification process.
  • Choose between a Free card or fee-free transfer.

‍

If you do need any support when creating your account, please reach out to Wise's customer support for assistance.

‍

‍Top Tip: Make sure to let your home bank know about your international account to avoid any potential issues!

‍

And there you have it! By following these tips and taking advantage of our exclusive offer with Wise, you can make your time abroad more enjoyable and financially stress free. So don't wait, open your Wise International bank account today!

Host a retreat or school group at Camp Mokulēʻia

Looking for the ideal setting to inspire creativity and strengthen bonds? Our camp offers a unique retreat experience that combines the beauty of our ʻāina with top-notch facilities tailored for your organizational needs.

﻿ 

Our customizable programs include team-building activities, educational programs, and recreational activities. Plus, our dedicated staff will ensure every detail is handled, allowing you to focus on what truly matters— growth and success.

Additional Fees/Discounts

Linen Rental: $125 (optional)

Payment Options

1. Deposit due at time of enrollment. Balance of account is processed in even, monthly installments paid on the 1st of each month with the final payment on June 1. Credit Card, E-Checks, or Cash are accepted.

2. PAYMENT IN FULL at time of registration. Credit Card, E-Check, Cash,
or Wire Transfer payments are accepted.

Time to Be a Kid

Explore Camp

Brother-Sister Sleepaway Camp in the Hudson Valley.

  • “[My children] have not stopped telling us stories, which echo the unbelievable creativity, humor, and kindness of Scatico staff and campers. The stories also communicate how completely carefree, safe, and, most importantly, liberating, camp feels for them. It’s a place that they can be themselves. There is no greater gift.”

  • "You feel the heartbeat of Scatico as soon as you step onto camp. Scatico deeply understands what children need- spirit, tradition, and meaningful ways to connect without the noise of modern life. Scatico is a place to slow down and just be a kid."

  • "Thank you for seeing all the amazing that our son is and for giving him the space and opportunity to be himself and for creating, fostering and nurturing an environment that allows that. From the staff, to the campers, to the activities. We feel so incredibly grateful that we found Scatico. Thank you for being such a wonderful bright spot..."

  • "In all the things that truly matter in life, Scatico knocks it out of the park. Those that experience Scatico are all the better for it."

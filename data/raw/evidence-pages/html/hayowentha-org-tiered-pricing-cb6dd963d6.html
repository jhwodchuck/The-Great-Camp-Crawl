New for 2025: Tiered Pricing

Hayo-Went-Ha Camps is excited to introduce tiered pricing in 2025! As camp costs rise, we recognize the need to adjust our fees to keep camp affordable while sustaining our operations and maintaining the highest level of quality. We’re also committed to recruiting and paying our staff competitive salaries and providing top-notch equipment and exceptional wilderness programming.

Here’s how tiered pricing works:

  • Tier A: Reflects the true cost of camp.
  • Tier B: Offers a more affordable option, subsidized by our annual fund.

Families can choose either tier at registration, with no difference in the camper experience. This new structure helps maintain affordability while encouraging those who can contribute more to ensure Hayo-Went-Ha provides exceptional staffing and programs for everyone.

Please select your camper’s desired program/session(s) and click “Add to cart.”  Then, choose Tier A or Tier B. Your Cart on the right side of the page will keep a running total of the applicable fees. Once selected, click “Continue” to proceed. 

Should your program/session of choice be unavailable, your camper will be placed on the waitlist. If a spot opens up and your camper can come off the waitlist, the Registrar will contact you to confirm this.

Come Find MORE at
Camp Fire Camp El Tesoro

A sharp smell of fresh cut grass, distant laughter and “ker-splash!” at the pool. Ripping open a letter that sings of home, softly talking while playing cards or painstakingly making sure each thread of a friendship bracelet is “just right”- these small moments are the glue that bind a summer at camp. Since 1934, Camp El Tesoro‘s overnight camp has inspired youth in all moments, quiet and loud.

Get ready to hike a shaded trail, splash in the creek, sing your heart out around an evening campfire and share time with trusted friends.

El Tesoro overnight camps provide an immersive experience for campers to explore the outdoors, make new friends and awaken their sense of adventure. Intentional moments at camp encourage youth to step out of their comfort zone, try something new and find more:

Connections • Independence • Self-Confidence • Adventure • Laughter
Questions? Contact Us

Camp Fire Camp El Tesoro is proud to be Accredited by the American Camp Association for more than 70 years.

Find MORE Growth at Camp

El Tesoro’s overnight camp has inspired youth to let their curiosity be their guide – to nature, their world and what they can accomplish.

Age-appropriate activities develop confidence and growth mindsets in our campers while they build new skills and make new friends in the outdoors. The wide range of activities from horseback riding and archery to crafts, woodshop, swimming and kayaking create opportunities that show each camper they can achieve whatever they set their minds to.

You Belong Here

Camp Fire offers programs where every young person can be their whole self and connect with others and nature in a safe, affirming environment.

Camp accepts you for who you are.
At Camp YOU can be YOU.
Camp shows you how to love nature, yourself and others.

Start building a lifetime of magical camp memories filled with the traditions of camp life and spark lifelong growth. We can’t wait to see you cross our iconic swinging bridge this summer!

Camp Fire’s Statement of Inclusion: Camp Fire believes in the dignity and the intrinsic worth of every human being. We welcome, affirm, and support young people and adults of all abilities and disabilities, experiences, races, ethnicities, socio-economic backgrounds, sexual orientations, gender identities and expressions, religion and non-religion, citizenship and immigration status, and any other category people use to define themselves or others. We strive to create safe and inclusive environments that celebrate diversity and foster positive relationships.

Find MORE Space to Explore

Camp El Tesoro is located on 223-acres where the Brazos River and Fall Creek meet in Granbury, Texas.

The camp has welcomed five generations of campers for almost 90 years. Campers stay in beautifully modern cabins featuring full restrooms and electricity.

Our highly-trained diverse staff members lead youth through active exploration, building connections and reflection while finding more time to laugh, listen and grow together in a safe and enriching environment.

Plus, in each session, we welcome campers with mild intellectual or developmental disabilities. Our Inclusion Program enriches the camp experience for all campers.

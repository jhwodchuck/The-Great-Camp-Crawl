Looking for an unforgettable overnight summer camp for your kids or teens in North Carolina for 2026? From old-school traditional camps to wild-and-free nature camps in the Blue Ridge Mountains, we hand-picked 10 of North Carolina’s most incredible sleepaway camps that are sure to give your kids the best summer ever. Want more options? Browse all North Carolina overnight camps. 🌞

Camp Timberlake & Camp Merri-Mac – Marion, NC and Black Mountain, NC

Located just 25 minutes apart, Camp Timberlake for boys and Camp Merri-Mac for girls are two exceptional single-gender Christian camps with matching dates, rates, and coordinated schedules—including sibling days throughout the summer. Every day at camp leads to growth through friends and adventure, and campers feel like insiders from the very beginning. Both camps offer the quintessential sleepaway experience, top-notch facilities, incredible staff, and days packed full of unforgettable activities, outdoor adventures, and cherished traditions. 2 and 4 week sessions.

Camp Mondamin – Zirconia, NC

Camp Mondamin is a non-religious, non-competitive boys’ camp in the Blue Ridge Mountains. Purposely rustic and screen-free, this traditional camp has a big emphasis on outdoor adventure and wilderness trips. Unlike many camps, Mondamin allows boys to choose their activities each day. This self-directed approach is perfect for boys craving independence or a reset from an overscheduled school year. Alongside a small enrollment size, excellent staff, high-quality instruction, and inclusive community, Mondamin is the right fit for any boy looking for fun, friendship, and real adventure.

Camp Mondamin

Zirconia, NC
  • All Boys
  • Ages 6-17
  • Traditional

Falling Creek Camp – Zirconia, NC

Falling Creek Camp is a traditional Christian boys’ camp nestled on over 900 private mountaintop acres in western North Carolina. With a strong focus on character development, adventure, and unplugged living, boys in grades 1st–11th choose from over 30 activities including mountain biking, rock climbing, fishing, and team sports. Campers also embark on daily and multi-day wilderness trips, building resilience and camaraderie. With 1- to 4-week sessions and a Junior Camp for first-timers, Falling Creek offers a faith-centered experience where boys grow in confidence, leadership, and their connection to nature.

Falling Creek Camp

Zirconia, NC
  • All Boys
  • Ages 6-17
  • Traditional

Green River Preserve – Cedar Mountain, NC

Set on a stunning 3,400-acre private wildlife preserve in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Green River Preserve is a coed camp for curious, creative kids who love nature. Campers in grades 1–9 explore waterfalls, caves, and forests on mentor-led hikes, while older campers (grades 8–12) join expedition programs. With a focus on environmental stewardship and experiential learning, GRP offers activities like fly fishing, theater, pottery, and mountain biking. Small cabin groups and a purposefully limited enrollment create a close-knit, inclusive community where kids thrive.

Green River Preserve

Cedar Mountain, NC
  • Coed
  • Ages 7-17
  • Traditional, Specialty

YMCA Camp Hanes – King, NC

YMCA Camp Hanes is a coed camp for ages 6-15 located on 500 scenic acres at the base of Sauratown Mountain. Camp Hanes blends traditional camp fun with off-site adventures like rafting and caving in epic week-long sessions. Campers enjoy activities such as ziplining, paintball, and the Treetop Village, plus family-style meals and a peanut-free environment. With excellent programing, a strong emphasis on personal growth, and affordable rates, Camp Hanes is perfect for those wanting a shorter camp experience.

YMCA Camp Hanes

King, NC
  • Coed
  • Ages 6-15
  • Traditional

AICL Summer Enrichment Camp – Mars Hill, NC

Appalachian Institute for Creative Learning (AICL) is an academic summer camp held at Mars Hill University for rising 3rd to 12th graders. With small class sizes and a wide-ranging curriculum—from science and writing to crafts, music, and performance—campers dive into four daily classes designed to spark curiosity and challenge every learner. Afternoons feature outdoors activities like hiking, soccer, and chalk art, while evenings bring campus-wide alien hunts, discos, and talent shows. AICL is a vibrant, inclusive camp where learning is joyful and self-expression is celebrated.

AICL Summer Enrichment Camp

The Vineyard Camp – Westfield, NC

The Vineyard Camp is an international, interdenominational Christian camp offering over 35 activities on a beautiful campus in Westfield. With one of the lowest camper-to-staff ratios in the country, Vineyard provides a deeply personal experience rooted in faith, fun, and intellectual exploration. Campers enjoy devotionals, outdoor adventures, and a wide range of electives—from sports and arts to apologetics and leadership. Welcoming children of all backgrounds, Vineyard fosters spiritual growth and lifelong friendships in a loving, Christ-centered environment.

The Vineyard Camp

Westfield, NC
  • Coed
  • Ages 5-16
  • Traditional

YMCA Camp Weaver – Greensboro, NC

YMCA Camp Weaver is a 132-acre ACA-accredited camp just minutes from downtown Greensboro. Known for its exceptional facilities—including a lake, pool, ropes course, and climate-controlled cabins—Camp Weaver offers a dynamic mix of outdoor fun and personal development. Campers enjoy horseback riding, ziplining, paintball, and even a mini farm, all while building confidence and social-emotional skills. With day and overnight options, Camp Weaver is a vibrant, inclusive space where kids grow, connect, and make memories that last a lifetime.

YMCA Camp Weaver

Greensboro, NC
  • Coed
  • Ages 7-16
  • Traditional

Camp Rockmont – Black Mountain, NC

Camp Rockmont is a Christian boys’ camp located on 580 acres in the Blue Ridge Mountains, just east of Asheville. Rockmont provides a transformative sleepaway experience rooted in faith, friendship, and outdoor adventure. Boys have over 40 activities to choose from like blacksmithing, mountain biking, rock climbing, and watersports. Campers live in tight-knit cabin groups and enjoy thrilling camp-wide activities where boys can have fun, make friends, and grow as individuals in a meaningful Christian community. 1 to 4 week sessions.

Camp Rockmont

Black Mountain, NC
  • All Boys
  • Ages 7-17
  • Traditional

Camp Arrowhead – Zirconia, NC

Camp Arrowhead is a traditional all-boys overnight camp in Zirconia, NC, offering a rugged, unplugged experience rooted in self-reliance, leadership, and adventure. Campers dive into hands-on activities like blacksmithing, canoeing, and woodshop, all while building confidence and making friends in a tight-knit community. With rustic cabins and a focus on personal growth and self-expression, Arrowhead delivers a meaningful sleepaway experience where boys learn to lead, explore, and create.

Camp Arrowhead

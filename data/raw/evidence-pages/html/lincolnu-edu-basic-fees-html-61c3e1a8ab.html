Student Tuition and Fees

Lincoln University of Missouri offers flat-rate tuition for undergraduate students enrolled in 12–18 credit hours. Below you’ll find tuition and standard fee information for undergraduate and graduate students.

Fee schedules are provided for both the 2025–2026 and 2026–2027 academic years. Please select the appropriate year below.

The tables that follow outline tuition, incidental fees and mandatory fees. Housing and meal plan costs are listed separately.

Note: The Board of Curators reserves the right to change tuition and fees at any time.

For questions or assistance, please contact the Office of Student Financial Services (Financial Aid) at 573-681-6156 or SFS@LincolnU.edu.

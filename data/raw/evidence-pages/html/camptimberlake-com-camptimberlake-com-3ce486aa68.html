Our Community
An outstanding summer camp can be one of the most important experiences in a boy’s life. Located in the mountains of western North Carolina, Timberlake is a Christian summer camp for boys. We strive not only to provide a summer of fun and recreation but, in a larger sense, to develop a boy’s character so that they will be stronger mentally, physically and spiritually.

Welcome to Camp Timberlake!

These are the first words that every boy hears when he arrives at camp, and they mean a lot to us. We love welcoming boys to our camp family and Timberlake is a small enough program where everybody knows your name. It’s a place where new boys feel like camp insiders from the very beginning.

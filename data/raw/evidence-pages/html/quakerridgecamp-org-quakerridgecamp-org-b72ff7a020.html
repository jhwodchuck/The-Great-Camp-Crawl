Quaker Ridge Camp & Conference Center
Christian retreat center in Woodland Park, CO

Quaker Ridge Camp is a unique, year-round venue for your Christian ministry! Located on 600 acres in Pike National forest in the Rocky Mountains, Quaker Ridge Camp provides an amazing setting for Christian youth camps in the summer and Christian retreat groups throughout the rest of the year.

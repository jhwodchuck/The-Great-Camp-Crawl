WELCOME TO KAMP!

Kanakuk operates as a fun-filled summer camp that specializes in developing both Biblical knowledge and athletics, empowering kids to greater courage, confidence, and character. Kanakuk Kamps range from 1-week sessions to 4-week programs, offering flexibility to meet the needs of families. We believe that enrolling your kid in Kamp means more than sending them off for the summer—it’s an investment in their future. By developing Christ-like character, learning the value of team, and taking opportunities to try new things, kids make unforgettable memories and build lifelong friendships at Kanakuk. There’s truly no other summer camp experience like it.

Why Choose Kanakuk

Fun

Fun

From flying down the zipline to bouncing off the blob, everything we do at Kanakuk is rooted in fun, exciting experiences that allow kids to be kids!

Courage & Confidence

Courage & Confidence

With activities that challenge Kampers to grow in their courage and confidence, our Kamps teach kids of all ages to be leaders in their schools and communities.

Role Models

Role Models

Our professional staff are individually interviewed and hand-selected from universities across America and are Christ-like examples that lead and coach our Kampers during their entire Kanakuk experience.

Faith

Faith

At Kanakuk we live I’m Third—God First. Others Second. I’m Third. This foundational principle is integrated into each of our Kamp’s programming, equipping Kampers to be Christ-like examples who lead with responsibility and care.

Lifelong Friendships

Lifelong Friendships

For generations Kampers have come to Kanakuk from across the country and around the world, establishing friendships that last a lifetime.

Best. Summer. Ever!

With over 100 years of experience in Summer Camping, Kanakuk’s premier experience is built around Kids—that’s why we use K to spell Kamp around here!

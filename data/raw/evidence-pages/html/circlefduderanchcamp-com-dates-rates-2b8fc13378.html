Summer Camp Tuition Includes

At Circle F, campers dive into over 40 exciting activities, a well-rounded program and outstanding facilities built for comfort and adventure. From hearty and nutritious meals and weekly wash-and-fold laundry service to sweet treats from Cantina and Store, we handle the details-so kids can focus on making memories that last a liftime!

Save on our summer camp rates when you enroll siblings, or when you make referrals! Discover summer camp credits.

Optional Add-Ons

Paintball ($85/week)

Have a blast playing paintball five days a week, unless there is a special activity planned.

Excursion ($285 per trip)

Have fun with summer camp friends on camp theme park excursions. These trips include transportation in a motor coach bus, meals, admission, and counselor supervision for the day.

Airport Transportation ($180/each way)

Transportation is offered to and from the Orlando and Tampa airports.

Payment Policies

A non-refundable deposit of $750.00 per session per camper is required at the time of registration.

All remaining tuition and optional add-on fees are due in full by May 1.

Sibling Credit

Families who have more than one sibling attending the same session are eligible for a credit. The credit applies to every sibling after the first camper. Families will receive the following credits depending on which session the campers are registered.

  • 3 Week Session: $300.00
  • 2 Week Session: $200.00
  • 1 Week Session: $100.00

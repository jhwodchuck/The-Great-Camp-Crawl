# OUR HISTORY

## OUR MISSION

### A LEGACY OF TRUST, QUALITY, AND PASSION

We are Canadian Aqualine Sales Inc. – your trusted partner in plumbing and heating solutions for over five decades. Founded with a vision and commitment to excellence in 1971, our journey began with a modest office on Main Street in Vancouver, British Columbia, where our dedicated team of two set out to make a mark in the plumbing and heating industry. The late 70s saw us expand our footprint, opening an office in Calgary, Alberta.

From those early days with two product lines, we've evolved into now offering several hydronic, plumbing, and HVAC products in both BC and Alberta. Our growth is a testament to our unwavering dedication to quality and service. Today, our comprehensive team of 15+ in both Calgary and Delta stands strong, equipped with decades of collective experience.

### OUR COMMITMENT

At Canadian Aqualine, staying ahead of the curve is in our DNA. Two of our seasoned sales representatives hold the prestigious Red Seal designation, bringing a wealth of expertise to every interaction.

We believe in continuous learning and global engagement, regularly attending technical training sessions worldwide to keep our team abreast of the latest technologies. In-house training seminars for our customers and the trades further reflect our commitment to knowledge-sharing.

With over 10,000 square feet of warehouse space in Delta and 4,000 square feet in Calgary, we are equipped to meet your demands efficiently.

## MEET OUR TEAM

OUR PEOPLE

We take immense pride in recognizing our people as our greatest asset. The heartbeat of our success lies in the dedication and expertise of our team, creating a legacy that extends beyond decades. Our dynamic team is a mosaic of talent, skill, and affability. Committed to excellence, each member plays a pivotal role in our continued success. Their collective expertise spans diverse areas, from product knowledge to customer service, ensuring that Canadian Aqualine remains synonymous with quality and reliability.

The company owners are the embodiment of our enduring commitment. With over 30 years of unwavering loyalty to the company, they bring a wealth of experience and insight that resonates throughout every aspect of our operations.

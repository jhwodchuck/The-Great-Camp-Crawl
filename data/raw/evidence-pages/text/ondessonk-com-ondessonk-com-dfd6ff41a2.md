Get ready to offer someone you love a summer packed with thrilling outdoor adventures. Camp Ondessonk’s summer camp program is the ticket to unforgettable experiences in the heart of the Shawnee National Forest in Illinois. Whether your loved one is a seasoned camper or venturing into the wilderness for the first time, our camp offers something for everyone.

## Experience the Ultimate Adventure at Camp Ondessonk’s Summer Camp Program

We present a week of non-stop excitement, where each day is filled with epic activities like horseback riding through scenic trails, conquering our challenging rock-climbing walls, and paddling across pristine lakes. Imagine sleeping under the stars, telling stories around a crackling campfire, and forging friendships that will last a lifetime. At Camp Ondessonk, we don’t just offer a camp, we offer an adventure.

Our summer camp is designed to push boundaries and ignite the spirit of exploration in every camper. Our campers can discover the thrill of scaling new heights, mastering archery, or navigating the twists and turns of our high ropes course. Every moment is an opportunity to embrace the wild and discover what you’re truly capable of.

With over 60 years of tradition, Camp Ondessonk is not just a summer camp, it’s a place where legends are made. Our experienced staff are passionate about creating a safe, fun, and empowering environment for every camper. [Click here](https://ondessonk.com/summer-camp/) to learn more about what we have to offer then contact us and give someone you love a memorable adventure. The experience of a lifetime awaits at Camp Ondessonk!

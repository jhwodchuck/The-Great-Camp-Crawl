# Camp Carvela

Boundless Nature for the Builders & Dreamers

[Summer 2026 Schedule](https://www.campcarvela.com/schedule)

## Welcome to camp!

Sitting on 50 acres on the shores of Bass Lake in Traverse City, Camp Carvela gives campers seemingly boundless space to hike, adventure, craft, and imagine. At Carvela, we’re unplugged, giving our campers time and space to choose their own adventures every day.

With supportive staff, campers co-design what camp days look like – days that are inclusive of all and full of creativity. You’ll find Carvela campers working on masterpieces in the beloved art house, swimming in Bass Lake, playing games throughout camp, or even writing songs. There is no shortage of inspiration for campers at Carvela!

## The Carvela philosophy

At Camp Carvela, we empower young people through experiential learning. Our unique programming model emphasizes creativity, exploration, inclusion, and community building. Our goal is to make a positive impact on each member of our community by building connections to self, others, and the natural world.

[LEARN MORE](https://www.campcarvela.com/philosophy)

Collaboration

[We empower campers to co-create their camp experience  through teamwork and creativity](https://www.crystalaireadventures.com/program-philosphy)

Non-Competition

We prioritize participation, inclusion, and community-building over competition

Unplugged

We disconnect from screens to reconnect with ourselves and others in an authentic way

Free Time

We believe creativity thrives when we have unstructured time to dream and play

Playfulness

We view play as a form of self-expression and an encourage silliness, creativity, and joy

    Yumping was launched in 2009 as part of Grupo Intercom, an internet group leader in Spain and Europe for the creation and development of online classifieds business (infojobs.net, softonic.com, emagister.com, weddingspot.co.uk, etc.)..
    Today our team is comprised of 25 people, including web developers, marketers, sales associates and customer specialists obsessed with perfecting the experience of booking adventure sports and leisure time activities online..

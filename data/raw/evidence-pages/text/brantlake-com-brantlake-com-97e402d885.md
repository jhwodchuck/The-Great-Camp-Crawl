# **Brant Lake Camp** for Boys

## Over 100 Great Summers. One Family Ownership.

Brant Lake Camp, founded in 1916, is one of the oldest single-family owned camps in the country. At Brant Lake Camp, we have a thoughtful approach to caring for kids. We acknowledge that a boy’s needs change as he grows. This allows us to know each child’s personality, and recognize their strengths and needs. Nestled in the Adirondack Mountains, 3.5 hours from New York City, Brant Lake is home to boys 7 to 15 who enjoy a summer steeped in tradition and fun.

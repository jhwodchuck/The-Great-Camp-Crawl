#### Counselor-In-Training (CIT) Program:

The CIT Program is available to campers aged 15-16 and is optional but requires an application and interview.
CIT Program is 7 weeks, although a minimum of 5 weeks can be done with approval of the camp director.
Campers do not have to participate in the CIT Program and are welcome to attend as regular campers.

Canteen Fee: Canteen fee for daily snacks and camp t-shirt is included in the camp tuition

Sibling Discount: $250

Bus Transportation: $110 fee each way for bus transportation. Bus transportation available from Merrick, NY or the Bronx, NY. (Subject to restrictions)

Airport Transportation: There are two airports we can provide transportation to and from. JFK in New York and BDL in Connecticut.

- JFK: $165 fee each way for airport transportation during specified times. Flight arrival times are Sunday (6/28, 7/12, 8/2) between 11:00am-5:00pm. Flight departure times are Saturday (7/11, 8/1, 8/15) between 11:00am-7:00pm.
- BDL: $85 fee each way for airport transportation during specified times. Flight arrival times are Sunday (6/28, 7/12, 8/1) between 11:00am-5:00pm. Flight departure times are Saturday (7/11, 8/1, 8/15) between 11:00am-7:00pm.
- Arrival and departure outside of those times must be approved by the camp and incur an $85 surcharge each way.

Linen Rental: $55 per session.

## WHO WE ARE

YMCA Camp Pendalouan on the shores of Big Blue Lake offers a quiet, rustic setting for campers and groups throughout Michigan. Pendalouan is located near Muskegon, Michigan, on over 105 acres of mature oak-pine forest, adjacent to the Manistee National Forest and the White River.

**Schedule a tour with our team to see firsthand what our beautiful and historic property has to offer.**

camps

Pendalouan is where imagination and adventure come alive. Camp Pendalouan offers a variety of camp programs including Overnight Camps, Day Camps & Specialty Weekends to provide opportunities for campers of all ages! A century of operation has led Pendalouan to become a premiere Michigan camp!

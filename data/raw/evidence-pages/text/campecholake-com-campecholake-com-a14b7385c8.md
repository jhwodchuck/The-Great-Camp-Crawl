## Camp Echo Lake Is a full season co-ed sleep away camp in New York’s Adirondack Mountains

Since 1946, the Stein family has been providing summer camp experiences that are built upon a passionate dedication to human development. Camp Echo Lake is a loving and supportive camp community that looks for – and cultivates – the best in every camper and staff member. We would love to have you join our camp family!

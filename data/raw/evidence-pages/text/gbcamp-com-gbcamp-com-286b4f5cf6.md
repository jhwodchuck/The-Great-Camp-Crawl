# Welcome to GLEN BERNARD CAMP Overnight Summer Camp for Girls

---

---

Glen Bernard Camp provides a caring experience in which campers and staff develop self-confidence. The environment fosters both independence and group belonging. Fun is fundamental to the success of GBC.

---

Glen Bernard Camp is one of Ontario's premier overnight summer camps for girls and Outdoor Education Centres for co-ed school groups. Located just north of Huntsville and 6 km west of Algonquin Park in Ontario, GBC is ideally situated on 700 acres of land with a kilometer of safe shoreline along the east side of Lake Bernard.

Above all else you'll prize
Those friendships formed in camping days,
Beneath the open skies.

For comrades of the water ways,

Have worn no cheap disguise.

Though paths of life turn east or west,
Camp friends can ne'er divide.

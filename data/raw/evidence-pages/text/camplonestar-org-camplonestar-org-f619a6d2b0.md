#### CAMP starts here

Camp Lone Star is passionate about planting seeds of faith, cultivating Christian leaders, and growing the kingdom of God. Our summer camp in **La Grange** offers overnight and day camp opportunities for campers ages 5-18, plus family camp for all ages. At our **Tomball** campus, Adventure Woods provides an exciting day camp experience for campers ages 6–12.

[explore our summer camps](https://camplonestar.org/summer/)

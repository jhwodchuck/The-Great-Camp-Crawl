# Camp Runoia is a sleepaway summer camp for girls located in Belgrade Lakes, Maine.

---

Our sleepaway summer camp has been providing profound camp experiences for girls ages 6-16 since 1907. [Camp Runoia programs](https://runoia.com/about-runoia/programs/) include horseback riding, sailing, waterskiing, swimming, arts and crafts, climbing and challenge course, field sports, target sports, and more. In addition, our exciting wilderness trip program has girls traveling throughout the state of Maine for overnight hiking and canoeing trips. At Camp Runoia, we focus on personal growth and increased independence balanced with learning to be a part of a larger community. Camp Runoia is unique among summer camps. Small and tight-knit enough to provide personalized camper care and [directors](https://runoia.com/about-runoia/directors/) who are involved in [daily camp life](https://runoia.com/families/a-day-in-the-life-at-runoia/), Runoia still offers all of the amazing activities of a larger camp.

It is a place you belong to and that belongs to you. At Runoia, campers create treasured moments that they carry in their hearts each and every day throughout their lives.

[Request Info](https://runoia.com/request-info/)

# Have free time. Enjoy time with friends.

Camp Runoia is an authentic, down to earth, caring sleepaway camp for girls in Maine. 150 campers with 80 adults helping to make the experience at camp genuine and safe. Most girls stay for one session and some come for the whole summer.

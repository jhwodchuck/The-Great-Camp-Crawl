## Welcome to YMCA Camp Nissokone

YMCA Camp Nissokone is located near Lake Huron in Oscoda, Michigan. Now in our 112th year, we offer day and overnight camp for children ages 5-17 from Michigan and beyond.

**Registration for all 2026 camp sessions** are open! Busing is available from the Birmingham Family YMCA and the Plymouth Family YMCA.

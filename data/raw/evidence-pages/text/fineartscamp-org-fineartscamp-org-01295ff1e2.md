***Unplug and immerse yourself in the arts at the Sitka Fine Arts Camp

Art is magic.** It connects us as people, it builds community, raises our self-esteem, celebrates the great joys and helps us make it through the deep sorrows. Art can remind us of who we are. Art can also help us hear the voices of people forgotten. It can shock us, wake us from our slumber, challenge our assumptions and beliefs; it can also provide comfort, solace, and peace.
​
**The Sitka Fine Arts Camp** **is also magic.** Camp is a special place where communities quickly build deep bonds and, from this community, life-long friendships spring up and life-long passions are discovered. At the Sitka Fine Arts Camp we are able to be deeply present, self-expressive, empathic, curious, and engaged in the moment. We are able to work cooperatively with each other and strive to find beauty and love in the work we make. The Sitka Fine Arts Camp happens for a short time each year but during this precious time magic is created that endures far beyond the short summer season and into the fabric of our lives.*

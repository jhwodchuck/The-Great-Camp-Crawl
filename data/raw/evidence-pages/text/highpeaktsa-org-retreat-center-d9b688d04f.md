# Retreats & Rentals

**High Peak Camp's facilities are a unique blend of rustic and new. Among the rustic buildings, new structures have been placed.**

For overnight stays, our five newer dormitories sleep 176 people. Our Rocky Mountain Lodge has 25 rooms accommodating up to 90 people. For indoor, group meetings needs, our two story mill building, state of the art chapel, coach stop and lodge areas provide adequate space. Also available are various smaller cabins.

We have the capacity to host and serve meals for over 150 people.

Also available to our guests are six trout ponds, where you can fish while enjoying the beautiful views of Mount Meeker, Longs Peak, Mount Lady Washington, Estes Cone, and Twin Sisters.

For outdoor facilities, we have new zip line that was completed in 2016, a miniature golf course, archery range, as well as a 40 foot climbing tower that was completed in 2012. We have a state of the art Astroturf soccer field, sand volleyball court, and basketball court. There are various easy hiking trails on our property, and local access to Rocky Mountain National Park.

Our kitchen is the envy of many restaurants, with the capabilities of serving 300+ hungry people. Our menu features meals ranging from $9 to $15 per person, basic to upscale.

Samantha Ridgewell - Director of Camps

Samantha brings a deep passion for youth development, leadership, and community building. She earned her Bachelor’s degree in Health Science while playing Division I hockey at Merrimack College, then went on to compete professionally in the SDHL, PHF, and PWHL. A strong believer in multi-sport participation, Samantha also competed in softball, volleyball, basketball, badminton, and track & field while pursuing her hockey scholarship.

She now coaches high-level hockey, including Team Saskatchewan, where she attended the U18 Women’s National Championship and is preparing for the 2027 Canada Winter Games. In addition, Samantha coaches basketball and has attended two HOOPLA Championships, and also coaches volleyball and softball. With extensive experience working with children and teens in both school and camp settings, Samantha is dedicated to creating a fun, inclusive, and faith-filled environment for every camper.

## A UNIQUE SUMMER CAMP EXPERIENCE

LCBI has been running summer camps for five years now. We have seen the camps grow from two sports and 50 athletes the first year to multiple activities and 202 campers last year, and now we cannot wait to see what the summer of 2025 has in store! ​

​

Throughout your camp, you will learn technical and tactical skills relating to your activity. Off the court, you will be taught about Strength & Conditioning, Nutrition, Mental Toughness in Sport, Recovery Sessions, Hand Eye Coordination Sessions, and hear from our high performance coaches about their road to success, and how their faith played a large part in that.

​

Outside of your sport/activity, you will participate in group activities such as beach volleyball, camp wide games, campfires, chapel, and hanging out in our Student Centre!

​

LCBI Summer Camps are held at LCBI High School - A Christian Boarding School in Outlook, Saskatchewan. Campers will stay overnight in the dorms and enjoy access to the entire campus featuring a gymnasium, football field, beach volleyball court, courtyard, weight room, chapel, student center, family room, and cafeteria. The LCBI kitchen staff will provide nutritious meals to fuel you throughout your training sessions. Along with expert coaches, you will have camp counsellors by your side the entire time to provide support in the dorms and plan the fun activities!

​

LCBI Summer Overnight Camps include a faith-based component as part of the camp experience. Coaches will share how their personal faith has impacted their journey to success, and in the evenings, guest speakers may also discuss the role of faith in their lives. During the Chapel and Campfire sessions, camp counselors and campers will participate in action-based fun worship songs. In the evenings, camp counselors will meet with their dorm groups to reflect on the day and engage in a brief devotional discussion.

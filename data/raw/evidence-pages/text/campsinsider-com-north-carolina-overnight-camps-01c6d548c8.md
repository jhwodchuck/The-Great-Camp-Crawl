# Best Overnight Summer Camps In North Carolina

There’s nothing like attending a summer camp in North Carolina. With scenic forests like the Pisgah National Forests and beautiful lakes, your child has a lot to explore when they attend a sleepaway camp in North Carolina. The experience of camp is highly beneficial for kids and a week or more away from home can mean new friends, fun activities, and learning about the natural world. This state is the perfect place for your children to enjoy the outdoors.

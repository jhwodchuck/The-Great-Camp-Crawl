# Experience the Safe and Empowering Environment of Camp Kupugani

At Camp Kupugani, nestled near Chicago, Illinois, we prioritize the safety and well-being of our campers above all. Our multicultural summer camp ensures a secure setting for children ages 7-15 for both domestic and [international campers](https://campkupugani.com/international-campers/). Our expertly trained staff, selected for their skills in fostering a safe and supportive atmosphere, are dedicated to guiding campers through an enriching journey of self-discovery and growth.

We offer specialized two-week sessions exclusively for [girls](https://campkupugani.com/girls-camp/) and [blended](https://campkupugani.com/blended-camp/) (all genders). Sessions last either two or four weeks, promoting meaningful interactions among all campers. At Camp Kupugani, your child will not only embrace diverse friendships spanning a wide range of cultural backgrounds—including Hispanic/Latine, White/Caucasian, Black/African American, Asian, Indian, Hawaiian, and more—but also engage in a plethora of outdoor activities designed to foster life skills and personal growth. Under the vigilant guidance of our experienced team, campers enjoy an adventurous outdoor environment where safety is seamlessly integrated into the fun and learning.

In addition to our girls and blended summer camp sessions we also offer:

- Teen Leadership Programs
- [Mother Daughter Weekend](https://campkupugani.com/mother-daughter-weekend/) (for girls and moms)
- [Parent Child Weekend](https://campkupugani.com/parent-child-weekend/) (for kids and a parent of any gender)
- Team Building Programs
- [Rentals](https://campkupugani.com/camp-rental/rent-camp-white-eagle/)
- [Retreats & Event Space](https://campkupugani.com/camp-rental/special-events-retreats-rental/)
- Outdoor Education

Since 1951, Kupugani’s home, Camp White Eagle, has been a sanctuary where children from all races and backgrounds are embraced. This enduring legacy of diversity and inclusion underpins every aspect of Camp Kupugani, offering a safe and nurturing summer camp environment that fosters self-discovery and cultivates a deep appreciation for the natural world.

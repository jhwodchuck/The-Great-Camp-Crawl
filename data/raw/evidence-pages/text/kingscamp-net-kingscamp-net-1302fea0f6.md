# Summer Camp

Reaching The Next Generation

Come join us this summer and experience the adventure and beauty of Kingdom living.

Our weeks for King’s Camp 2026 are:

Grades 3-4 Session 1: June 1 - June 6

Grades 5-6 Session 2: June 8 - June 13

Grades 7-8 Session 3: June 14 - June 20

Grades 9-12 Session 4: June 21 - June 27

​

Remember that as you are registering, the grade refers to the grade your camper will go into in for the Fall of 2024, the next school grade after your child’s current school grade. For example, if your child is currently in sixth grade, you will register your child in Session 3.

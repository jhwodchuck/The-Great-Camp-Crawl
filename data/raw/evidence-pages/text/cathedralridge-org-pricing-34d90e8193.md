### Price Calculator Below

This pricing calculator is designed to provide an estimated cost for standard priced camps and retreats. It will also help provide an estimate if you are planning to host your own retreat. Please fill out the information below and scroll down to see the estimate.

Note: Pride, Multicultural, and Latino Family Camps and Abound Retreats are covered under a generous grant making them $75 a person for the whole retreat.

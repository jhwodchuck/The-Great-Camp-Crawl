# Reservation

To make a reservation request at Camp Mokulēʻia, please fill out the form with the information that pertains to your visit. After submitting the form, we will contact you within two to three business days to finalize your reservation. We look forward to talking with you in order to compete your reservation.

We offer daily tours upon appointment and are happy to assist you in planning your next event at Camp Mokulēʻia.

​Call us at [(808) 637-6241](tel:(808) 637-6241)  to schedule your next tour, or to check on dates for your next event.

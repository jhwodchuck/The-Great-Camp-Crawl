# Tuition & Fees for Summer Courses

### Undergraduate Rates

Undergraduate part-time summer tuition is charged by the credit hour. [View current rates](https://studentaccounts.gwu.edu/undergraduate-tuition) for undergraduates.

### Graduate Rates

Graduate summer tuition rates are charged by the credit hour and vary by program and location. [Rates are listed by school](https://studentaccounts.gwu.edu/graduate-tuition).

### Visiting Student Rates

Visiting students are charged [non-degree tuition rates](https://studentaccounts.gwu.edu/non-degree-tuition-rates). Rates are billed by credit hour.

### Additional Fees

An additional Student Association Fee will be assessed. Some exceptions may apply for specific programs.

Additional program fees may apply for [Summer Institutes](https://columbian.gwu.edu/summer-institutes).

### Other Summer Programs

Rates vary by program, so be sure to review the tuition and fees for the program you’re interested in:

- [International Summer for visiting international students](/international-summer-tuition-fees)
- Pre-College Program for high school students

  - [College Intensive (credit)](/college-intensive-tuition-fees)
  - [Summer Immersion (noncredit)](/summer-immersion-tuition-fees)
- [Corcoran Arts Continuing Education non-credit programs](https://corcoran.gwu.edu/continuing-education)

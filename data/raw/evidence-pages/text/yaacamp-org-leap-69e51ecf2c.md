# Little Explorers Adventure Preschool in Bozeman, Montana

Little Explorers Adventure Preschool (LEAP) is a nature-based Christian preschool in Bozeman, Montana, serving children ages 3–5 in the heart of the Gallatin Valley.

At LEAP, kids don’t just learn inside four walls—they explore, create, and grow through hands-on outdoor learning, meaningful relationships, and faith-centered teaching.

Whether it’s discovering the changing seasons, building friendships, or preparing for kindergarten, our goal is simple: help every child thrive—academically, socially, and spiritually.​

​

​

​

## ​A Nature-Based Preschool in the Gallatin Valley

Located at Yellowstone Alliance Adventures, our preschool offers a one-of-a-kind setting where children experience daily outdoor learning in Bozeman’s beautiful natural landscape.

From forest trails to open fields, our campus becomes the classroom—giving children space to move, imagine, and engage with the world around them.

Outdoor learning helps children:

- Build confidence and independence
- Strengthen problem-solving skills
- Develop a lifelong love for creation

​

At LEAP, nature isn’t a break from learning—it’s where learning comes to life.

## Mission

To provide a high-quality early childhood learning program rooted in Biblical principles that
meets the developmental needs of preschool-aged children while inspiring a lifelong love for God, learning, and nature. Our curriculum combines traditional kindergarten readiness with nature-based exploration, helping children develop a deep appreciation for the world around them and the Creator who made it.

## Preschool for Ages 3–5 Focused on Kindergarten Readiness

Our program is intentionally designed to prepare children for what’s next.

Through a balance of structured learning and play-based exploration, students develop the skills they need for kindergarten readiness and lifelong learning.

We focus on:

- Early literacy and language development
- Foundational math skills
- Social-emotional growth
- Listening, routines, and classroom confidence

Every child learns differently—and we create space for each one to grow at their own pace.

## A Safe, Supportive, and Joy-Filled Environment

We know choosing a preschool is a big decision—and the people guiding your child matter deeply.

At LEAP, our dedicated team is made up of passionate educators and leaders who are committed to nurturing both the hearts and minds of young explorers. With a shared love for children, nature, and Jesus, we create a safe, engaging preschool environment in Bozeman where every child is truly known.

Together, we bring years of experience in early childhood education, faith formation, and outdoor learning—united by a desire to reflect Christ’s character in all we do.

Because of this, children at LEAP feel:

- Safe
- Known
- Encouraged

We don’t take lightly the trust families place in us. Our team is passionate about building meaningful relationships—not just with students, but with parents as well.

At LEAP, your child isn’t just another number—they are deeply valued.

LEAP is a program—not childcare—and tuition reflects the value of consistent, structured, and intentional learning experiences for your child. Because our program space, staffing, and materials are planned around enrolled students, tuition is charged on a program basis rather than by attendance.

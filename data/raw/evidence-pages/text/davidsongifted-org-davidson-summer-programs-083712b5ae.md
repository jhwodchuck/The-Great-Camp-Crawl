# Gifted Summer Programs

## Davidson Summer Programs

Academically challenging residential summer camps designed exclusively for
Davidson Young Scholars ages 8-13, hosted on the University of Nevada, Reno Campus!

## DSP Sessions

[How To Apply](https://www.davidsongifted.org/gifted-programs/summer-opportunities/davidson-summer-programs/dsp-info/)

Ages: 8-11
Academics: Exploration-Style Workshops

Dates: June 21-25
Ages: 8-11
Academics: Exploration-Style Workshops

### Session Three

Ages: 11-13

### Session Four

Ages: 11-13

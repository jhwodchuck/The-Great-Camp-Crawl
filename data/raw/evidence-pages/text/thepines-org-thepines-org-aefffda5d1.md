### Intentionally, joyfully Catholic

Our mission is to empower every camper and staff member to **encounter God** and to live his or her faith with **joy and confidence** through a **distinctly Catholic camp ministry** built on **faith formation and fellowship** set within **the beauty of God’s creation.**

Our Sacramental life includes the frequent celebration of the Mass, the opportunity to visit the Blessed Sacrament in our chapel, and priests who serve as chaplains to offer the Sacraments and fellowship. Our staff is enthusiastic and passionate about sharing the joy of the faith with our campers.

Jesus is not just in the chapel at The Pines — He is at the top of the zipline, down every trail, in every conversation, and outside through the glory of Creation!

# What We Do

Empowering rural Alaskan students to succeed in higher education since 1983.

## Rural Alaska Honors Institute

RAHI is a six-week summer program at the University of Alaska Fairbanks that helps
high school students get a head start on college.

With RAHI, students live on campus, take real college courses, and build the confidence
and skills they need to succeed.

RAHI is offered at no cost to students and their families.

[Learn More](/rahi/about/index.php)

Free Credits

Dive into college courses, taught just for you—earn up to 10 credits.

Lifelong Friendships

Meet peers who share your interests and let your true self shine—friends for life.

College Ready

Gain confidence navigating campus, classes, and college life—succeed anywhere.

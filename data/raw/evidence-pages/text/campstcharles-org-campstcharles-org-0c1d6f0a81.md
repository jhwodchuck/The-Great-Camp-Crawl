Camp St. Charles is a catholic, overnight summer camp and retreat center located in Southern Maryland. Our beautiful water front location is an ideal setting for our summer camp program and weekend events. Camp St. Charles was established in 1952 as a boys' camp and now offers summer adventures to both boys and girls ages 6 to 13 years old. Our rustic cabins and wide range of activities provide the setting for personal growth, lasting memories and life long friendships. Our daily schedule includes scheduled and free time activities, so that campers may spend additional time at their favorite events. Our unplugged program gets campers outdoors and engaged with each other. Campers enjoy swimming, canoeing, escape room, stand up paddleboarding, camping, speedboat rides, tubing, ceramics, a variety of team sports, archery, riflery, camp fires, environmental education, high and low ropes and more under the careful supervision of our caring counselors and staff.

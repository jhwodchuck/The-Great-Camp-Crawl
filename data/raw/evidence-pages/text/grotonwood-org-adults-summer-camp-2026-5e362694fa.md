# **Summer camp for**

# **ADULTS with disabilities**

Grotonwood offers three-day, one-week and two-week camping sessions for adults with mild to moderate physical, cognitive and developmental disabilities. Each session is designed to meet the specific needs of our campers; through appropriate staff to camper ratios, adaptive recreational camp activities, appropriate pacing, built-in transition times, medical oversight, and the option for off-campus trips (restrictions apply).

## FIRST TIME CAMPERS MUST COMPLETE AN IN-PERSON ASSESSMENT AND TOUR the facilities PRIOR TO REGISTERING FOR CAMP.

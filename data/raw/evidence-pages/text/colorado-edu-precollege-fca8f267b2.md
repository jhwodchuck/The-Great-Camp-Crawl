# Homepage

**Discover Your Path**

The **Office of Precollege Outreach and Engagement,** which is part of the Division of Enrollment Management, partners with communities across Colorado and beyond to provide first-generation and low-income students and their families with comprehensive college prep and social development programs.

These programs help support students academically and personally while in middle and high school and prepare them to transition successfully to postsecondary opportunities.

Explore our site to learn more about these programs, their impact and how to connect.

A Lifetime of Impact

"The impact this program had on me has rippled through the very fabric of who I am by allowing me to not only realize my own educational goals, but instilling the confidence of a leader [who can] coach others into realizing and surpassing their own expectations."

*– Luis Morales, CU Boulder Class of 2013
 Roaring Fork Precollegiate participant*

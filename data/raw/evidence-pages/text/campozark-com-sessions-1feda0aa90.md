## THREE WEEK SESSIONS

Campers may enroll in three or more consecutive weeks of camping. However, Camp Ozark no longer makes accommodations for residency between sessions. Camp families must check-out their campers at the close of a session. Campers will be able to check back in the next Opening Day. Camp Ozark will coordinate luggage and cabin changes between sessions.

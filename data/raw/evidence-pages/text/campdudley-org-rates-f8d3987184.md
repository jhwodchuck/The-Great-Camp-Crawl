#### [2026 Tiered Tuition](#1574366938665-8a1558e0-d560)

**One Session****Full Season****Tier 1**:    $7,650              $13,500

**Tier 2**:    $6,650              $11,750

**Tier 3**:    $5,650

**Tier 4**:    $4,650

**Tier 5**:    $3,650

**Tier 6**:    Tuition Assistance Program

At Dudley and Kiniya, we are proud of our mission, and we work to make the camp experience available to as many families as possible, which is why the Board of Trustees has prioritized continuing to offer our tiered pricing program for 2026.

We recognize that camp is a significant investment. We also know that it is of tremendous value to the development of every child. The tuition a family can pay for an equally valuable experience varies drastically based on their financial position.

The tuition tier selected by each family is expected to reflect the family’s income level and asset base. Our goal is that our camps will be populated by campers drawn from a wide range of socio-economic backgrounds.

**Your tier selection is confidential, and regardless of your selection, the experience and program for every camper are the same.** We offer Tiers 2, 3, 4, 5, & 6 at a subsidized rate due to the generosity of the Dudley & Kiniya family and their support of [The Annual Fund](/bl-support-our-annual-fund/).

In 2025, through the generosity of our camp family, we subsidized nearly $1.9 million of camper tuition through our tiered pricing and tuition assistance program.

---

**Tier 1:** $7,650: This tuition most accurately reflects the true cost of camp for each camper.

---

**Tier 2:** $6,650: This tuition tier is in line with our traditional tuition and reflects a slightly subsidized rate for each camper in which endowment funds are used to off-set some costs.

---

**Tier 3:** $5,650: This tuition tier reflects a more heavily subsidized rate for each camper.

---

**Tier 4:** $4,650: This tuition tier reflects a deeply discounted rate for each camper and is heavily funded through the Annual Fund, which is made possible through the generosity of our alumni and parents.

---

**Tier 5:** $3,650: At this tier, our Annual Fund covers over 50% of the camp cost. We hope this tier allows families to forego our traditional tuition assistance program.

---

**Tier 6:** Reflects our traditional tuition assistance program. For this tuition option, we ask families to tell us how much they can afford and then complete a financial aid application. We then do our best to award aid based on financial need.

#### How Much Should I Pay?

In Tiers 1-5, we ask that you pay the rate that best suits your family’s financial picture while recognizing the investment you are making in your child and the Dudley & Kiniya experience.

In Tier 6, we’ll ask how much you can contribute toward tuition and assess your need following the aid application. We expect every family to invest in their camper experience.

**NOLS pricing – $7,650 (Tier #1 Only)**

Flights are included in NOLS. Lower tiers are NOT available for NOLS. **Financial Payment Plans are available.**Please email [Jesse@campdudley.org](mailto:Jesse@campdudley.org) to set up a plan.

#### [Tuition Assistance Program](#1576252535844-3fdc9036-7f77)

We are extremely proud of our Tuition Assistance Program, which allows us to support families through a robust financial aid program. In 2025, approximately 19% of the campers at both Camps participated in our Tuition Assistance Program. This is made possible through the generous support of [The Annual Fund](/bl-support-our-annual-fund/).

**Tuition Assistance Application**

When you complete the Dudley & Kiniya Enrollment Application and choose Tier 6, you will be directed to our Financial Aid Form. This link will be created directly from your specific application and is individualized to your camper. In order to return to this application, you will need to retain the email received when your Camp application was started.

Please contact our office with any questions, 518-962-4720.

#### [Flexible Payment Options](#1602869919951-c7ebd468-35e5)

We offer flexible payment options to spread payments out over a period of time that is manageable for your budget. You can contact our Chief Financial Officer, [Jesse Smith](mailto:jesse@campdudley.org), for more information on setting up a payment plan that works for you and your family.

#### [Deposit & Payment](#1574367092255-65f8842b-17c4)

### Deposit

We ask that families submit a non-refundable $400 deposit (or a reduced amount that works for them) when they complete the enrollment application. This amount is applied toward the final tuition balance.

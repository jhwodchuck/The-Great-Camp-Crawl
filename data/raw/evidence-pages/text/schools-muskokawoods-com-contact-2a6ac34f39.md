# Contact Muskoka Woods

### We can hardly wait to book your overnight trip.

## Booking Form

### You can expect your overnight school trip to Muskoka Woods to include:



#### Safety

Safety is one of your top values and has been ours for over 43 years of youth development. Your students will be safe thanks to Muskoka Woods prioritizing safety training for our staff and time tested policies and procedures while on site including meeting, and in many cases exceeding, all TSSA, OCA, and OPASSE/OPHEA guidelines.



#### Connected Learning

Connect your curriculum to best-in-class Leadership Experiences run and facilitated by Muskoka Woods Staff. You and your group will benefit in many ways including having a built-in piece of your program already taken care of (meaning one less thing for you to worry about) and a model of learning that is a proven to help participants both remember the learning and apply it in the future.



#### Stay overnight!

You don’t have to worry if your entire group will be able to come at the same time. We have space and accommodations for everyone in modern and clean facilities built for groups up to 700.

### What teachers and students experience after a Muskoka Woods overnight school retreat

##### Students

- Understand they are capable of so much more than they thought before the overnight school trip
- Are more engaged in class and become more involved in school life
- Have increased trust for their teachers, seeing them as having valuable input for their life beyond academic learning

##### Teachers

- You’re able to connect much easier with your students
- The classroom becomes more fun because of your shared life experience
- You are able to share the heavy lifting with the Muskoka Woods team by leaning on our experienced staff

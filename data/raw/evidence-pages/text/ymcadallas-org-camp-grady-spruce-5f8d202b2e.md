# Camp Grady Spruce

Located on scenic Possum Kingdom Lake in Graford, Texas, Camp Grady Spruce is an ACA accredited co-ed overnight camp providing campers of all ages with opportunities to participate in exceptional outdoor experiences while learning about the surrounding natural landscape.

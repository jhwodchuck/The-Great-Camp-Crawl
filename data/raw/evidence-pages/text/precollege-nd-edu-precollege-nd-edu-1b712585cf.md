## Do Something Extraordinary

Notre Dame Pre-College delivers a true college experience on campus, abroad, and virtually for exceptional high school students! Students engage with dynamic Notre Dame faculty in exciting and challenging college level classes, grow in leadership through service, and connect with like-minded students from around the world.

## Programs

- ![Leadership Seminars Web](/assets/481273/600x400/leadership_seminars_web.jpg)

  ## [Leadership Seminars](/leadership-seminars/)

  Live and learn on campus while tackling major global issues with like-minded peers from around the globe. Tuition and housing are paid for by the University in this competitive academic program.
- ![Summer Scholars Web](/assets/481271/600x400/summer_scholars_web.jpg)

  ## [Summer Scholars](/summer-scholars/)

  Explore a college-level course, live on campus, and experience the unique Notre Dame community!
- ![Study Abroad South Africa](/assets/407013/600x400/img_0281sa.jpg)

  ## [Study Abroad](/study-abroad/)

  Travel abroad to Italy, Ireland, South Africa, or London for a taste of the global Notre Dame community.
- ![Summer Scholars Online laptop](/assets/406991/600x400/laptop_stickers_19a.jpg)

  ## [Pre-College Online Programs](https://precollegeonline.nd.edu/)

  Unlock your potential with the opportunity to participate in an enriching online course, all taught by Notre Dame faculty.
- ![Adam Navigation Photo](/assets/639670/600x400/adam_navigation.jpg)

  ## [ADAM: ND Jerusalem](https://precollege.nd.edu/adam-gap-year-program/)

  ADAM is a competitive pre-college gap year program in Jerusalem offered by the University of Notre Dame, designed to nurture confident, capable, and community-minded Jerusalem students for success in higher education.

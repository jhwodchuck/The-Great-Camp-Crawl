Mountain Camp is a [**co-ed summer overnight camp**](/about/ "Learn more about Mountain Camp, a premier sleepaway camp in Northern California") for campers entering 2nd – 12th grades. One of the premier summer camps in California, [we are located **near Lake Tahoe** on the beautiful shore of **Ice House Lake in the Eldorado National Forest**](/about/map-to-camp-directions/ "Get directions to Mountain Camp near Lake Tahoe in California") in the High Sierras.

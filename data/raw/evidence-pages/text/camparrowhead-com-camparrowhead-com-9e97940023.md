## Small, Hands-On Boys Sleepaway Camp in the Mountains of Western North Carolina

### Outdoor Adventure & Maker Camp

Nestled in the wildernesss of the Blue Ridge Mountains of Western North Carolina, Camp Arrowhead for Boys is a small overnight summer camp where boys trade screens for real-world skills, outdoor adventure, and lasting friendships. Our Hendersonville, NC mountain summer camp blends traditional camp adventures with maker camp experiences — from blacksmithing and woodworking to bushcraft and archery — campers have the chance to create with their hands and explore the great outdoors.

Every day at Arrowhead is built around growth, confidence, and the unforgettable experiences you can only have in the mountains of WNC. We’re a small, supportive community on a mountain that’s full of fun and adventure.

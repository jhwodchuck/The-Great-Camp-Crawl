### Summer Camp Tuition Includes

At Circle F, campers dive into **[over 40 exciting activities](/dude-ranch-summer-camp-activities)**, a **[well-rounded program](/florida-summer-camp/program-daily-schedule)** and **[outstanding facilities](/about-circle-f/the-ranch)** built for comfort and adventure. From **[hearty and nutritious meals](/about-circle-f/farm-to-table)** and weekly **wash-and-fold** laundry service to **sweet treats** from Cantina and Store, we handle the details-so kids can focus on making memories that last a liftime!

Save on our summer camp rates when you enroll **siblings**, or when you make **referrals**! Discover **[summer camp credits](#block-id-80546)**.

### Optional Add-Ons

##### [Paintball](/dude-ranch-summer-camp-activities/paintball-extreme-adventures) ($85/week)

Have a blast playing paintball five days a week, unless there is a special activity planned.

##### [Excursion](/florida-summer-camp/parent-info/summer-camp-faqs/#block-id-80899) ($285 per trip)

Have fun with summer camp friends on camp theme park excursions. These trips include transportation in a motor coach bus, meals, admission, and counselor supervision for the day.

##### Airport Transportation ($180/each way)

Transportation is offered to and from the Orlando and Tampa airports.

#### Payment Policies

A non-refundable deposit of $750.00 per session per camper is required at the time of registration.

All remaining tuition and optional add-on fees are due in full by May 1.

### AwesomeMath Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does AwesomeMath offer?

AwesomeMath provides comprehensive advanced mathematics and physics  training through multiple flagship programs designed to nurture mathematical talent at every level:

- **AwesomeMath Summer Program (AMSP)** – An intensive three-week online program featuring daily lectures and problem-solving sessions in Algebra, Combinatorics, Geometry, and Number Theory. This premier advanced mathematics training experience offers 90-minute lectures followed by 60-minute collaborative problem-solving sessions, plus weekly assessments and office hours. [Learn more about the Summer Program](https://www.awesomemath.org/summer-program/overview/program-information/)
- **AwesomeMath Academy** – Fall and Spring  weekend classes offering **advanced mathematics training** across four progressive difficulty levels, from beginner (MATHCOUNTS/AMC 8 prep) to advanced (USAMO preparation). Physics competition preparation from F = ma through USAPhO. No application required – students choose classes that match their current skill level. [Explore Academy courses](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/online-courses/)
- **College Prep Courses (NEW)** – Self-paced Abstract Algebra and Analysis courses offering university-level **advanced mathematics training** for motivated high school students seeking to explore higher mathematics. [Explore College Prep Courses](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/online-courses/)
- **Mathematical Reflections** – A free online journal featuring challenging problems and research articles contributed by mathematicians worldwide. [Browse the journal](https://www.awesomemath.org/mathematical-reflections/)
- **Purple Comet*****!*** **Math Meet** – A free international team-based online competition for middle and high school students, running since 2003. [Learn about Purple Comet!](https://purplecomet.org/)
- **AwesomeMath Bookstore** – A curated collection of problem-solving books authored by Dr. Andreescu and other renowned mathematicians, supporting independent **advanced mathematics training** at home. [Visit the Bookstore](https://www.awesomemath.org/shop/)

Who can participate in AwesomeMath programs?Toggle 2

AwesomeMath training programs welcome intellectually curious learners from around the world:

- **Age Range:** Primarily middle and high school students ages 12-18, though exceptional younger students demonstrating remarkable mathematical and personal maturity may be considered.

What is Dr. Titu Andreescu’s background?

[Dr. Titu Andreescu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Titu_Andreescu), founded AwesomeMath in 2006 and is one of the most influential figures in competitive mathematics education worldwide. His credentials include:

- - **Head Coach, USA International Mathematical Olympiad Team (1995-2002) –** Led Team USA to historic success, including the legendary 1994 “perfect team” where all six American students achieved perfect scores – a unique achievement in the 45+ year history of the IMO.
  - **Director, Mathematical Olympiad Summer Program (MOSP) (1995-2002) –** Oversaw the nation’s elite training program for top mathematical talent.
  - **Director, MAA American Mathematics Competitions (1998-2003) –** Shaped the AMC 8, AMC 10, AMC 12, and AIME competitions that serve as the pathway to Olympiad-level mathematics.

- **Chairman, USA Mathematical Olympiad (USAMO) (1996-2004)**

- **IMO Advisory Board Member (elected 2002)** – Serves on the governing body of the world’s most prestigious mathematics competition.
- **Prolific Author –** Authored or co-authored over 60 mathematics books focused on problem-solving techniques and Olympiad preparation, published by Birkhäuser, **Springer and XYZ Press**
- **Academic Position** – Associate Professor of Mathematics at the University of Texas at Dallas, specializing in Diophantine Analysis.

How do I get started with AwesomeMath?

Beginning your advanced mathematics training journey with AwesomeMath is straightforward:

**For the Academy (Fall and Spring Semester  Program):**

- No application required – simply assess your readiness level using the [Readiness Guide](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/readiness/)
- Browse the [Course Catalog](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/online-courses/) to find courses matching your level
- [Enroll](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/enrollment/) directly through the website – 12-week semesters
- Academy classes meet once a week, offering flexible advanced mathematics training that fits your schedule

**For the Summer Program:**

- Review the [Readiness Guide and Sample Test](https://www.awesomemath.org/summer-program/application/readiness/) to ensure you’re prepared
- Create a student account and complete the online application form (opens December 15th for Summer 2026)
- Submit your admission test solutions and recommendation letter
- Apply early for discounted tuition rates and best course selection. [View application details](https://www.awesomemath.org/summer-program/application/how-to-apply/)

*Not quite ready for the Summer Program?* The Academy provides an excellent pathway to build your skills through structured mathematics training before applying. Self-study resources are also available through our online bookstore.

Are programs available year-round?

Yes! AwesomeMath offers continuous opportunities for advanced mathematics training throughout the entire year:

- **AwesomeMath Academy** – Operates on a Fall/Spring semester schedule with 12-week courses meeting once a week . Fall semester begins in early September, Spring semester in January. [View the weekly schedule](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/schedule/)
- **AwesomeMath Summer Program** – Intensive three-week sessions offered June through August. Multiple session options allow students to choose the timing that works best for their schedule while receiving focused advanced mathematics training.
- **College Prep Courses** – Self-paced independent study format with rolling enrollment. Students have 5 months from enrollment to complete coursework, allowing flexible advanced mathematics training year-round.
- **Mathematical Reflections Journal** – Published bimonthly with new challenging problems and solutions, providing continuous problem-solving practice.
- **AwesomeMath Bookstore** – Books available year-round for independent study and supplementary advanced mathematics training. [Browse competition prep books](https://www.awesomemath.org/shop/)

What topics are covered in AwesomeMath courses?

AwesomeMath advanced mathematics training covers the four pillars of competition mathematics, with depth increasing as students progress

- **Algebra** – Polynomials, sequences and series, functional equations, inequalities, complex numbers, and advanced algebraic manipulation techniques.
- **Combinatorics** – Counting principles, probability, graph theory, game theory, extremal combinatorics, and algorithmic thinking.
- **Geometry** – Euclidean geometry, coordinate geometry, trigonometry,, transformations, and advanced techniques. .
- **Number Theory –** Divisibility, modular arithmetic, Diophantine equations, prime factorization, and classical theorems essential for competition success.

 Academy courses  are designed to be taken for a minimum of two semesters to fully cover the material necessary for progression to the next level of advanced mathematics training. [View detailed course curricula](https://www.awesomemath.org/stem-academy/online-courses/)

How are AwesomeMath classes structured?

AwesomeMath has refined its approach to advanced mathematics training to maximize learning effectiveness:

Summer Program Format (Per Course):

- Three consecutive weeks of daily instruction (Monday-Friday)
- 90-minute live lecture introducing concepts through elegant problems
- 60-minute collaborative problem-solving session with peer interaction
- Daily homework with personalized feedback from instructors and TAs
- Weekly assessment tests on Saturdays (not proctored)
- Office hours twice weekly for additional support

Academy Format (Per Course):

- 12 weeks per semester, meeting once a week
- 90-minute live sessions for math courses combining lecture and problem-solving
- 60-minute live Q&A sessions for physics courses
- Small class sizes (maximum 25 students) for personalized attention
- 2-3 homework problems per week with instructor feedback
- All lectures recorded for review

Both programs emphasize active learning – students don’t just watch, they engage with problems during class, building the skills essential for success in competition mathematics and physics through hands-on advanced training.

Can I participate from outside the United States?

Absolutely! AwesomeMath advanced mathematics and physics  training programs are fully virtual and welcome students from around the world. Our global community includes participants from all over the world, creating a rich international learning environment. The online format eliminates travel requirements while maintaining the high-quality, live instruction that makes AwesomeMath effective. Class times are scheduled with international students in mind, and all lectures are recorded for those who cannot attend live sessions due to time zone differences.

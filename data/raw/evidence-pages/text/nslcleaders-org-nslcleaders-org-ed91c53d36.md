## Why choose NSLC?

### Create worldwide connections

Meet students from all 50 states and over 110 countries, building a network of like-minded new friends who share your aspirations and interests.

### Improve as a leader

Gain confidence and independence through leadership workshops that emphasize the positive impact you’re capable of creating on the world around you.

### Make informed decisions

Spending time on a college campus, diving into a potential career, and exploring new city surroundings enable you to make educated decisions for your future.

### Feel ready for college

Tackling advanced course material, adapting to a new environment, and living more independently prepares you - and your family - for the transition from home to university.

## Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Accessibility

NSLC believes that all students have the right to an education, and that a diverse learning environment is an education in itself. We strive to ensure that students of all races, genders, backgrounds, and abilities have equal access to our programming.

Frequently Asked Questions

#####

The National Student Leadership Conference provides an immersive pre-college experience that prepares students for life beyond middle and high school, allowing them to study in university classrooms, learn to navigate a campus, and experience living with roommates. Students are able to explore a career concentration they are interested in before going into college and declaring a major. Each NSLC program also contains a leadership curriculum designed to teach skills that can apply to all areas of life.

#####

We encourage all 6th-12th grade students who demonstrate academic excellence and leadership ability to apply to our summer programs.

For high school programs, students must be between the ages of 14-18 and have completed at least one year of high school. Students may attend NSLC through the summer following high school graduation.

For middle school programs, students must be at least 11 years of age and currently enrolled in 6th, 7th, or 8th grade.

#####

[Click here](https://portal.nslcleaders.org/register) to get started! NSLC applications are completed online and require a deposit with a credit or debit card. If you would like to place your deposit with a check, please email [info@nslcleaders.org](mailto:info@nslcleaders.org).

#####

While we have suggested seasonal deadlines, NSLC will continue to accept applications if space is available in a program or on a program’s waitlist. The online enrollment application will display all current availability.

#####

Students are selected for NSLC in a variety of ways. Teachers, counselors, and alumni nominate students from across the United States and around the world each year. Students may also be invited based on individual results of standardized testing or selected from one of several national talent identification surveys.

# Summer Camp Programs

## Summer Camp﻿

Located on the beautiful North Shore of Oʻahu, Camp Mokulēʻia has been creating unforgettable summer camp experiences for children and youth since 1947. When families choose to enroll their child at Camp Mokulēʻia, they are doing something truly extraordinary—placing their trust in us to care for the most precious part of their lives. We do not take that responsibility lightly. Camper safety, well-being, and emotional care are always our highest priorities, and our dedicated counselors and staff are deeply committed to creating a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for every child.

Through experiencing the beauty of God’s creation and living in a genuine Christian community, campers are invited into moments of growth, reflection, and spiritual transformation—always guided with care, encouragement, and respect. Camp Mokulēʻia is a ministry of the Episcopal Diocese of Hawaiʻi, and we warmly welcome campers of all faith backgrounds, or none at all.

During their time at residential camp, campers build meaningful friendships, grow in confidence by trying new things, and discover more about themselves and the world around them—all while having an incredible amount of fun. Each day is filled with laughter, adventure, and memory-making through age-appropriate activities such as swimming, paddle boarding, kayaking, arts and crafts, group games, team-building challenges, the high ropes course, and hiking. Campers leave with new skills, strong friendships, and memories that will last a lifetime.

Camp Mokule‘ia is deeply committed to the safety and well-being of every camper and guest. We take a proactive approach by partnering with experts in abuse prevention and maintaining thorough staff screening, training, and supervision practices. In addition, our team receives ongoing training in weather preparedness, medical response, and activity safety. Our goal is to provide an environment where parents can feel confident and at peace entrusting their child to our care for a week of Summer Camp. ﻿

## Facility Rental

Howard H. Cherry Scout Reservation is open year-round for Scouting units and other organizations. We offer nonprofit pricing for registered 501(c)(3) organizations.

**New facility and programming rates apply for new reservations made for after July 1, 2026. If would like to schedule programming at Camp Wakonda, please fill out the [Wakonda Facility Rental Application](http://hawkeyescouting.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Wakonda-Rental-Application-Fillable.pdf) and submit it to Sarah Dawson at [Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org](mailto:Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org). Inquiries about packaging may also be directed to
[Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org](mailto:Sarah.Dawson@Scouting.org).**

If you have any questions regarding camp grounds, please contact Josh Yoder at <joshua.yoder@scouting.org>.

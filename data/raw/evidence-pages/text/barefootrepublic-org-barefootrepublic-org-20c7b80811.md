###### Our Camp is UNLIKE ANY OTHER! At Barefoot, we strive to facilitate Christ-centered relationships between individuals from diverse racial, cultural, and socio-economic backgrounds. We believe that camp is special, and that every child should be able to have shared experiences, like camp, to help them grow into who God created them to be. At Barefoot Republic, we offer a variety of opportunities for children and families to experience the Barefoot difference.

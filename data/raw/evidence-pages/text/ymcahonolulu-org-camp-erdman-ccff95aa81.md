# Camp Erdman

## Smiles are Bigger Here

YMCA camp is a great way to unplug from the every day, including electronics, and connect with yourself, your fellow campers, and nature. You will have opportunities to explore our ‘aina (land) from the mountains to the ocean, find new talents, try new activities, make lasting memories, and experience the Hawaiian culture and values connected to this special place on our island.

## Camp Erdman's 100th Anniversary

Camp H.R. Erdman is turning 100! Help us celebrate by sharing your favorite camp memories. From friendships to life lessons, your stories keep the spirit of camp alive. Submit your story and be part of our legacy!

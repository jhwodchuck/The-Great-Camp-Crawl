# **Point O'Pines Camp for Girls**

## Treasured Memories to Last a Lifetime

Come with us on a journey to a very special summer camp where lifelong friendships are born, confidence is discovered, skills are honed, and fun defines every day. At Point O’Pines Camp for Girls, traditions come alive, dreams are realized, and girls of all ages come together to create lifelong memories. Point O’Pines is nestled in the spectacular Adirondack Mountains on a peninsula surrounded by the crystal-clear waters of beautiful Brant Lake.

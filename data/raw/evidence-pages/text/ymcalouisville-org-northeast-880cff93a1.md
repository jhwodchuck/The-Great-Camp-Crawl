### How It Works

Our state-licensed program follows the Creative Curriculum, emphasizing child-led, teacher-guided exploration. Learning happens through play, problem-solving, and imagination—tailored to each child’s interests and developmental level.

Children will:

- Build foundational skills in language, literacy, and math
- Engage in art, STEM, imaginative play, and music
- Strengthen independence, empathy, and collaboration
- Learn through hands-on, nature-based, and group activities

### Enrichment Opportunities

**Fitness Program**
Our 3–5-year-olds participate in structured physical activity led by our Youth Sports team—building coordination, movement skills, and teamwork.

**Swim Lessons**
Children ages 2+ receive swim lessons led by certified instructors, both in summer at Calypso Cove and year-round in our indoor pool—at no additional cost.

**Music Education**
Weekly music classes are provided by University of Louisville students, introducing melody, rhythm, and hands-on exploration of musical concepts.

**Nutrition & Mealtimes**
Children receive a healthy breakfast, lunch, and snack daily, with family-style dining that promotes independence and good habits. Formula, baby food, and accommodations for younger infants are also provided.

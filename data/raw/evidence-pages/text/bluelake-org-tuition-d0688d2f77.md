# Tuition and Fees

The basic fee per session includes instruction, supervision, housing, and meals. Campers are admitted without charge to all camp events. Tuition differs slightly by major, based upon the specific costs associated with each program, such as student/teacher ratios, materials, equipment, and services. There is an additional fee to add a minor for Central Camp students. Other expenses include uniform costs and spending money for incidentals. Blue Lake strives to make camp affordable and accessible for our students, and **83% of our campers receive tuition assistance** through Blue Lake’s [Scholarship Program](https://bluelake.org/summer-camp/scholarships/).

| --- | --- |

**Early Arrival**: **$110**  Blue Lake strongly discourages campers from arriving a day early for their camp session. If deemed necessary due to unavoidable circumstances, students may not arrive earlier than 5:00pm Tuesday before Session 1, or 9:00am Tuesday before sessions 2, 3, and 4. The camp store and uniforms will not be available until Opening Wednesday. The early arrival fee covers all meals, supervision, and activities for the day or any portion thereof. To register for an early arrival, visit [My Account](https://bluelake.org/ultracamp/). For details, contact Admissions at 800-221-3796 x209 or [admissions@bluelake.org](mailto:admissions@bluelake.org).

**Late Departure (Sessions 1 – 3 only)**: **$110**  If you are unable to pick your camper up before 4:00pm on Final Sunday of Sessions 1 ,2, or 3, you may arrange to pick them up later that evening or the next day. All late departure campers must be picked up by 5:00pm on Monday. The late departure fee covers all meals, supervision, and activities for the day or any portion thereof. To register for a late departure, visit [My Account](https://bluelake.org/ultracamp/). For details, contact Admissions at 800-221-3796 x209 or [admissions@bluelake.org](mailto:admissions@bluelake.org).

**Staying Between Sessions:** **$190**  Campers who enroll in two consecutive sessions have the option of leaving or staying on camp in between the sessions. Campers opting to stay on camp are subject to a fee for meals, supervision, activities, and accommodations. To register for an additional session and to stay between sessions, visit [My Account](https://bluelake.org/ultracamp/). For details, contact Admissions at 800-221-3796 x209 or [admissions@bluelake.org](mailto:admissions@bluelake.org).

**Transportation: $65 – $250 one way** Blue Lake can provide transportation for students to and from commercial transportation depots within a one-hour radius of the campus. This includes Grand Rapids Air, Train, and Bus and Muskegon Air, Bus, and Ferry. To learn more, visit [Transportation Assistance](https://bluelake.org/summer-camp/registration/transportation-assistance/) and contact Brie Tyler, Executive Assistant Summer Camp, for details and arrangements at 800-221-3796 x206, or [btyler@bluelake.org](mailto:btyler@bluelake.org).

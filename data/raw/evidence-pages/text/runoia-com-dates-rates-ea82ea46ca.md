## Main Camp

### Our classic, choice-based program for 6-16 year-old campers

**The Experience**:
In this choice-based program, campers express their unique interests to create their own schedules for 3-day blocks. Campers are provided with high-quality instruction and the support and structure to build skills and pass levels over the session and from year to year.

## Counselor-in-Training (CIT)

| Counselor-in-Training (CIT) |
| --- |
| A leadership-building program for 16-year-old campers  The Counselor in Training program spans between being a Runoia camper and learning how to teach and counsel youth. As a CIT you help counselors in residential life and activity programs to teach and support children to have a successful time learning. CITs will have the opportunity to earn certifications like Red Cross Lifeguarding and identify their program areas in camp. CITs will also perform community service and leadership work. |

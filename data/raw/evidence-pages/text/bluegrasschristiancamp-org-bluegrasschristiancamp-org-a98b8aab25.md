- At Blue Grass Christian Camp we believe your child's spiritual development is very important and should be a high priority. We strive to create a program and experience that will, in partnership with you and your church, help your child become a lifelong disciple of Jesus. The high quality camp experience that we provide can be expensive, but we believe that tuition cost should not be a barrier to any child who wants to attend BGCC. Many people who believe strongly in the spiritual well being of children contribute each year so that scholarship assistance can be given. Therefore, we use the following five principles to guide us in our efforts to help as many people as possible:

            1. So that we can assist as many as possible, Blue Grass Christian Camp does not award any full scholarships from our general scholarship fund.

            2. There is an expectation that other sources of assistance have been sought out (local church, relatives, etc.).

            3. Scholarship assistance will only be considered when a [Scholarship Form](https://form.jotform.com/253604863467162) is completely filled out and submitted prior to May 15th.

            4. Our scholarship fund is limited, and assistance is granted based on need and awarded in the order requests are received.

            5. A scholarship award cannot be guaranteed even if a scholarship application is submitted on time and income requirements are met.

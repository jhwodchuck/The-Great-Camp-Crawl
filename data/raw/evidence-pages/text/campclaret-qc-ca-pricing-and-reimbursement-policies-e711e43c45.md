# Accessibility pricing and reimbursement policies

Pricing and possible discounts for families or children with special needs and our reimbursement policy

## **Pricing policy – Camp Claret du lac Elgin 202**6

There are three types of rates:

**Standard rate:** The basic rate for 6- or 12-day stays, as posted on the Camp Claret website and on Qidigo, our registration platform.

**Family discount:** As most of our clientele consists of brothers and sisters, we prefer to offer reasonable prices to all our campers. There is no discount for two children in the same family; however, from the 3rd child on, there is a discount of 10% starting at the child. **This fee applies automatically when you register online.**

**For our community :** permanent resident from Stratford will automatically receive a 25% discount.

## Reimbursement policy

Upon cancellation, it is important to contact the office. The fees are fully refundable, prior to 24 hours of the beginning of the stay.

If a child has to leave the camp during his or her stay, the cost of the session fees for the days following departure will be reimbursed.

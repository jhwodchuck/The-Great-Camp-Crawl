# Pre-College @ UMass Amherst

### **Unlock your potential while having fun this summer! Get a taste of the college experience, explore your interests, and experience campus life.**

Discover unique, faculty-led summer program for high school students available at UMass Amherst. Offering academic intensives in over 25 topics including STEM, art & design, business and more, plus unique six-week lab research experiences.

You'll also find year-round college prep workshops that offer you the practical skills and knowledge to confidently navigate the college application process.

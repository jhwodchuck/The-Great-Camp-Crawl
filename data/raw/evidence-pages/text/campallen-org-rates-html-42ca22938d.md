## facility rates

Click on the tabs below to view current rates of all of Camp Allen's facilities. To request availability or for more information, contact us **[here](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#442f2128283d200427252934252828212a6a2b3623)**.

**Hotel and conference Center**

**​**The per-person nightly rates include hotel style lodging, three buffet meals per day, a primary meeting room with most audio-visual equipment. Reservations require a 25% deposit. [Learn more.](/hotel--conference-center.html)
​
**Individual Guest Rates**
Single Occupancy: $160
Double Occupancy: $120 per person
Children (ages 3-12): $50
Day Rate (includes one meal): $35

**Additional Meals**
Breakfast: $12
Lunch: $14
Dinner: $20

​**Additional Meeting Spaces**
Small Meeting Room: $100
Medium Meeting Room: $225
Large Meeting Room: $350 (requires a two night minimum)
All Saints’ Chapel: $750

**Links for Conference Center Amenities**
[Snacks & Socials](/socials.html)
[Services & Rentals](/services.html)
[Specialty Meals](/meals.html)

Campsites 1, 2, 3A, 3B

A two night minimum is required. Reservations require a 25% deposit. Unavailable during the summer months.​ [Learn more.](/campsites-1-2--3.html)

**Nightly Campsite Rates**
Campsites 1 & 2  (Groups <50 people): $850
Campsites 1 & 2 (Groups >50 people): $1,250
Campsite 3A: $850
Campsite 3B: $850

**Campsite Capacities**
Campsite 1: 92 people
Campsite 2: 92 people
Campsite 3A: 64 people
Campsite 3B: 64 people
​

Campsite 4 & bishop doyle center

A two night minimum is required. Reservations require a 50% deposit. Unavailable during the summer months.​ [Learn more.](/bishopdoylecenter.html)

**Campsite 4 Nightly Rates (two night minimum)**
Includes Campsite 4 and Bishop Doyle Center facilities. Sleeping Capacity: 112 people
Groups <50 people: $2,000
Groups >50 people: $3,000

**Bishop Doyle Center Only**
Excludes Campsite 4 bunkhouse facilities. Max Capacity: 400 people
$1,500 per day

GrovE LODGES

A two night minimum is required. Reservations require a 50% deposit. Unavailable during the summer months. [Learn more](/grove-lodges.html).

**Dunn Center**
Rate: $350 per night
Max Capacity: 12 people / 12 beds​

**Grove Lodge 1 or 2**
Rate: $550 per night
Max Capacity: 22 people / 20 beds

Lakeside cabins and meeting center

A two night minimum is required. Reservations require a 50% deposit. [Learn more.](/lakeside-cabins.html)

**​3 Bedroom / 2 Bathroom Cabin**
Rate: $350 per night
Cabins Available: 5
Max Capacity: 10 people / 7 beds

**5 Bedroom / 4 Bathroom Cabin**
Rate: $450 per night
Cabins Available: 2
Max Capacity: 16 people / 11 beds

**Lakeside Meeting Center**
​Rate: $500 per day / $1000 per weekend
Rate with adjoining suite: $600 per day / $1200 per weekend
Max Capacity: 70 people

QUIET HOUSE

A two night minimum is required. Reservations require a 50% deposit. [Learn more.](/quiet-house.html)

**Quiet House Nightly Rate:**
$250; Complimentary 4th night stay
Max Capacity: 4 (ideally for 2)

***A 3% per person facility usage fee will be applied for all overnight guests.****All facility bookings are subject to the state hotel occupancy tax rate of 6%. Tax-exempt organizations are instructed to submit documentation when contracting a facility to have the tax removed from their final bill.*

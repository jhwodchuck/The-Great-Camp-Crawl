# [SUMMER CAMPS](https://www.camplakestephens.com)

Since 1946, Camp Lake Stephens in Oxford, MS, has been a place where faith deepens, friendships grow, and adventure fills each day. Our dedicated staff create space for campers to thrive, laugh, and discover who they are while enjoying nature and our facilities at CLS.

[Learn More](https://www.camplakestephens.com/summer)

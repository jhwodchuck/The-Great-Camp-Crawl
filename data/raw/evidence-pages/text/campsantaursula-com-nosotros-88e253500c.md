## Who are we?

We are an organization with more than 40 years of experience dedicated to the training of children, young people and adults. We focus on teaching in an active, practical and fun way, promoting values such as companionship, honor and solidarity, while promoting contact with nature and sports.


Our goal is to provide exceptional facilities and high-quality service so that each participant has a unique experience. Over the years, we have developed a wealth of experience and have created various forms of organization, games and activities, making us one of the best camps in the world.


We strive to constantly improve, training our staff, keeping our facilities and equipment in optimal condition, and innovating every year with new facilities or activities that are interesting and beneficial to all participants.

### Our Cause

Camp Santa Ursula exists to create fun, formative and memorable experiences in a unique and magical place.
With the mission of building a more conscious society and a better Mexico.

### OUR DREAM

To be the best camp in the world.
To be an international example of warmth, quality, creativity, fun, safety and excellence, always putting care before the environment.
May every experience lived in our programs, in our facilities and with our staff, generate positive changes in people and opportunities to transcend life.
Involve different entities so that, with their support and ours, the experience of a camp reaches thousands of children in vulnerable situations.

### Our values

Security

- Above all, it guarantees the physical and emotional security of each person, starting with your own.
- Before making any decision, think about the first, second and third consequences.
- Keep equipment, tools and facilities in perfect condition.
- Fully master everything you do and always stay up to date.
- Detect, report and ensure that any unsafe conditions are corrected.

Memorable fun

- Create, innovate and build extraordinarily fun things.

- Turn any unpleasant situation into a memorable experience.

- Eradicate bad vocabulary.

- Do things right from the start.

Passionate commitment

- Make sure to date each task and complete it.
- Be the person who can be trusted the most with responsibility.

## Meet our Staff!

At Camp Santa Ursula, our staff is carefully selected and trained to provide the best service, and most of our collaborators were participants in the camp when they were children. With more than 100 employees, we strive to stand out thanks to our facilities and fun environment.

Our administrative team includes roles such as general management, event management, graphic design and office staff, as well as a director in charge of instructors and assistants. Our qualified cooks, together with our Chef, prepare delicious and nutritious meals.

We also have specialized facilitators and instructors for business events, as well as medical, maintenance and quartermaster services to ensure that our facilities are always in optimal condition.

[Join the staff!](/en/staff)

Privileged location

We are in the city of San Luis Potosí, in central Mexico, 1,800 meters above sea level.

San Luis Potosí has excellent land and air connections, as well as a pleasant climate and a rich cultural and natural heritage. If you are coming to drop off or pick up your children, we recommend staying at nearby hotels with which we have agreements, such as the Hilton, the Hyatt Regency and the Hampton Inn.

These hotels offer special rates to our participants!

Camp Season Opens: Sunday, June 28th
Parents Visiting Day: Saturday, July 18th
Camp Season Ends: Saturday, August 15th

**Tuition: $16,600 ($16,300 for returning families only, if enrolled by September 18th)**
LIT Tuition is $15,600 ($15,300 for returning families only, if enrolled by September 18th)

[Senior Village trip fees](http://campecholake.com/future-families/sv-trips/) and baggage constitute the only additional expenses.

**Tuition Payment Options**

**Option 1:** (Three Installments: Check, Credit Card or Automatic Bank Withdrawal (ACH/ETF) One third of the balance (after payment of the $2,000) deposit will be due on December 1, 2025, February 2, 2026 and April 15, 2026. Payments by credit card will include a 3% surcharge per transaction.

**Option 2:** (Monthly Installments: Credit Card and Automatic Bank Withdrawal (ACH/ETF) Only) Beginning October 1, 2025 through May 1, 2026, your credit card will automatically be charged or money withdrawn following monthly balances (after payment of the $2,000 deposit) on the first business day of each month. Payments by credit card will include a 3% surcharge per transaction.

**Option 3:** Pay in full.

## **Traditional Sessions (19 nights)**

- 3 sessions available (Traditional Session 1, 2 and 3) in 2026.
- Traditional Session 1 & 2 for campers entering 3rd – 10th grades in Fall 2026.
- Traditional Session 3 for campers entering 3rd – 11th grades in Fall 2026.
- **$4,950 per camper.**
- Tuition inclusive of all activities, housing, food and snacks, bedding, weekly laundry, CGL t-shirt and water bottle and airport transportation (if required).
- Our sessions begin on Monday afternoons, with drop off times assigned in intervals between 2.30-4pm. Pick up is the final Saturday at 9am.
- **New families only**: once you have submitted your application, one of the Director Team will reach out to schedule a short Zoom call. This is so we can share more about life at CGL with you and your camper, and answer any questions.

**[Follow this link to learn more about our Traditional Sessions.](https://www.campgranitelake.com/traditional-sessions/)**

## **Mini-Camp Sessions (5 nights)**

- 3 mini-camp sessions available in 2026. Mini-camps run at the same time as Traditional Session 3.
- For **new** campers entering 2nd – 5th grades in Fall 2026.
- Former mini-campers who are still eligible for the program and have only attended Mini-Camp once are able to attend **Mini-Camp 3 in 2026.**
- **$1,550 per camper.**
- Tuition inclusive of all activities, housing, food and snacks, bedding, weekly laundry, CGL t-shirt and water bottle and airport transportation (if required).
- Sessions begin on Monday afternoon, with drop off times assigned in intervals between 2.30-4pm. Pick up is the final Saturday at 9am.

**[Follow this link to learn more about our Mini-Camp programs.](https://www.campgranitelake.com/mini-camp/)**

## **Counselor In Training Program (19 nights)**

- Our Counselor in Training (CIT) program is a leadership opportunity for campers entering 11th grade in Fall 2026.
- **$4,150 per camper.**
- Takes place during Traditional Sessions 1&2.
- Camp fees are inclusive of ALL activities, housing, food and snacks and weekly laundry, and campers also receive a CGL Nalgene water bottle and t-shirt with the camp logo.

**[Follow this link to learn more about our CIT Program.](https://www.campgranitelake.com/counselor-in-training-program/)**

## **Payments & Refund Policies**

No charges will be made to credit cards until we confirm that we have space. We will reach out if we need to place your camper on a wait list.

#### **Payment Options**

- A **non-refundable deposit** of **$750** per camper is due at enrollment.
- Monthly payment plans available starting in October 2025. First installment must be paid by January 2026.
- Monthly installments are charged on the second Wednesday of each month, and we will send you an email reminder two days before.

#### **Payment Methods**

- Credit Card: Mastercard or VISA. Each transaction comes with a 2.85% credit card processing fee.
- Debit Card: No fee.
- eCheck: Requires checking/saving account number and routing number. No fee.

#### **Refunds**

- Deposits are non-refundable.
- After the initial deposit, all payments are refundable for 30 days.

## **Discounts**

- **Returning Family Discount**
  - $200

  - One per family.
- **Early Bird Discount**
  - $200
  - Enroll before October 1st, 2025.
  - One per family.
- **Sibling Discount:**
  - $200 (two siblings)
  - $600 (three siblings)
- **Referral Discount**
  - $100
  - For both the referring and the referred family.

## **Financial Aid**

We have a small, self-funded Financial Aid program to help support families give their camper/s the experience of overnight camp. Unfortunately we are not able to provide full scholarships at this time.

Financial Aid applications for returning families opens in January 2026, and applications open in March 2026 for new families.

Please email directors@campgranitelake.com for more information about Financial Aid.

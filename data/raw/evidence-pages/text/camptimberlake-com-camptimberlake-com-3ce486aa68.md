## Welcome to Camp Timberlake!

These are the first words that every boy hears when he arrives at camp, and they mean a lot to us. We love welcoming boys to our camp family and Timberlake is a small enough program where everybody knows your name. It’s a place where new boys feel like camp insiders from the very beginning.

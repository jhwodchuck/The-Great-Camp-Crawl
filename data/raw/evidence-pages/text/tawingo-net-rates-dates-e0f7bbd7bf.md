## **2026 DAY CAMP**

Each week of Day Camp is 5 days - from Monday to Friday. (\*This is also the case for Week E which begins on a Statutory Holiday - August 3.)

Camp begins at 9:00am and runs until 4:45pm. Transportation to and from Camp is available from downtown Huntsville.

The cost of one week of ***Day Camp*** is ***$389.00*** and ***INCLUDES*** a healthy lunch and 2 snacks.

There are no taxes for Day Camp.

**Transportation** to and from Camp for one week is ***$85.00.***

### OUR NEXT GREAT ADVENTURE

Welcome to YMCA Camp Tumbleson Lake, a traditional lakeside day and overnight camp located on 300 beautiful acres near Ward, Colorado. Just 20 miles from Boulder, Colorado, we provide children ages 6-17 with outdoor adventures, resilience-nurturing experiences, support from a welcoming camp community and countless moments of fun and silliness in the heart of the Indian Peaks Wilderness Area. YMCA Camp Tumbleson Lake is unlike any camp in Boulder County.

We are less than an hour from Boulder and Longmont and less than 2 hours from Denver, Loveland, Fort Collins and the North Metro area of Colorado.

### A NEW ERA IN CAMPING

For the past 18 years, YMCA of Northern Colorado leased the Camp Santa Maria property in an effort to provide Colorado’s children and teens with overnight camp experiences that foster resilience, confidence and a love for the outdoors. However, in 2024, the Y purchased its own camp property in Boulder County and has transformed it into YMCA Camp Tumbleson Lake. In 2024, the Y held day camps at Camp Tumbleson Lake, and in 2025, we launched overnight camps at this picturesque lakeside mountain escape.

We are ready for our next great adventure. Are you?

We have options for campers ages 6 to 17, providing age-appropriate experiences that welcome and help kids and teens foster confidence, independence and resilience. From day camps and our Explorers mini camp for first time campers to traditional camps to teen expedition and leadership programs, we have something for every camper.

### IT’S GONNA BE AN EPIC SUMMER

Our camp is perfectly set up to provide campers with the ultimate summer experience: hiking, mountain biking, climbing, watching sunsets, paddling on the lake, creating art, hitting a bulls eye, bouncing through our lake-top obstacle course and roasting marshmallows by the campfire. Songs, chants and dance parties included!

Plus, our camp counselors nurture campers’ knowledge and abilities by teaching them survival skills, leave no trace principles, nature education and much more. Children and teens are given opportunities to take risks in a safe environment and with a community to support them along the way. And it’s not just kid stuff. From adventure camps that take campers through rugged wilderness to our leadership and counselor-in-training programs that give teens a chance to guide others, camp is a powerful way to provide young adults with mentorship opportunities, confidence-building exercises and real-life experience.

### CREATING HAPPY CAMPERS SINCE 1885

What sets us apart from other mountain camps? We follow in the footsteps of camping giants. In the 1880s, the YMCA opened America’s first known summer camp program. For nearly 140 years, the Y has made summer special for hundreds of thousands of young people, offering campers a positive developmental experience through building bonds, nurturing confidence and growing in self-reliance — all in the great outdoors. We are continuing that legacy.

**Our Vision:** Get Kids outside to build character, make friends, and have fun.

**Our Mission:** YMCA Camp Tumbleson Lake provides a community where kids gain skills, become more independent and learn to grow with others while having fun.

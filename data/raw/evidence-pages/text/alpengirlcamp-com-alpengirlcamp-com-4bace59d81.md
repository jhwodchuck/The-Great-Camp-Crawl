## We Are Alpengirl

Alpengirl offers adventure trips
for girls ages 11-17 and for adult women.

Discover yourself and connect with others through adventure in the great outdoors.

![](https://www.alpengirlcamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/54658365982_2bc22b58ca_o-1-1024x858.jpg)

From the trails of the Pacific Northwest to snow-capped peaks in Slovenia, we offer one to two week trips for every type of adventurer.

![](https://www.alpengirlcamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/kayak-1024x768.avif)

No two days look the same. With multi-adventure trips, you could be hiking on one day, surfing the other, and llama packing the next.

![](https://www.alpengirlcamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/54644691798_7ccc6a5850_o-1-1024x818.jpg)

Trips are designed for each individual’s wellbeing with healthy meals that meet dietary considerations and comfortable nightly accommodations.

![](https://www.alpengirlcamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/cmaper-group--768x1024.avif)

With small group sizes no larger than 12 participants, you’ll enter as strangers and leave as family after days spent on the trail and stopping for roadside dance parties, and nights around the campfire.

![](https://www.alpengirlcamp.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/54661172091_145e0ece62_o-768x1024.jpg)

Rest assured you’re in good hands with our incredible, highly trained two to three guides per trip, creating a highly supervised and supported one-to-four or one-to-six participant to staff ratio.

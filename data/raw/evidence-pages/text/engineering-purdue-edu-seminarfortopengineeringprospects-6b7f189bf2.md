Ready to turn your passion for innovation into the foundation of an extraordinary career? Seminar for Top Engineering Prospects (STEP) is more than just a summer camp — it’s a gateway to the world of engineering excellence. ## Explore engineering at Purdue University

Since 1985, STEP has offered a unique, weeklong summer residential program for rising high school seniors. STEP provides an exciting opportunity to dive into various engineering disciplines through hands-on learning, teamwork and innovative challenges.

## Why choose STEP?

- **Learn core engineering skills:** Explore design principles, teamwork, physics, programming, 3D modeling and robotics in an interactive learning environment. Build your creativity, communication, design and analytical modeling skills.
- **Team-based challenges:** Collaborate with peers to tackle exciting design challenges, culminating in a final design competition.
- **Expert guidance:** Learn from staff and current engineering honors students.
- **Fun beyond the classroom:** Enjoy Purdue traditions, exploring campus, a scavenger hunt, a movie night and more!

### STEP offers a perfect balance of academic and social activities!

## What to expect

### Engineering = Teamwork + Creativity + Real-World Problem Solving

- **Interactive learning:** Experience collaborative classroom sessions and apply theoretical concepts to practical challenges.
- **Skill development:** Build creativity, communication, design, and analytical modeling skills.
- **Teamwork:** Work in groups of four throughout the program to tackle exciting design challenges.
- **Discipline tour:** Explore two Engineering disciplines through guided facility tours.
- **Career exploration:** The Office of Future Engineering presents a career engagement activity.

## STEP application process

### Who should apply

- Open to rising high school seniors (Class of 2027).
- Recommended: Completion of three years of high school math and one year of chemistry or physics by summer 2026.

### Application details

Applications are accepted until all spots are filled. Once full, qualified applicants will be placed on a waiting list. A complete application includes your standardized test scores (PSAT, SAT, or ACT) along with an official/unofficial transcript. Your application will not be reviewed until this information is fully completed.

- Attendance Fee: $2,500
- Scholarships: Need-based scholarships are available. Contact the College of Engineering Honors Program, [step@purdue.edu](mailto:step@purdue.edu), after being accepted into the program. Scholarships are awarded on an individual, first-come, first-served basis until all available funds have been fully dispersed.
- Notification: You’ll be informed of your application status via email after all components are submitted and reviewed.

STEP is a transformative experience designed to inspire and challenge future engineers. Apply today to take your first step toward a brighter future in engineering!

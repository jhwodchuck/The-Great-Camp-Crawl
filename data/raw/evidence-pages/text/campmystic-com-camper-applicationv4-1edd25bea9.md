# Camper Application

Campers are eligible to attend Camp Mystic the summer after they complete the 2nd grade. Each camper application requires a $300 deposit, which is **fully refundable** until March 1st of the year the camper is registered to attend camp. For campers who are on the waiting list, the deposit is **fully refundable** at any time.

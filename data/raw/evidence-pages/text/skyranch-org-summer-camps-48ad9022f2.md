## An incredible week of Sky Ranch Summer Camp includes:

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvN2g4aHh4Z3IzcF9udW1iZXJfaWNvbi5zdmciXV0/number-icon.svg)

##### Small Camper to Staff Ratio

Our camper to staff ratio ranges from 6:1 in the cabin, to even 3:1 depending on the activity. Cabin sizes vary from 11 to 22 campers based on age. There are 3 -5 counselors in every cabin – ensuring campers are known, cared for and encouraged.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvMThqemZ0YmNlcl9jb3Vuc2Vsb3JfaWNvbi5zdmciXV0/counselor-icon.svg)

##### Relational Counselors

Your child will spend every moment with their cabin group led by our highly trained, college aged staff. Counselors are with their campers 24/7 – present during every meal, activity, and night event. Building trust, encouraging community, and modeling what it looks like to live out the Gospel.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvM3F2c2lxdDlxcl9iaWJsZV9pY29uLnN2ZyJdXQ/bible-icon.svg)

##### Bible Study

Curriculum guided by the intentional plan of the Life Map. Our rotating world view questions meet campers right where they are with Gospel truths that reinforce their unique value, their identity in Christ, and dependence on Him.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvMWlwb3lnZTA5Ml9jb252ZXJzYXRpb25faWNvbi5zdmciXV0/conversation-icon.svg)

##### New Friends

Cabin groups quickly feel like family – creating community, a sense of belonging, and developing friendships that last long after their week of camp is over. Campers learn empathy, how to encourage others, and how to invest in meaningful relationships.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvODhpbTVuaWUyeF9yYWRpb19pY29uLnN2ZyJdXQ/radio-icon.svg)

##### Parties Every Day

Dance parties, costume parties, messy parties - each night there is a different themed event tailored for your kid’s age and gender! These shared memories of fun and craziness will become some of their favorite stories to tell.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvN2JtMXFqcjl3cl9zdXBlcmhlcm9faWNvbi5zdmciXV0/superhero-icon.svg)

##### On-Site Superheroes

We have trained staff for every situation. From a 24-hour health center utilizing nurses, EMTs, and doctors, to certified activity specialists, and experienced leadership ready to address homesickness and dietary concerns – we are well-trained and prepared to respond.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjEvMDQvMDEvN2FsaWJqNTJyYl91bnBsdWdnZWRfaWNvbi5zdmciXV0/unplugged-icon.svg)

##### An Unplugged Experience

Freedom from screens and social pressures reduces comparisons, overstimulation, and constant notification-driven stress. Being outside, engaging in physical activity, and connecting with nature restores normal sleep rhythms. Your child will come home refreshed with a greater sense of hope and purpose.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMjMvMDEvMjYvOWdmM3JxcWE4cl9waG90b19pY29uLnN2ZyJdXQ/photo-icon.svg)

##### Camper Photos and Parent Emails

We provide a glimpse into camp life through the Bunk1 app with daily photos, updates, and one-way Bunk Notes to your camper.

### Bible Study

Sky Ranch’s biblically-based study curriculum answers one of the annually rotating worldview questions:

- Who is God? (The character and nature of God)
- What is our need? (The person and work of Jesus)
- Who are you? (The body of Christ)
- What is Truth? (The Word of God)

Each program will tackle these 4 questions from the Life Map meant to intentionally challenge and encourage our campers where they are at developmentally. By moving through them every four years, campers work through these questions in the different stages of childhood – developing and deepening in their understanding as they mature.

![](/media/W1siZiIsIjIwMTcvMTIvMTQvMnViaTJyM3d5aV9hY3Rpdml0aWVzX2NyYWNrZXJfYmFycmVsLmpwZyJdLFsicCIsInRodW1iIiwiMjAweDIwMCMiXV0/activities-cracker-barrel.jpg)

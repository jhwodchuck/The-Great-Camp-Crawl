## **Mini Camp**

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5c3ac18dc5f5755932b76a9f/1761752249608-GQHJMRJZ4IAZ2716IG4T/B77A8230.JPG)

Mini Camp serves as an introduction to camp life. Targeted toward younger campers, boys attend activities together with their cabin groups, sampling a variety of what Camp Arrowhead has to offer. They’ll experience **council fire**, an **overnight campout**, and evening games like **Capture the Chicken**.

Unlike longer sessions, Mini Campers won’t choose their own morning activities or choice blocks, but they’ll still get to enjoy favorites such as [**blacksmithing**](/blacksmithing), [**archery**](/archery), and [**art**](/arts-crafts).

*This session is intentionally small to ensure our youngest campers receive the highest level of support and have the best possible first camp experience.*

## **Session 1**

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5c3ac18dc5f5755932b76a9f/aee3b486-ce4f-4a00-a0fd-c7a093e08fc3/B77A9751.JPG)

This two-week camp is perfect for first-time campers or boys still getting used to camp life. Campers select their own [**morning activities**](/morning-activities) and [**choice blocks**](/choice-blocks), building skills and confidence as they explore. Projects are simpler than in later sessions, giving boys a chance to try new things and learn at a comfortable pace.

Session 1 includes all the best parts of camp — **council fire evenings**, **Arrowhead Adventures**, and skill-building in areas like [**survival skills**](/bushcraft), [**woodworking**](/woodshop), and **archery**.

## **Session 2**

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5c3ac18dc5f5755932b76a9f/8eacc8cb-e958-4521-b0f9-4935eef88292/B77A2035.JPG)

Campers can sign up for either **two or three weeks** during Session 2. While similar to Session 1, this session offers more opportunities for boys to advance their skills and tackle more challenging projects. Whether it’s **long-range archery**, a **complex blacksmithing project**, or mastering a new craft, Session 2 encourages growth and deeper engagement.

Campers will also take part in **overnight camping trips**, **council fires**, and another exciting **Arrowhead Adventure**.

*Three-week campers may stay for Maker Camp as a fourth week.*

## **Maker Camp**

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5c3ac18dc5f5755932b76a9f/72aa9eeb-4e08-4441-a796-0efa989f506a/IMG_2402.jpg)

Do you have an older boy who loves using tools, building things, and creating with his hands? [**Maker Camp**](/maker-camp-week) is designed just for him. This session is for more mature campers who are ready to dive deeper into the **craft and process of making**— from **blacksmithing and knife making, leatherworking, and** [**flint knapping**](/flint-knapping) to **woodworking and toolmaking**.

Expert instructors join our experienced summer staff to provide high-quality, hands-on instruction. Campers focus on detailed projects, learning advanced techniques and taking pride in their craftsmanship.

*This session is extremely limited to ensure each camper receives the best possible guidance and attention.*

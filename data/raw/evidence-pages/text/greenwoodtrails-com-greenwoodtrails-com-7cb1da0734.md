### Caring. Camp. Community.

Greenwood Trails is a true camp community. We celebrate each camper’s uniqueness, while embracing the common ground that brings us together. Our dedicated staff creates a caring atmosphere where campers feel comfortable being themselves, and where genuine friendships thrive. Greenwood Trails is a place where people are encouraged to treat each other with respect, support one another, and live with integrity, creating a warm, positive community where everyone feels welcome.

## CAMPER SPOTLIGHT

# Becky

*I always get a lot of personal growth and it's really cool to look back to see how far or different I've become. When I was little I was not very confident at all. I was generally a shy person and was not good at making friends. Now looking at where I am, I'm a CIT, I can get up on stage, I do the school plays. I know I never, ever would have been able to do that without camp. Everyone's there supporting you, they want you to succeed and everyone wants you to have fun. Looking back, I've become more confident and I think more capable. I'm definitely learning a lot of life skills.*](https://greenwoodtrails.com/campstory/becky/)

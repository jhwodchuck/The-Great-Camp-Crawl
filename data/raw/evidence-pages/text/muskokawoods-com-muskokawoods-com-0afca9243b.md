In addition to traditional summer camp activities, we also feature a high ropes course, media arts, waterski and wakeboarding.

Muskoka Woods is led by a qualified and experienced full-time team. In addition to decades of experience at Muskoka Woods, many hold degrees in education, youth development and master’s degrees in leadership.

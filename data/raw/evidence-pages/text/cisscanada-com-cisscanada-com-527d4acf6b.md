## What We Do

CISS is committed to providing a stimulating, challenging, and safe learning environment for students of all nationalities. We offer the highest quality student products available, and take pride in presenting them with a distinctly Canadian flavour. Our programmes are carefully crafted so students from age 8 to 18 can have an unforgettable experience.

## Choose the best path for you in Canada

Come in the summer, in the winter, or for a full year. Choose an academic experience in a Canadian High School, or activities by the lake in a Summer Camp. Stay in a homestay or at School Residence. Send your students for individual programmes or customize a programme for a group of students. CISS has a varied offer of programmes that meet students and partners needs and interests!

# About Us

#### Alexander College is a private post-secondary institution serving approximately 3,000 students every year.

![Alexander College student wearing an Alexander College tshirt](https://alexandercollege.ca/web-2024/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/alexander-college-student-wearing-college-t-shirt.jpg)

## About Alexander College

Alexander College offers a variety of undergraduate programs, including University Transfer, an Associate of Arts degree, and an Associate of Science degree. An integrated English for Academic Purposes program is also available to assist non-native English speakers with their transition to university level studies in the English language.

Alexander College programs are approved by the BC Ministry of Post-Secondary Education and Future Skills, by the Degree Quality Assessment Board (DQAB), and by the BC Education Quality Assurance (EQA) branch. Alexander College is also a proud participant in the BC Transfer System, organized by the BC Council on Admissions and Transfer (BCCAT). The academic year has four terms: Fall (Sept-Dec), Winter (Jan-Apr), Spring (May-July) and Summer intensive (July-Aug).

## Vision

Located in British Columbia Canada, Alexander College aspires to be internationally recognized as a welcoming, inclusive community, where students are academically and socially supported on their quest for success in their studies and careers.

## Mission

Alexander College offers excellence in higher education for international and domestic students through university transfer courses, degrees, and other programs to meet the needs of students and the community in an inclusive and supportive environment.

##### Insurance Based Membership

At the Treasure Valley Family YMCA, we offer 3 insurance-based membership plans: Silver Sneakers, Silver & Fit and Renew Active. Many Medicare Advantage/Supplemental health plans offer these programs to eligible insurance participants. If you qualify, you’ll get to work out more, meet new friends, and be more social. The best part of all is that there is no cost to you. Our Insurance Based members also have the option to add a second family member on their membership for $40/month and family upgrade for 2+ family members is $55/month.

To get started today, you can call the number on the back of your insurance card or visit any of our 4 Treasure Valley Family YMCA facilities to find out if you qualify. All you need to bring with you is your license or any government-issued identification and your insurance card.

##### Military Membership

The Treasure Valley Family YMCA offers a waived join fee and 5% discount on memberships for all active-duty or veteran service members. [Contact your nearest branch to join today](/facilities/)!

We also partner with the Armed Services YMCA to offer free membership to eligible service members and their families. [Click here to learn more about ASYMCA](https://asymca.org/).

##### Annual Payment

Dues may be paid in full for 12 months. Payment is accepted via cash, check, or credit card. Contact a Y facility for more details.

##### Short Term Membership

Short term memberships are available and can be purchased on a month-to-month basis. Due to increased accounting costs, short term membership fees are 50% higher than quoted membership rates and automatically terminate after one month. Please see the membership desk for details.

##### Checking/Savings Account or Credit Card Draft

This is the easiest and most convenient way to pay for your membership. Your fees are automatically withdrawn from your checking, savings, or credit card account once a month. To reduce our expenses, we appreciate it if members consider providing us with a voided check for monthly payments.

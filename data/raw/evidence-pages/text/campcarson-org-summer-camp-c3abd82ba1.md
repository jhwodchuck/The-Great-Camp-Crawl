## The Value of Camp

# CAMPERS DESIGN THEIR ACTIVITY SCHEDULE

Our program encourages campers to choose their own activities. [Click HERE](https://www.campcarson.org/freedom-of-choice-benefits) to learn more.

​

# ONGOING SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Campers can choose activities from a recreational perspective or work on skill development, which we track throughout their years at camp.

​

# INTERNATIONAL STAFF

We bring the world to camp. Our campers will meet staff from across the globe. We usually have  8-10 nations represented each summer.

​

# TAKE A TOUR!

We invite you to visit YMCA Camp Carson before your camper arrives.  Please contact us at 812-385-3597 to schedule a personal tour.

## Unplug. Connect. Grow.

Welcome to Surprise Lake Camp, where generations of campers grades K-10 call home. SLC is one of the nation’s longest-running Jewish sleepaway camps—and the starting point for lifelong friendships, spectacular adventures, skills, confidence, and warm community. We can’t wait for you to join us!

# Quality Maine Camping

Laurel South, on the shores of Crescent Lake in Maine’s Sebago Lakes region, is known for taking great care of children! Through each action-packed 3.5-week session, campers are exposed to a wide range of activities across our core program of: Athletics, Waterfront, Adventure, Inter-Arts, Tennis, Equestrian and Special Areas. Our caring, nurturing and professional staff of coaches, educators and college students encourages our campers to develop new interests, gain new skills and discover new strengths in our inclusive and welcoming community. Laurel South is about making friends, building confidence and having fun!

# A Guide For First-Time Campers

Sending a child to summer camp is a tremendous act of faith by parents. At Camp Laurel South, we take that responsibility seriously and encourage our campers to develop new interests and skills as they discover new strengths. We maintain an environment that encourages safety and reinforces a positive self-image.

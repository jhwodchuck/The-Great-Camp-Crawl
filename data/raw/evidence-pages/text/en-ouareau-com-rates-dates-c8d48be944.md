### Rebates & deposit

- A **10% rebate** will be applied to all registrations for **a second (or third, or fourth, or fifth...) sister** that comes to Ouareau.
- If you tell **another family** about Ouareau, and they register for the current season, a **$200 rebate** will be applied to your account at the end of the season once everyone has attended.
- A 500$ deposit is due upon registration - the balance is due April 15th, 2026.

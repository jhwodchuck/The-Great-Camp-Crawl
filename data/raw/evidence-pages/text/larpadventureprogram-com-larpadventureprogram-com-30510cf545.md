### **What we offer**

Through martial arts, theater-based storytelling, and social-emotional learning, we strive to create a community where our players can discover the hero within.

![](https://larpadventureprogram.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/Homepage-Programing-2025_72dpi.jpg)

##### Afterschool classes

###### Fall, Winter & Spring

Continue your adventure afterschool and build community with fellow LARPers! Play and create LARP games, create new characters, and develop your own fantasy world. Here, players will engage in longer-term projects centering their creative aspirations and teamwork.

 [Learn More](/classes/)](/classes/)[![](https://larpadventureprogram.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/event-scroll.png)

##### LARP SEASON

###### Weekends Oct to May

Delve into a LARP story twenty years in the making! We offer all-day and overnight weekend events in the land of Etheraz, fraught with mystery, magic, and where heroes are born. Leave reality behind and step into a world where anyone can change the course of history… if they have the courage. The year’s events culminate in a residential camp where the players’ mettle and might will be tested!

 [Learn More](https://larpadventureprogram.com/product/2025-2026-season-of-ashen-grace/)](https://larpadventureprogram.com/product/2025-2026-season-of-ashen-grace/)[![](https://larpadventureprogram.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/daycamp-scroll.png)

##### Day Camps

###### Summer, Winter & Spring

Design and build your own sword, train for epic battles, and enter the fantasy world of Etheraz where you join the ranks of intrepid heroes. Through this camp, heroes-in-training will learn the basics of LARP that culminates in an immersive fantasy game at the end of the week. Learn teamwork, creative solving, and see if you have what it takes to become a legend!

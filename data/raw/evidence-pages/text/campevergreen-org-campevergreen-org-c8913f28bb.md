Wilderness-centered summer camp experiences that build confidence, and foster strong relationships with others and with God.

[JOIN US](/join-us)

CAMP EVERGREEN IS A

## wilderness-centered Christian camp located in the North Georgia mountains.

[WHO WE ARE](/about)

**God’s creation, unplugged.**Nestled in the North Georgia mountains along a creek, we provide campers with the opportunity to escape to nature, experience adventure and connect with God’s creation.

**Feels like family.**Since 1999, family-owned-and-operated Camp Evergreen has welcomed thousands of kids and teens to experience the joy of summer camp.

**Everything that you need, nothing that you don’t.**
From daily worship and playing in Moccasin Creek to silly, wild evening games—and everything in between—we are intentionally small and wholeheartedly believe in good, old-fashioned, unplugged fun.

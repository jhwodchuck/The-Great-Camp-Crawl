# Alive Again

### December 18-20, 2026

Registration to be Announced

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/64c1337ae6ae683cd52b304a/2e57eff9-87df-4d31-930b-f606a5cf16ac/aa1.jpg)

Join us for Alive Again, our annual December retreat where counselors and campers come to Big Sandy for a weekend filled with all the best things about The Pines without the summer heat! As we prepare our hearts for the coming of Jesus during this Advent season, we will be reminded in a new way how His love burns brightly within us.

This weekend is for 8-12th campers, and is filled with fun activities, great talks, Mass, adoration, and friends!

### Activities

It’s a jam-packed weekend full of fun, including:

- Mass & Adoration
- Bonfire
- Hype Night
- All your favorite camp activities
- Zipline & Adventure Park
- Winner’s Circle
- and SO MUCH MORE!

## Are you ready for a winter weekend to remember?

## Details

- ####

  **Tuition for the weekend is $240**. This covers all meals and activities. For an additional cost, bus transportation is available to and from our Dallas office.

  - Round Trip: $105
  - One-Way: $70
- ####

  To add transportation to your account, simply fill out the Transportation Form [**in your online account**](https://thepines.campintouch.com/v2/login.aspx).

  Pricing:

  - $115.00 Round-trip
  - $75.00 One Way

  If your camper will be traveling by bus from the Dallas Office on Friday, please arrive by 3 pm. The bus will leave no later than 4 PM.

  The bus will be returning on Sunday. It is scheduled to arrive at 1:30 PM.

  **Pines Dallas Office Address:**

  2009 Chenault Dr, Suite 110
  Carrollton, TX 75006

  *\*Please note, campers are not allowed to drive a car to camp for the weekend. They must be signed in and out by a parent or guardian over 21 years of age.*
- ####

  **Gates will be open for check in from 5:00-6:30 pm**.

  **Alive Again programming will begin with dinner at 7:00 PM**, so please make sure you are checked in and in your cabin by then.

  **Upon your arrival,** we ask you to remain in your vehicles until our staff greets you and gives you directions for dropping off luggage and where to park.

  **If you need to check in late,** email us (registrar@thepines.org) and let us know in advance! Please provide an approximate arrival time so our staff can be prepared to welcome you.

  *\*Please note, campers are not allowed to drive a car to camp for the weekend. They must be signed in and out by a parent or guardian 21+ years of age.*

  —

  **Check-out** will be on Sunday starting at 11:00 AM.
- ####

  Well-labeled clothing and items help campers return home with all of their belongings. [**Download the Alive Again packing list here.**](/s/Packing-List-Alive-Again.pdf)
- ####

  All Alive Again registrations must be accompanied by a $50 deposit. This deposit is non-refundable, non-transferable between campers, and cannot be applied to the following year.

  All payments other than the initial deposit are refundable if cancellation is made in writing by email to registrar@thepines.org prior to November 21, 2025.

  Cancellations after November 21 are not eligible for refund. In the case of cancellation due to illness or injury, please provide documentation that a medical professional has directed the camper not to attend. In either of these cases, all funds paid beyond the deposit will be returned.

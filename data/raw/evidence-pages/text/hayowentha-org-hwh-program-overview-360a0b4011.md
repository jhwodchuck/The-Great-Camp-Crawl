# Overnight Camps

# Unique Summer Adventures for All

YMCA Hayo-Went-Ha Camps promote personal growth and teamwork through wilderness adventures and summer camp experiences, focusing on core values like Respect, Inclusion, and Teamwork. By unplugging from the digital world and engaging with nature, campers develop essential life skills such as resilience and leadership. Our wilderness trips are tailored to each age group, encouraging campers to step out of their comfort zones, overcome challenges, and build strong friendships. Led by qualified trip leaders, these experiences ensure safety while fostering character and a sense of community. Campers return home with a profound sense of accomplishment.

![Image](https://hayowentha.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/926A2CD5-5D18-4DD0-8F71-BA88202F20E8_1_105_c-e1760116669924.jpeg)

# Find Your Perfect Program

Our two-week programs give campers the opportunity to try all the traditional summer camp activities that Hayo-Went-Ha Camps offer, along with the exciting challenge of a short wilderness adventure trip.

Our four-week sessions allow campers plenty of time to enjoy our in-camp activities and embark on longer, more challenging wilderness trips. The extended sessions also encourage campers to form deeper bonds with their cabinmates, often resulting in lifelong friendships.

## A secret, small paradise

Camp Balcones Springs is located in the beautiful Texas Hill Country, just 40 miles northwest of Austin. Established in 1993, founded on Christian principles, CBS is among Texas’ most progressive camps, redefining the overnight summer camp experience for boys and girls, ages 6 to 16.

Because **this is a HUGE opportunity for growth**.

### Office of the Registrar

**Email:** [registrar@alexandercollege.ca](mailto:registrar@alexandercollege.ca)
**Office Hours:** M-F, 8:30am – 4:30pm

The Registrar is a member of the College’s senior leadership responsible for student records and
primarily involved in the development and application of College policy and procedures in admissions, placement
testing, financial aid, term scheduling, course registration, academic advising, and student records (e.g. privacy, storage, grades,
records retention, etc.), as well as transfer credit, articulation, course and program development, graduation and
conferral of degrees.

The Registrar’s work is primarily behind the scenes, serving on various administrative committees and providing
direction to staff and faculty related to the aforementioned admin areas.

**Email:** [associateregistrar@alexandercollege.ca](mailto:associateregistrar@alexandercollege.ca)
**Office Hours:** M-F, 8:30am – 4:30pm

The Associate Registrar’s role is to support the Registrar with the day-to-day leadership of the department in all functional areas, and also to specifically lead term course scheduling and course registration as well as coordination of needs with information systems.

Her work is primarily behind the scenes, problem solving, training, and providing guidance to staff and faculty.

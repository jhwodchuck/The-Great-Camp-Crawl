# Camp Lohikan

## For the most fun on the planet!

### Overnight Summer Camp in Lake Como, Pennsylvania

Camp Lohikan has been owned and operated by the Buynak family since 1957. For 70 years we have provided a safe, nurturing camp environment where children try new things, learn new skills, make friends, and most of all have FUN!

Amazing things happen when kids are having the time of their lives! In our supportive camp community campers receive lots of positive reinforcement and come home after camp with more independence, stronger self-confidence, and enhanced social skills. It’s an experience with benefits that last a lifetime!

What sets Camp Lohikan apart from other camps is our program and staff. Nowhere else will you find a summer program with more challenging, exciting activities than Lohikan.

Campers get to choose from 65+ arts, sports, and adventure activities every day! And they’re surrounded by highly skilled staff members who enjoy working with children. At least 50% of Lohikan’s staff are experienced, returning staff members. Our key management staff have over 200 years of combined camp experience.

Campers come to Camp Lohikan from all over. Last summer we had campers come to camp from 21 states and 19 countries. It’s a diverse population that shares two goals: TO HAVE FUN and TO MAKE FRIENDS! If this is your goal this summer, you’ve found the right camp! Any questions, call us. We’re here to help!

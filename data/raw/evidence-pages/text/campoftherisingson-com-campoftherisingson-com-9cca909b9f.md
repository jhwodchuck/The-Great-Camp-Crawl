# Camp of the Rising Son

Give your child the *best summer ever* by sending them to an unforgettable week at Camp of the Rising Son! Get ready to experience life-changing fun, crazy themes, incredible activities, memory-making adventures, and new friends while learning about and experiencing the love of Jesus Christ!  For kids ages 6-17, Camp of the Rising Son offers exciting Christ-centered overnight camps and a variety of teen adventures sure to give your child an amazing week!

We are located on beautiful Lake Ann with over 300 acres of woodlands along the breath-taking Natchez Trace Parkway, in French Camp,

.![](https://campoftherisingson.com/storage/app/media/25-26%20Update%20Images/frenchcamp_ms.gif)

Camp of the Rising Son has over 46 years of experience providing a safe, fun-filled summer experience for campers.

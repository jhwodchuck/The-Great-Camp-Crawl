# ABOUT US

CAMP OLYMPIA is a private, overnight Texas summer camp for boys and girls ages 6-16 offering one-week, two-week, and three-week camp sessions.

Summer camp at Olympia is an experience like no other. We have TONS of fun outdoors, but the camp experience is so much more than that. We strive to create a positive, tech-free environment where children can be themselves, be encouraged by adult role models, grow in confidence and independence, discover new qualities and skills, and learn the true essence of friendship and community.

What makes Olympia unique:

- **Location:** We’re on the beautiful shores of Lake Livingston in the tall pines of Trinity, Texas
- **Activities:** Campers CHOOSE from over 45+ activity options, letting each camper create personalized schedules
- **Values:** Growth, confidence, independence, friendship, community, and tons of FUN!
- **Staff:** Experienced & enthusiastic staff maintaining a 1:4 staff-to-camper ratio
- **Safety:** Accredited by the American Camping Association
- **Tech-Free:** Campers leave their phones and screens at home so they can make REAL connections and have REAL experiences
- **Facilities:** 30 air-conditioned cabins and fields, courts, and buildings to accommodate our huge variety of activities and sleepaway experience

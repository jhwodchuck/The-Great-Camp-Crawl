## Register Online

It’s easy to register for camp online! Simply pick the week you’d like your child(ren) to attend and begin the process. After completing your online registration, you’ll receive an email confirming your week. We require an initial deposit of $400 for each week per child, which you can book with your VISA or MasterCard. Our online system is safe, secure and confidential. Final account payments are due April 15, 2026​. If registering after April 15, 2026​, full payment is required at the time of registration.

## Additional Options

We offer additional options that you can purchase when registering or by logging into your account. All prices are subject to HST.

### Camp Essentials Kit

A ready-to-go bundle that includes a sleeping bag, pillow with pillowcase, fitted bed sheet, beach towel, gym bag, water bottle, sunglasses, sunscreen, toothbrush and toothpaste. Perfect for first-time or international guests, it saves time, shopping, and suitcase space before camp even begins!

$270

### Laundry Service

Available only for guests who are staying for multiple consecutive weeks.

$55

### Kraken Waterslide Lunch/Dinner Pass

**Weeks 1-9 ONLY.** This is offered at a limited capacity for safety on the waterslide. Guests with this pass can use the Kraken Waterslide, one of our most popular activities, during lunch and dinnertime Monday – Friday.

$69

### Kraken & Wibit Combo Pass

**Weeks 1-9 ONLY.** This is offered at a limited capacity for safety. Guests with this pass can use the Kraken Waterslide or the Wibit, two of our most popular activities, during lunch and dinnertime Monday – Friday.

$105

### Laser Tag Lunch Pass

**Weeks 1-9 ONLY**. This is offered at a limited capacity for safety. Guests with this pass can play Laser Tag, one of our most popular activities, during lunchtime Monday – Friday.

$80

### Coach Service One Way

Chartered transportation to/from La-Z-Boy Customer Care Centre (9040 Keele St, Concord, ON L4K 2N2) and Muskoka Woods. Muskoka Woods staff meet families at the pick-up location and accompany guests for the entire bus ride.

$195

### Airport Service One Way

Ground transportation to/from Pearson International Airport (YYZ) and Muskoka Woods. Two Muskoka Woods staff meet your child at the airport and accompany them for the entire drive.

$600

### Yondr Cell Phone Pouch Rental – Mandatory if Bringing a Cell Phone

We strongly encourage guests to leave technology at home so they can fully engage in the camp experience. However, if your child is in Senior High or Crew and you choose to send them with a phone, a Yondr pouch rental is mandatory. The pouch allows guests to safely store their phones and access them for a limited time each day, as outlined on our website. This helps guests stay present while still having the chance to connect when appropriate.

Wild, Venture, and Junior High guests are not permitted to bring phones to camp.

The rental fee of $15 includes a personalized pouch for the week, which will be collected at the end of their stay. If your child is bringing a phone, you must add the Yondr pouch to your registration. If not, no action is required.

$15

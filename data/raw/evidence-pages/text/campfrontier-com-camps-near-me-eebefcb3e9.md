If your are asking are there "camps near me", Camp Frontier is for you. Florida’s OverNight Summer Camp is located just south of Interstate 4 in central Florida.

Our outstanding [facilities](https://www.campfrontier.com/overnight-summer-camp-facilities), located on 12,000 acres, include air-conditioned cabins, many indoor activity spaces, as well as a huge lake, river, swimming pool and a nature preserve.

Camp Frontier is Florida’s OverNight Summer Camp.
With over 40 [activities](https://www.campfrontier.com), Camp Frontier is exclusively an overnight adventure camp for boys and girls, ages 7 to 17.

## Camps Near Me

There is nothing like sleepaway summer camp!

As a residential camp, Camp Frontier is a fantastic experience for a child looking for friends and summer fun.

We offer a [schedule](https://www.campfrontier.com/datesandtuition) of up to 8 weeks of overnight camp. While our programs, [activities](https://www.campfrontier.com), and policies are based upon decades of summer camp tradition, we strive to meet the ever-changing needs and challenges faced by today’s youth in a kind and caring community.

Campers from all over the world and of several religions return each summer. Camp Frontier is rooted in traditional Judeo-Christian values, a love of country, and commitment to honor and respect each other and our God.

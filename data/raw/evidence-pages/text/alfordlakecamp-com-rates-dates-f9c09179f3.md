# The Summer of 2026: Celebrating 120 Years of Friendship & Adventure at ALC!

Alford Lake Camp offers a full season 7-week program and two 3.5 week sessions. Both options provide each camper with time to settle into camp routines; explore and take a deeper dive into those activities on which she chooses to focus; to forge strong, lifelong friendships; and to have the time needed to challenge herself to gain increased confidence and self-awareness.​

## Session I - June 23 - July 16, 2026 Session II - July 19 - August 10, 2026

Which Program is Right for your Camper and Family?

The Half Session

ALC’s 3.5-week option provides families with some flexibility and gives campers the opportunity to benefit from the Alford Lake Camp experience who would otherwise be unable to come. Some Half Session campers eventually choose to be Full Season campers, and some are Half Session for their entire camper career. In three and a half weeks a camper has the time to settle in and adjust to camp life, immerse herself in our choice-based program, and enjoy time with friends and counselors.

The Full Season

Alford Lake Camp’s hallmark 7-week program provides campers with the opportunity to fully immerse themselves in all aspects of camp life and maximize character development. In today's world, the gift of 7 weeks, where a camper spends her time in person with lasting friends and committed counselors, is an opportunity that can instill an improved sense of self-worth and balance.

​

Other Important Details​

SIBLING DISCOUNT: There is a $300 discount for each additional sister enrolled at ALC.

A non-refundable tuition deposit of $1500.00 is required upon registration. This deposit will be credited toward the total tuition.

Tuition for Residential Campers covers all camp activities including Out of Camp Trips and Riding. Also included: optional bedding (sheets, pillows, and blankets); laundry for the season; Camper supplies (e.g. batteries, soap, shampoo, sunscreen, etc.).

Not included is the cost of the required official camp clothing, transportation to and from camp, or lice check/treatment at the beginning of session/season.

Monthly Payment Plan Available

Amount due per month will be determined by enrollment date and the total tuition due.

There will be a 3.5% convenience payment fee if you choose to pay by credit card.

Deposit and credit card fees are non-refundable.

## FINANCIAL AID

It is our desire to develop a diverse community and to share the gift of ALC with campers who otherwise would not be able to attend. For more than fifty years, ALC has provided scholarships that have enhanced the lives of the recipients and the whole camp community.

​

Our Scholarship Committee, made up of alums, past camper parents, and friends of ALC who know first hand the benefits of summers at ALC, work tirelessly throughout the year raising money to assist families hoping to provide an Alford Lake experience for their child.

#### HOW BOOKING WORKS

We are here to make the booking process smooth and easy! Give us a call or email us to discuss your retreat needs, and we will look at calendar availability. We will provide a quote, and then send you a booking agreement (contract) and deposit invoice for 20% of the total event cost. Your signed booking agreement and paid deposit secure your spot on our calendar!

## MLC Founders’ College Scholarship Program

The MLC Founder’s College Scholarship Program proudly selects one or two exceptional students each year who have demonstrated outstanding critical thinking, extensive knowledge, and a passion for STEM. These students are chosen for their potential to create innovations that will positively impact the world. Many recipients start their journey with MLC as campers and continue as valued staff members, further refining their expertise and excelling in their college careers. These distinguished individuals are recognized in STEM fields for their curiosity, work ethic, integrity, leadership, communication skills, and collaborative spirit.

This scholarship celebrates students committed to shaping a brighter future for generations to come. At MLC, we champion immersive education that empowers bright young minds to become pioneers in the sciences. Our scholars actively seek opportunities to make a positive impact, applying scientific principles with confidence and determination to create meaningful change.

Gil & Marilyn Alexander, founders of the Montana Science Institute

*The scholarships are generously supported by Merrilee Alexander Kick, a Capital High School alumna from Helena, Montana, and the entrepreneur behind BuzzBallz, a premium ready-to-drink cocktail brand. This scholarship honors her parents, Gil and Marilyn Alexander, founders of the Montana Science Institute, which evolved into today’s Montana Learning Center. Their passion for science earned them recognition from two U.S. Presidents and the Christa McAuliffe “Teacher in Space Fellowship.” The scholarship also pays tribute to the institute’s educators who played a crucial role in guiding students during the early years. Over 40 years later, the founders’ legacy continues to inspire students to discover their passion for STEM.*

Supporting our future scientists and explorers

To be eligible for the Founders’ Scholarship, applicants must meet the following criteria:

- Be a **high school senior** or a **current undergraduate student** who will be enrolled **full-time** during the **2025–2026 academic year**
- Be a **current or past participant** in a **Montana Learning Center program**
- Have a **minimum cumulative GPA of 3.5 or higher**
- Be available to attend the **Evening Under the Stars** event on **September 13, 2025**, where the scholarship will be awarded

**Please create a video, no longer than 5 minutes, addressing the following points:**

1. Introduce yourself and your hometown.
2. How has the MLC influenced your future plans?
3. Share a memorable experience from the MLC that has stayed with you.
4. How do you plan to utilize this award if awarded?

Please submit this video no later than **August 1st.** Place a link to an unlisted YouTube video or a public access Google Drive video into the following form:

<https://forms.gle/1MDTBAJTiNWQnvNm7>

Recipients of this award will receive a $5,000 check sent directly to their academic institution, to be applied towards tuition and related college expenses. Please note, previous awardees are not eligible to reapply.

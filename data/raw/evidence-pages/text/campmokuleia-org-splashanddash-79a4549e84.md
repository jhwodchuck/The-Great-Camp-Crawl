# Splash & Dash

### October 11, 2026

#### 500m Swim + 5K Run | Dive In. Dash Out. Do Good.

Get ready to make a splash and break a sweat at the Second Annual Splash & Dash — a high-energy 500-meter swim followed by a scenic 5K run, all for a great cause!

#### **What to Expect:**

🏊‍♂️ A refreshing
**500-meter open water swim**
 👟 A fast and fun
**5K run** along Farrington Hwy
 🎉 Post-race celebration, breakfast, and aloha spirit
 🏆 Awards for top finishers in each age group

All proceeds go toward Camp Mokulēʻia, making your effort count both on and off the course.

**Register today** and be part of something special from the very beginning. Let’s make a splash and kick off a new tradition together!

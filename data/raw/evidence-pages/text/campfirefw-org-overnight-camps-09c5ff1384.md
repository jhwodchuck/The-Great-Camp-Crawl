## **Come Find MORE at Camp Fire Camp El Tesoro**

A sharp smell of fresh cut grass, distant laughter and “ker-splash!” at the pool. Ripping open a letter that sings of home, softly talking while playing cards or painstakingly making sure each thread of a friendship bracelet is “just right”- these small moments are the glue that bind a summer at camp. Since 1934, [Camp El Tesoro](https://www.campfirefw.org/about-us/camp-el-tesoro/)‘s overnight camp has inspired youth in all moments, quiet and loud.

Get ready to hike a shaded trail, splash in the creek, sing your heart out around an evening campfire and share time with trusted friends.

El Tesoro overnight camps provide an immersive experience for campers to explore the outdoors, make new friends and awaken their sense of adventure. Intentional moments at camp encourage youth to step out of their comfort zone, try something new and find more:

##### *Connections • Independence • Self-Confidence • Adventure • Laughter*

## **Find MORE Space to Explore**

Camp El Tesoro is located on 223-acres where the Brazos River and Fall Creek meet in Granbury, Texas.

The camp has welcomed five generations of campers for almost 90 years. Campers stay in beautifully modern cabins featuring full restrooms and electricity.

Our highly-trained diverse staff members lead youth through active exploration, building connections and reflection while finding more time to laugh, listen and grow together in a safe and enriching environment.

Plus, in each session, we welcome campers with mild intellectual or developmental disabilities. Our Inclusion Program enriches the camp experience for all campers.

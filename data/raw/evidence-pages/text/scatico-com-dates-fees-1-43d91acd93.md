# DATES & FEES

### Important Dates 2026

**Opening Day-** Saturday, June 27

**Visiting Day**- Saturday, July 18

**4-weeker Departure-** Sunday, July 26

**1-week SIT Program-** Sunday, July 26- Saturday, August 1

**Closing Day-** Friday, August 14

### Tuition 2026

**Entering 8th grade and younger** - $13,900

**Entering 9th grade and older** - $14,400

*Canteen, laundry, Opening and Closing Day transportation, and all offsite trips besides those listed below are included in the cost of tuition.*

### Partial Sessions for First-time Campers

**4-week Program-** $9,750 for first-time campers only

**1-week SIT Program**- $1,200 or campers entering 3rd grade and younger. This tuition can be applied to a full-session enrollment for summer 2027.

### Tuition Discounts

- **$800** – **FULL SESSION early enrollment discount** **for first-time campers** with official 7-week enrollment and refundable deposit by October 1. **This is only available to campers who are at camp for all 7 weeks.**
- **$400** – **PARTIAL SESSION early enrollment discount** for first-time campers with official 4-week enrollment and refundable deposit by October 1.
- **$700**- **FULL SESSION** **early enrollment discount for returning campers** with official 7-week enrollment and refundable deposit by September 1. **This is only available to campers who are at camp for all 7 weeks.**
- **$600**- In addition to the early enrollment discount, there is a $600 sibling discount for when there are **two or more full-session campers** (eg. $600 off for two siblings that are 7-weeks each, or $1,200 for three siblings that are all 7-weeks.) **There is no sibling discount offered for partial session campers.**
- **$1,600- 2025 SIT FULL SESSION CREDIT.** Campers who were an SIT in 2025 and are back as 7-weekers in 2026 are eligible for a $1,600 credit in lieu of other early enrollment discounts.

### Trip Fees + Additional Charges

**$1,500**- Soopers and Upper Seniorstrip to the Dominican Republic

**$1000**- Seniors trip to Lake Placid

### Camperships + Financial Aid

Scatico reserves a portion of its spaces for campers on camperships or who qualify for financial aid. To apply email nicki@scatico.com.

## awaken your spirit

Established in 1921 by the Episcopal Diocese of Texas, Camp Allen is a year-round facility located one hour northwest of Houston near Navasota, Texas. Camp Allen provides scenic facilities for spiritual, leadership, recreational and corporate retreats of all ages, sponsored programs open to the public, outdoor education program for students, and our founding summer camp program.

Our 1,100 beautiful acres features a hotel and conference center, dormitory campsites, family lodges, lakeside log cabins, two lakes, miles of trails, and much more. We invite you to explore, retreat, and awaken your spirit in our piney woods!

# Viola Seminar

Main Content

The Viola Seminar offers Baroque music workshops for violists of all ages and skill
levels. Participants will improve technique, explore polyphony, and engage in masterclasses
and dance forms. The focus is on historical performance practices. Tuition varies,
and partial attendance options are available.

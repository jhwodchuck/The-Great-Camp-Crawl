# Girls belong in our summer family.

Romaca is an all-girls sleep-away camp located in the Berkshire mountains of Massachusetts. Romaca has been in continuous operation since 1916.

Our camper population consists of approximately 250 girls, 6-16 years of age. Campers benefit from watchful supervision and the expertise of approximately 140 dedicated staff members. We help girls throughout their summers at Romaca to become well-rounded, confident young women.

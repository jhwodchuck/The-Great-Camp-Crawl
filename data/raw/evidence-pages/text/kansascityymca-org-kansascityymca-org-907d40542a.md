### EXPLORE THE BENEFITS OF MEMBERSHIP

Ready for a healthier you? Your YMCA is easy to join with a wide range of activities, equipment, facilities, and programs throughout greater Kansas City. Find your Y and explore the benefits of:

- **Y All Access, Always!** Use your membership at any YMCA of Greater KC location: [Explore Locations](/locations)
- **Traveling?** Enjoy nationwide YMCA access: [Learn More](/blog/always-welcome-every-community-nationwide-membership)
- **FREE Classes!** Group exercise classes are included with your membership: [View Schedules](/schedules)
- **Get Started!** FREE Member Orientation—visit or call your local Y: [Learn More](/programs/nutrition-personal-training-weight-loss/member-orientation)
- **Make a Splash!** Swim at any Y indoor pool near you: [Find Pools](/schedules)
- **Kids Zone Fun!** Free childcare for kids 12 weeks-8 years: [Find Kid Zones](/programs/kids-zone)
- **Personal Goals?** Reach them with Y Personal Training: [Check Options](/programs/nutrition-personal-training-weight-loss/personal-training)
- **Discounts Galore!** Save on youth sports, swim, and more—ask at your Y or register online. [Explore Programs](https://www.kansascityymca.org/programs)
- **YMCA360 Online!** Free workouts, anywhere, anytime: [Get Started](/membership/ymca-360-online-wellness)

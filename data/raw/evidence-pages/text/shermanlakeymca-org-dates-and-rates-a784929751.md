# **2026 Dates and Rates**

***\*REGISTRATION TO OPEN NOVEMBER 1, 2025!****\**

Summer is closer than you think! Browse our calendar below to learn more about session dates, availability, and pricing. Please note - our staff updates this list daily but it is NOT connected directly to our registration system. Status differences may occur.

**Waitlists**

If a session is waitlisted, it is currently full. If you wish to join our waitlist, please fill out our registration form. Deposits are NOT required to join the waitlist. We highly recommend joining the waitlist, as we are often making changes for campers. Please do not call the office to ask if space has opened up - we promise you’ll be notified via email and/or phone as availability occurs! Please note, that while we do our best to make space for everyone, we can’t guarantee spots will become available for campers on the waitlist.

**Cancellation Policy**

Please notify us as soon as possible if you decide to cancel your camper’s enrollment in any session. Our Camp Office can be reached at [269.731.3030](tel:+12697313030) or by email at [logistics@ymcasl.org](mailto:logistics@ymcasl.org). Please [click here](/parents) to learn more about our cancellation policies.

### **Camp Scholarships**

It is our belief that no child shall be turned away due to an inability to pay.  Each year, we raise funds to help parents send their kids to camp.  If the camp fees are just not possible for you right now, we urge you to apply for a camp scholarship.  Please feel free to call us at [269.731.3030](tel:+12697313030) or [click here](https://zach-klipsch.squarespace.com/financial-assistance) for more information.

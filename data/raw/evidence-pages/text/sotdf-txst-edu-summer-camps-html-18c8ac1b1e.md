## NEXUS Musical Theatre Intensive

## June 15-25, 2026

### Applications for summer 2026 are now closed.

The NEXUS Pre-College Intensive is a residential summer camp designed to train not only the artist, but the person. Alongside a community of artists who were all working together to create the future of our art form, utilizing innovative training methods and guided by Texas State’s nationally renowned faculty in our state-of-the-art facilities.

## Bobcat Theatre Camp

### July 6-11 2026 (Grades 2-8) & July 12-17 2026 (Grades 9-12)

We are now offering two sessions of this week-long day camp. Session I will be designed for elementary and middle school students, and Session II for High School students. Both camps offer participants the opportunity to engage in a variety of acting and technical theatre workshops, allowing them to develop a wide range of theatre skills.

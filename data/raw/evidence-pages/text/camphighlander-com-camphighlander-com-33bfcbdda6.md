## Building boys and girls of courage, honesty, integrity and faith

Camp Highlander is a residential summer camp in Mills River, North Carolina, devoted to the growing and nurturing of boys and girls, ages 6-16, in a safe, fun-filled, and [electronics-free](https://www.camphighlander.com/parents/during-camp/partnering-with-parents) environment. Since 1957, we have partnered with parents and families to build character and confidence in the lives of children as we provide lifelong memories, authentic friendships, and a camp family to come home to year after year.

## Register today for Summer 2026!

We offer an **8 day session** for new campers, **2 week camp sessions** (our most popular), and one **4 week session**. Learn more about [session dates and rates](/sessions).

# Summer Camps and Programs

![Two students look at flowers at the CSU Trial Gardens](https://admissions.colostate.edu/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/flower-garden-hero1.jpg)

Summer is the perfect time for high school students to dive into new interests and explore future possibilities.

At Colorado State University, we offer a wide variety of summer camps designed to spark curiosity, build skills, and inspire confidence — all while experiencing life on our beautiful campuses in Fort Collins or Denver.

Browse the opportunities below to find the program that’s right for you or your student! Each camp offers something unique. Some are day programs, while others include overnight stays. While a few are free, others may require a fee or a short application process. All are held in Fort Collins unless otherwise noted.

No matter which camp you choose, you’ll gain hands-on experience, connect with like-minded peers, and grow in ways that will prepare you for your next big adventure. Add the breathtaking summer scenery of Colorado, and it’s an unforgettable experience waiting for you!

### Drone Aviation Program

**June 22 - 26, July 6 - 10, July 20 -24**
 **For:**  Denver-area high school students
 **Format:**  Weeklong day program
 **Location:**  CSU Spur in Denver
Cost: $199 per student
 **Application:**  Due May 1
 **Benefits:**  Immersive 40-hour Drone Flight School seminar for Denver-area high school students and instructors.
You will participate in classwork and hands-on flight operations and attend several aviation-related events. Afterward, you can take your FAA Remote Pilot exam and be credentialed as a commercial UAV pilot.

[DRONE AVIATION PROGRAM](https://connect.colostate.edu/register/?id=d232181b-432f-4e51-b604-5c5f21786bf4)

### GLOBAL BUSINESS ACADEMY

**July 12 - 22, 2026**
 **For:**  Students entering grades 9-12
 **Format:**  Days plus overnights in the university dorms
 **Location:**  CSU Fort Collins
Cost: $3,750 per student, scholarships available
 **Application:**  The early application deadline is February 1 and the final application deadline is May 1.
 **Benefits:**  This program shows students how they can use business to create a better world. Students build global leadership and international business skills through hands-on projects and experiences.

[GLOBAL BUSINESS ACADEMY](https://biz.colostate.edu/academics/high-school-programs/global-business-academy)

### FIRST-GENERATION BUSINESS SUMMIT

**July 20 - 25, 2026**
 **For:**  Students entering grades 10-12 or incoming college freshmen
 **Format:**  Days plus overnights in the university dorms
 **Location:**  CSU Fort Collins
Cost: Free
 **Application:**  Due April 21
 **Benefits:**  This free, immersive, week-long experience is designed to help you build the skills, confidence, and connections to envision your future in business, in college, and beyond.

[FIRST-GENERATION BUSINESS SUMMIT](https://connect.colostate.edu/register/?id=5ac03378-a2cb-4b05-9f17-7d93a8d9525b)

Echo Hill Camp on the Chesapeake Bay is a traditional sleep-away camp for 145 boys and girls ages 7-16. Our program is very flexible and is designed to match the natural, free and non-competitive atmosphere at Echo Hill. Daytime activity is organized on a free-choice basis. Instruction for all activities includes that for beginners and all campers are encouraged to try everything!

## Engaging youth and families with nature since 1934

Camp Fire Camp El Tesoro is a 223-acre multi-use camp established in 1934 and accredited by the American Camp Association. Located in Granbury, Texas, on the bend of the Brazos River at Fall Creek, Camp El Tesoro is equipped with two swimming pools, a covered equestrian center, a challenge course with both high and low elements and much more.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns%3D%27http%3A%2F%2Fwww.w3.org%2F2000%2Fsvg%27%20width%3D%27122%27%20height%3D%27103%27%20viewBox%3D%270%200%20122%20103%27%3E%3Crect%20width%3D%27122%27%20height%3D%27103%27%20fill-opacity%3D%220%22%2F%3E%3C%2Fsvg%3E)

Camp El Tesoro has grown over the years and now includes a variety of year round opportunities such as [family camping](/camps/camping-weekends/), spring break events, [group retreats](/camps/group-rentals/) and [outdoor education](/after-school/texas-outdoor-education-center/) for students. During the summer El Tesoro offers traditional [overnight camp](/camps/overnight-camps/), [day camp](/camps/day-camps/) and a [camp for grieving children.](/camps/grief-camp/)

Camp Fire First Texas embarked on a Capital Campaign in 2010 to renovate and upgrade Camp El Tesoro facilities so that future generations can experience nature through camping.

Camp El Tesoro is located at 7710 Fall Creek Highway, Granbury, Texas 76049.

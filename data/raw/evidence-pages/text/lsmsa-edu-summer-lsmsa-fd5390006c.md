## Registration for Summer @LSMSA is now open

## Camp Information

"Summer @LSMSA" allows students entering grades 7, 8, and 9 the opportunity to experience life at LSMSA for one week. All campers take three courses and spend six hours a day learning in a fun, hands-on atmosphere; there are no grades or tests in any courses - just the experiences!

In the evenings, overnight campers will have a variety of activities to choose from that are organized and monitored by professional LSMSA residential life staff. Overnight campers stay in the residence halls.

All campers have the chance to get a feel for the LSMSA experience in a fun and comfortable environment.

For the Summer of 2026, camp sessions are May 31-June 5 (Session 1); June 7-12 (Session 2); and June 14-19 (Session 3).

Registration is limited to one week (Session 1, 2, or 3) per camper.

**Registration is online. The link to online registration is the blue button that says "Camp Registration"**

### List of 6 items.

- #### [Check In & Check Out](#)

  Overnight campers can check in and move in to the residence hall on Sunday afternoon. Move out is Friday afternoon.

  Day campers can check in on Monday morning and check out on Friday afternoon.
- #### [Typical Summer Day Schedule](#)

  **All times are approximate**Overnight Camp
  7:30 AM - Wake up and go to breakfast
  8:45 - 10:30 - Period 1
  10:45 - 12:30 - Period 2
  12:30 - 1:20 - Lunch is served in the LSMSA Cafeteria.
  1:30 - 3:15 - Period 3
  3:30 - 5:30 PM - Choice of activities around campus and throughout Natchitoches.
  5:30 - 6:30 PM - Dinner
  6:30 - 8:00 PM - Choice activities for campers on campus and throughout Natchitoches.
  9:00 PM - Curfew
  10:00 PM - Lights out.

  Day Campers
  8:30 - Arrive on campus
  8:45 - 10:30 - Period 1
  10:45 - 12:30 - Period 2
  12:30 - 1:20 - Lunch is served in the LSMSA Cafeteria.
  1:30 - 3:15 - Period 3
  3:30 - 5:00 PM - Can choose to depart or participate in activities around campus
- #### [Course Offerings](#)

  Campers take 3 classes daily for a week.  Note that some classes have limited availability.  See "Camp Course Offerings" page for more info.
- #### [Meals](#)

  The dining hall will be open each day during the week for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.  Overnight campers will also receive dinner on Sunday evening.
  Day campers receive lunch only.

  The LSMSA dining hall offers a variety of options including vegetarian and vegan meals. Please list any additional dietary restrictions on the registration form.
- #### [Roommates](#)

  Students have the opportunity to request specific roommates and suitemates. If they do not wish to do so, our residential life staff will pair them with other summer campers.
- #### [Costs](#)

  This year's fees are:
  $450 for overnight campers and includes all meals and activities

  $350 for day campers and includes lunch and optional (post-class) afternoon activities

**JOIN OUR SUMMER TEAM!**

Every spring, we are looking for energetic young adults who enjoy working with inquiring young minds.  Our Camp Counselors lead fun activities, supervise mealtimes and bedtimes, and help keep our campers safe and happy.
We are hiring now! More information about the job is posted on our [Employment page.](https://www.lsmsa.edu/about-us/employment "Working at LSMSA")  If you have questions, please email summer@LSMSA.edu.

Louisiana School for Math, Science, and the Arts (LSMSA) is the preeminent state-supported residential high school with competitive admissions for Louisiana's high-achieving, highly-motivated sophomores, juniors, and seniors. As a public school, there is no tuition to attend, and assistance from the LSMSA Foundation ensures the program is available to all qualifying students regardless of family financial need.

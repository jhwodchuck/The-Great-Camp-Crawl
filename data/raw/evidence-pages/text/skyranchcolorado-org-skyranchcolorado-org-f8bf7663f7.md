## Encounter friends, community and Christ on the mountain top.﻿

Located west of Fort Collins, Sky Ranch Lutheran Camp is surrounded by the Roosevelt National Forest that encompasses more than 800,000 acres in north-central Colorado. It's here that we hope our campers encounter Christ from the mountain top to the blacktop and build relationships that last a lifetime.

## Experience summer camps, retreats, and outdoor education in the Rocky Mountains

At Sky Ranch, we welcome each of you to experience the great outdoors in a setting that is off the beaten path and unplugged. From week-long youth summer camps, to church retreats, and to outdoor ed, our camp is perfectly situated in the beautiful Colorado mountains outside of Fort Collins.

 Sky Ranch offers a variety of Christ-centered outdoor ministry experiences for people of all ages. Our caring and dedicated staff share God’s unending grace in spontaneous and powerful was—acting as compassionate role models for the campers in their care. Join us at Sky Ranch—where we Facilitate an Encounter with Christ on the Mountaintops. Guests experience the beauty of creation and embrace the simplicity and joy of life in community, seeking to live the Gospel through the rhythms of camp. Sky Ranch staff strive to facilitate meaningful and memorable camp experiences—all with the goal of equipping campers to live out the Gospel down the mountain—wherever they might go.

### GETTING HERE

- From Fort Collins, drive North on US-287
- Turn left onto Highway 14 (Poudre Canyon). You will see a Loaf 'N Jug gas station.
  This is your last place for gas and cell service!
- After following the Poudre Canyon Road for 26 miles, turn left onto County Road 63E (also known as the Pingree Park Road).
- Stay on the gravel road for 16 miles.
- Turn right onto Forest Road 145. You will see signs for Tom Bennett campground and Sky Ranch
- If you reach the CSU Mountain Campus, you've gone 1/4 mile too far and missed the turn onto Forest Road 145
- After 2 miles, arrive at Sky Ranch!
  The Bellevue, CO address won't pull up anywhere close.  Load this map up on your phone before you leave Fort Collins.

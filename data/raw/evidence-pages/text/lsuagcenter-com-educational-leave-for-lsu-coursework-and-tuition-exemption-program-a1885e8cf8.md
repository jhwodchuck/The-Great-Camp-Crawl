# Tuition Exemption Program and Educational Leave for LSU Coursework

[Ashley Gautreaux](/profiles/agautreaux)

[LSU AgCenter](/tags/LSU AgCenter), [All](/tags/All), [General](/tags/General)

## Educational Leave with Tuition Exemption

Eligible employees may register for job-related courses for up to six college credit hours per regular semester (three credit hours allowed per summer semester) and receive tuition exemptions. *(Note: the value of the tuition waivers may be considered taxable income to the employee in accordance with Internal Revenue Service regulations.)* An employee must meet the following criteria to be eligible for this exemption:

- employed full-time for at least one year,
- must be a full-time employee (includes faculty and faculty equivalent ranks, nonacademic and other academic employees),
- the college credit course(s) must be *job-related* and approved by the immediate supervisor and unit head; **and**
- only three hours per week of job-related courses may be taken during work time without charge to personal paid leave.
- Continued participation in this program is based on satisfactory academic progress.

## Educational Leave Without Tuition Exemption

During the first year of employment, eligible employees may register, ***at their own expense***, for up to six college credit hours per regular semester (three credit hours per summer semester). An employee must meet the following criteria to be eligible:

- must be a full-time employee (includes faculty and faculty equivalent ranks, nonacademic and other academic employees),
- the college credit course(s) must be *job-related* and approved by the immediate supervisor and unit head; **and**
- only three hours per week of job-related courses may be taken during work time without charge to personal paid leave.
- Continued participation is based on satisfactory academic progress.

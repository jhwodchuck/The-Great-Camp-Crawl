Each

of our sessions are jam packed with exciting adventures in the Florida Keys. We offer 4 residential camp sessions and weekly daycamp sessions.

Returning campers are recommended to participate in our two-week sessions to gain the most from their continuing experience.

Additional fees for SCUBA, Lifeguarding, and transportation options. Payment plans available.

Scholarships are available. Download the form and instructions [here](/docs/2026/Seacamp Campership Application 2026.pdf).

**Welcome to Oklahoma City University!** You can find us in the heart of OKC – a true city on the rise!

We're a national leader in education for performing arts, health care, business, science and leadership. At OCU, we strive for **excellence** while celebrating **individuality**. On this campus, you're part of a family where **everyone is welcome**. In OKC, you're part of a vibrant community where options for culture, dining, entertainment and fun abound.

Spend some time on our campus, in our city, and you'll know, **YOU HAVE TO BE HERE!**

## Build Character. Gain Independence. Learn Truth.

Camp at New Life Ranch is about so much more than having fun.

Trying New Things

### Character Development

Camp is one of the best ways kids can develop character! Kids get to step outside of their comfort zones and learn to navigate relationships, try new things, and learn resilience.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68532596df1b9743f367602e/6894cf2965481235703b848b_dsc08266_bd5299eaa144456bbdcf4023694b2497.jpg)

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68532596df1b9743f367602e/68545bd96a14371bde964ef3_IMG_4413.jpg)

On Their Own

### Independence

For many kids, this is their first experience away from their parents for an extended period of time. Camp gives them the opportunity to practice independence in a safe and fun environment under the watchful care of highly trained summer staff!

Hearing the Gospel

### Learning Truth

Kids now have access to more information than any other generation before them. With that access comes a lot of competition for their attention and beliefs. At New Life Ranch, we are singularly focused on making sure that while campers are here, **they are absolutely 100% guaranteed to hear the truth of the gospel of Jesus Christ.**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/68532596df1b9743f367602e/6894cf77192a68c28617b8c5_dsc09384_5b2a4ce71d6f490782b31dfb9ad94b35.jpg)

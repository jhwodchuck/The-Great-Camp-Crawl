# Florida Summer Camp & Outdoor Education Programs

#### We Foster Grit, Growth, and a love for the great outdoors.

Circle F Dude Ranch was founded in 1952 as a traditional sleepaway summer camp for girls and boys ages 6 to 16. With the perfect blend of Western flair and classic camp fun, we give kids the kind of summer they’ll never forget.

#### Activities

**Horseback Riding, High Ropes & Climbing, Swimming & Boating, Arts, Paintball & Extreme Adventures, Individual & Team Sports, Theme Days, Free Play, & Evening Programs**

###### Our mission is to provide opportunities for the emotional, physical, and social growth of our campers, guests, and staff.

We do this by creating a supportive environment where everyone feels valued, safe, and at home. Our [one-of-a-kind program](/dude-ranch-summer-camp-activities) encourages campers to try new things, make new friends, and grow into confident, kind, and remarkable individuals.

#### Nestled in Lake Wales, Florida an Hour South of Orlando

We are situated on [500 acres](/about-circle-f/the-ranch) of a southern live oak forest with a spring-fed [private lake](/about-circle-f/the-ranch) at the center of it all. As the leading Florida summer camp and private group venue, we offer a unique backdrop for unforgettable camp experiences.

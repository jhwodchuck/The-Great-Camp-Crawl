# Parent Portal

**Need help navigating our website?  We’ve made a parent portal to make your search more efficient!**

**[*UltraCamp*](https://www.ultracamp.com/clientlogin.aspx?idCamp=274&campCode=swn):** Login to your UltraCamp account to make a reservation, check your balances, email your camper, add trading post funds, request a cabin mate, and find other information.

***[Camp Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/campshaw)*:** Photos of your camper can be found here during each camp week.

**[*Camper Activities*](https://campshaw.org/summer-camp/parent-info/2/):** Read more about what your camper may get to experience at camp during their time here.

**[*Contact Page*](https://campshaw.org/contact/):** Our address, contact information, and general directions to arrive at camp.

**[*Emailing Campers*](https://campshaw.org/emailing-campers/):** Navigate sending emails to your camper through the week.

**[*Forms & Paperwork*](https://campshaw.org/forms-paperwork/):** Fill out all necessary forms before arriving at camp. Permission slips and Code of Conduct are filled out online as part of your registration. The Camper Health History, including immunizations, should be brought to check-in with your camper, as well as the Allergy and Asthma action plans (only if needed for your camper).

**[*Health and Medical Needs*](https://campshaw.org/parentguardian-information/camper-health-medical-needs/):** Health, immunizations, dietary needs, and more.

***[Parent/Guardian Information](https://campshaw.org/parentguardian-information/)*:** Some things you should know before sending your camper to camp for the week.

**[*Registration Page*](https://campshaw.org/registration/):** Prepare to register your camper for a fun week at Camp Shaw!

***[Summer Camp Page](https://campshaw.org/summer-camp/parent-info/)*:** General information about our sessions, such as prices, dates, etc.

- [Try Camp](http://campshaw.org/summer-camp/try-camp/)
- [Youth Camp](http://campshaw.org/summer-camp/youth-camp/)
- [Extreme Camp](http://campshaw.org/summer-camp/extreme-camp/)
- [Outdoor Adventure Camp](https://campshaw.org/summer-camp/outdoor-adventure-camp-ages-7-17/)
- [CIT Program](http://campshaw.org/summer-camp/cit/)
- [Family Camp](http://campshaw.org/family-camp/) Weekend

*For any other questions about our summer camp programs, you can contact our [Office Manager](mailto:office@campshaw.org)!*

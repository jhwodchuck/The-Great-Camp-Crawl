- 01

  Generous grants for NEW campers are being offered by the Foundation for Jewish Camp and partnering Jewish Federations.

  Visit [www.OneHappyCamper.org](https://www.jewishcamp.org/one-happy-camper/) to determine eligibility and apply - enter "Habonim Dror Camp Moshava" for the camp you want to attend.

  **These grants are NOT need-based. However, they are first-come-first-served, so apply now!**

  Synagogues and temples often offer scholarships for members to attend Jewish overnight summer camp. Ask your congregation's leadership if they are able to provide assistance for your family.
- 03

  If you register two or more children, you will receive a $100 discount on the 2nd and 3rd (or more) campers. We will automatically apply this discount to your account.
- 04

  Families who love Camp Mosh and talk about it with others are the best asset we have in recruiting new campers. Because you‚Äôve entrusted the care of your children to Mosh, you‚Äôre making a strong statement to others who are considering doing the same.

  A $400 discount is added for the first new registered camper you recruit, $750 for two new campers, and $1,000 for three new campers.

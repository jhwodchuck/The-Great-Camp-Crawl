# Summer Camp Dates and Rates

# For information about 2026 please contact our summer camp registrar Nicole Conner by calling 800-460-4401 ext. 234

Our one and two-week sessions are action-packed and offer a wide variety of activities. They offer a little bit of everything! They are a perfect opportunity to make new friends, try new things and discover what God has in store for you. Campers rotate through a schedule that allows them to experience activities from each of our program areas.

# **2026 Rates**

**One Week Sessions: $920**

**Two Week Sessions: $1850**

### Discounts

**Sibling discounts!** We offer a 5% sibling discount after one camper has registered at the full price! No promo code is needed this year! The discount will automatically be applied during online registration.

**Military discounts!** We offer a 10% active military discount. Please use promo code “momilitary” during registration.

**Refer a Mo-Ranch newbie to camp and save big on camp!**

Summer camp is better with friends. That’s why Mo-Ranch’s “Refer a Mo-Ranch newbie” discount makes it easy to bring your friends to camp this summer!

Referring families will receive a $100 discount off of their session rate for each new-to-camp family that attends Mo-Ranch Summer Camp. This offer is exclusive to our residential campers — this discount will only be available for a short time due to limited space for our 2026 summer sessions. So enroll by April 18 to receive this special offer!

New families must notate which family referred them on their application. Referral credits are applied once the referred family has signed up and deposits are processed. Please contact our summer camp office at 800.460.4401 ext. 234 for any questions regarding this limited promotion.

**Scholarships**

Every child deserves an opportunity to experience summer camp.  If you are concerned about a financial burden, please reach out to naconner@moranch.com so we can work to find a way for your camper to attend. The deadline to apply for a scholarship is April, 2026.

# Summer Sleepaway Camps

## Pine Cove Overnight Camps

Campers at Pine Cove have the most memorable week of their summer at our 2nd-12th grade camps in East Texas, Central Texas or Northeast Georgia! An accredited organization with more than five decades of experience, Pine Cove offers adventurous activities, solid Christian role models, and a safe, Gospel-focused environment that’s perfect for first-time (or eleventh-time!) campers. Whether they attend for one week or two, elementary, middle, and high school campers are sure to have an unforgettable experience at our Christian sleepaway camps!

[More Serious Fun](#)


## Overnight Camp Details

Kids thrive at Pine Cove! Not only do they get a chance to laugh at hilarious skits and let loose with new friends, but they'll spend time with caring and well-trained counselors, grow their confidence by trying new things, and learn what it looks like to have a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. From activities like swimming pools and zip lines to seriously fun theme nights and high-energy worship sessions, a week at Pine Cove is packed full of meaningful experiences that campers will remember forever!

## FAQs

## Overnight Camp FAQs

**What are the core values of Pine Cove?**

Pine Cove is Christ-centered, others-focused, and seriously fun! Read more about our core values [here](https://www.pinecove.com/about-us/core-values/).

**What religion is Pine Cove?**

Pine Cove is a non-denominational, Gospel-based Christian ministry. We welcome campers from a wide variety of faith backgrounds. Our [statement of faith](https://www.pinecove.com/about-us/beliefs/) summarizes what we believe.

**How do I know if my child is ready for a week away at overnight camp?**

Such a great—and tough—question! As you know, kids grow and mature at different rates, hitting various milestones at different times. With that in mind, we put together a guide to help you navigate this question! From whether your child can handle large groups and loud noises to their ability to follow instructions, there are a lot of markers to look for as you determine if your child is ready for camp. Check out [7 Signs Your Child is Ready to Thrive at Camp](https://www.pinecove.com/blog/7-signs-your-child-is-ready-to-thrive-at-camp/) for more information.

**Can my child be in the same cabin with someone else?**

Absolutely! We offer the option to request that your camper be in the same group as another specific friend or up to three friends (for a group total of four). We do not allow more than four campers to request to be together in a group so that we can make sure everyone in the cabin is included, and no one feels left out. You can manage friend groups [in your registration account](https://my.pinecove.com/camper/dashboard#friend-groups). Read more on our blog about [overnight youth camp friend requests](https://www.pinecove.com/blog/the-in-depth-guide-to-cabin-mate-requests-at-overnight-youth-camp/) and [day camp friend requests](https://www.pinecove.com/blog/the-in-depth-guide-to-friend-groups-at-day-camp/).

[Create or Manage Group](https://my.pinecove.com/camper/dashboard#friend-groups)

**What is the schedule of camp?**

We don’t have an online schedule for you to see, as different camps (and even different days) have a tendency to vary a bit. But here’s an idea of the flow of a typical day at youth camp: Flag and quiet time before breakfast, followed by Bible study and a few activities. Then it’s time for lunch, FOB (rest time), and more activities! After some free time, it’s dinner, that evening’s theme night, a snack, Club, and then bedtime. We make sure campers get about 7-8 hours of sleep each night so they’re ready for another full day of camp in the morning!

Check out our blog post for more details on a day that’s anything but typical.

[A "Typical" Day](https://www.pinecove.com/blog/self-what-does-a-typical-day-at-camp-look-like/)

**Are there discounts available for overnight youth camp?**

Yes! All new-to-overnight youth campers can get $250 off a week of camp by using the code on [our discounts page](https://www.pinecove.com/tools/discounts/). That page includes other ways to save on camp!

[More Discount Info](https://www.pinecove.com/tools/discounts/)

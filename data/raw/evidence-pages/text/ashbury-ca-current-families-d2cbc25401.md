## Documentation Requests

Ashbury College provides three kinds of documentation:

Letter of Acceptance: used for student study permit and visa applications.
Note: This document will be provided upon receipt of the first instalment of tuition fees.

Proof of Enrollment: a document supporting provincial health card and driver’s license applications, applications to financial institutions, and other official identification.

Letter of Invitation, Parental: a letter supporting parental applications for a visitor visa.

For further details for each document and to submit a request, please complete the form below. Requests may take up to five business days.

[Documentation Request Form](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=GsyEE65Mpk2EhQU50H5Dz1dCNXWnETVBh-1lhuhaEk5UNUNYREFCSVRLUzY1RlA0RVFCN0ZFWENYUC4u&web=1&wdLOR=c82A92B34-D5C2-45B0-97B5-765C98915850)

**[Johns Hopkins University's Engineering Innovation Pre-College Programs](https://ei.jhu.edu/?utm_source=constantcontact&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=ei_su25_rfi)** offer high school students like you the chance to explore your passion for math, science, and technology while experiencing college life. Whether you choose to live on campus, commute from home, or participate online, these programs are designed to help you see your future as an engineer.

### Program Options:

1. **Explore Engineering Innovation In-Person**
   Dive into various engineering fields such as chemical, civil, electrical, mechanical, and materials science through hands-on experiences on the Hopkins campus.
2. **Sustainable Energy Engineering In-Person**
   A new program focusing on sustainable energy engineering, held at the Johns Hopkins Homewood campus, perfect for students interested in renewable energy solutions.
3. **Explore Engineering Innovation Online**
   Participate in a largely synchronous online program exploring different engineering disciplines from the comfort of your home.
4. **Biomedical Engineering Innovation Online**
   Learn how to solve real-world medical and biological problems with this mostly asynchronous course, including design review meetings and optional virtual study sessions.

### Program Formats:

- **In-Person Residential:** Experience a four-week immersive program living like a college student on the Hopkins campus.
- **In-Person Commuter:** Attend classes on campus during the day and return home in the evening for the perfect mix of college life and home comfort.
- **Online:** Connect with students worldwide while exploring engineering innovation through virtual learning.

Don't miss this opportunity to explore your future in engineering with Johns Hopkins! For more information and to apply, **[check out their website](https://ei.jhu.edu/?utm_source=constantcontact&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=ei_su25_rfi)**.

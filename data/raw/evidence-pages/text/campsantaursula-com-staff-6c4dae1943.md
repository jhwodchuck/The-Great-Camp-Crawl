## Requirements

- Be between 16 and 20 years old
- To have been a camper in Santa Ursula, or to be recommended by someone who has previously participated with us.
- Know how to swim and ride a bike very well.
- Don't be afraid of heights.
- Love sports.
- To be super lively and enthusiastic.
- Have leadership skills.
- Enjoy working as a team and leading groups of girls and boys.
- Proyectar una imagen personal cuidada y apropiada, siendo un ejemplo positivo para los niños y sus familias, es decir, mantener una buena imagen personal y actitud ejemplar, sabiendo que eres un modelo a seguir para los niños durante toda la experiencia del campamento

## Staff

Join the team of summer adventurers at Camp Santa Úrsula!

In our camp there are different categories in the staff team and each applicant falls into the corresponding one according to their study.

### Auxiliary### Instructor### Coordinator

#### Auxiliary

The primary purpose of an assistant is to support and assist the instructor in planning and executing recreational and educational activities during the camp, ensuring a safe, fun and educational environment for all campers.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/6619777b5e3146320874dd12_(2023-07-15%2010-45-42)NIKON%20CORPORATION%20NIKON%20D7000%20(4928x3264)_Edit.webp)

#### Instructor

The purpose of an instructor is to lead, facilitate and enrich the participants' experience during their time in the camp. This role involves performing various functions with the objective of providing an educational, safe and fun environment. Create an environment where all participants feel valued, encouraging teamwork and collaboration.

It will act as a role model to inspire participants to follow a positive example. It will promote appreciation and respect for nature and the environment.

It will ensure a safe environment for participants, applying safety protocols and supervising activities. And finally, it will contribute to the creation of meaningful experiences and lasting memories for the participants, making their time at the camp unforgettable.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/661978f23f3e1024a44e00e2_P2250588.webp)

#### Specialized Coordinator

The purpose of a Block Coordinator is to ensure the efficient and successful operation of camp operations. This role is essential to ensure that the camp meets its objectives, providing a safe, educational and enriching experience for all participants. You will monitor and coordinate the activities of your block. It will help to plan the themes. You will monitor, coordinate and evaluate your staff in charge. It will enforce security policies and procedures.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/661979a5e7aaac33c937ddb4_CAPA1.webp)

## Selection process

To start your recruitment process, there are 3 steps to follow

# 01

### Forms

You will receive a document that will require relevant information about you, in addition to assigning you a specific mission.

# 02

### Interview and Monologue

You must show up for the interview at our camp. If you are not in San Luis Potosí, we will schedule an interview via Zoom.

# 03

### Group test

You will take a group test in person at our camp, where you will participate in activities that will evaluate your physical and mental abilities.

## Training

Before participating in the in-person training, we invite you to take part in a preliminary virtual session. By reaching this stage, you have successfully passed all of our selection criteria.

It is essential that you reserve availability for the designated four days, since training is a fundamental component of your preparation. If, for some reason, you are unable to attend on these dates, we recommend that you consider delaying your participation until next year.

During these four days of training, you will have the opportunity to immerse yourself in CSU in a practical way, in addition to receiving specialized tools that will empower you to perform your role as a member of the staff, thus contributing to the creation of memorable experiences.

## **Benefits of being part of the Staff at Camp Santa Ursula**

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/66197f6daea983f3aceae7d6_PHOTO-2024-01-06-11-42-39.webp)

### Leadership

#### Being a camp instructor offers a rewarding and unique experience with several benefits, highlighting strong leadership development.

This role allows us to cultivate leadership skills by supervising and motivating participants, fostering a positive learning environment. The ability to make informed decisions and manage diverse situations is developed, strengthening resilience and adaptability. Daily interaction with campers promotes effective communication skills and empathy, essential for collaborative leadership. In addition, the planning and execution of activities provides opportunities for creativity and problem solving. Being a camp instructor not only promotes personal growth, but it also provides the satisfaction of contributing to the integral development of participants, establishing the basis for strong and compassionate leadership in diverse contexts.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/6615cf8d253aa876d8e27c9a_IMG_1869.webp)

### teamwork

#### The role of camp instructor stands out as an enriching opportunity for the significant development of teamwork skills.

Close collaboration with colleagues and coordination of activities for a diverse group of campers cultivate the ability to work harmoniously in a dynamic environment. The capacity to contribute effectively to a team is strengthened, encouraging the joint resolution of challenges. Sharing common responsibilities and goals encourages building strong relationships and mutual understanding, creating a supportive environment. Being a camp instructor not only involves leading, but also being an active member of a team, promoting a culture of respect, trust and cooperation, essential skills for success in any work environment.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/6615cf8df6d1a439a38c7549_PRUEBA%20GRUPAL.webp)

### trust

#### As a camp instructor, you experience a significant development in personal and professional confidence.

The responsibility to guide campers and lead challenging activities nurtures confidence in one's abilities and decision-making. Ongoing interaction with colleagues and managing diverse situations builds strong confidence in communication and problem solving skills. Mutual support within the team creates an environment where trust in others is essential, promoting strong working relationships. The direct impact on the lives of campers reinforces confidence in their ability to inspire and lead positively. Being a camp instructor ultimately nurtures comprehensive confidence, establishing a solid foundation to face challenges with determination and confidence in any scenario.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/65da780ea7d31fb5fa88c9c3/6615cf8d28481f214d7f1749_NIK_4381.webp)

### communication

#### Being a camp instructor provides a unique platform for developing exceptional communication skills.

Daily interaction with campers and colleagues encourages effective and adaptive communication, essential for success in a variety of fields. The ability to express ideas clearly and concisely is strengthened by guiding activities, facilitating discussions, and providing constructive feedback. In addition, you learn to listen actively, understanding individual needs and fostering an inclusive environment. The diversity of challenging situations enhances the ability to communicate persuasively and resolve conflicts diplomatically. Being a camp instructor will not only refine communication skills, but will also instill the importance of empathy and interpersonal connection, essential skills in any communication context.

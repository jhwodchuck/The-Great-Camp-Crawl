# Darer

Miniwanca

Boys Camp
Girls Camp

Miniwanca’s youngest campers enjoy a wide range of activities in an awe-inspiring setting. Their schedules are a balance of opportunities campers choose for themselves, engage in as a cabin group, or participate in as an all-camp community. Days of laughter and music create lifelong memories as campers challenge themselves and make new connections.

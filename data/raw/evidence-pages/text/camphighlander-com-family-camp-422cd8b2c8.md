*\*Plus $350 for each additional son/daughter.
\*\*$675 for children ages 5-12, children 4 or younger are free*

Family Session Payments & Cancellations - Applications must be submitted with a one-third tuition deposit by wire transfer, echeck or credit card by October 15. This deposit will be deducted from the camp tuition. The Parent/Guardian agrees to pay the remaining two-thirds balance in two payments on January 15 and March 15. Deposits are refundable, less a $300 administrative fee, with written notice until January 15. There are no refunds for deposits after that date. Tuition payments, minus the deposit, are refundable if written notice is received prior to March 15. There is no refund made for cancellations after March 15. Applicants after March 15 must pay the full tuition upon application. All cancellations must be in writing to the camp office. Parent/Guardian understands there will be NO REFUND for cancellations, late arrivals or early departure from camp, including departure due to injury, illness or disciplinary dismissal of Participant.

## Educators Appreciation Tuition

Brant Lake Camp has a four generation history where every owner/director has roots as educators. In honor and appreciation of all that educators do for children, we proudly offer a substantial tuition reduction.  Please inquire by contacting us at [office@brantlake.com](mailto:office@brantlake.com).

For accepted families where the higher income of parents is an educator.

## Transportation

Round trip buses to/from the NYC metropolitan area are provided for an additional fee.
 Campers can be driven to camp or can fly to Albany, NY or NYC and be picked up.

We also offer chaperoned flights from Miami and Los Angeles.

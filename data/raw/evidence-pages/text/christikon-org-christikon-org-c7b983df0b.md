### Greetings, Christikon Community!

[![](https://christikon.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/boost-banner@2x.png)](www.christikon.org/housingboost)

As Christikon prepares for our upcoming season of ministry—expanding programs, extending retreats, and celebrating our 75th Anniversary in 2026—we’re excited for new possibilities! We’re inviting you to help shape what comes next.

Welcome to our Spring Housing Boost! Launching March 1st, this is more than a project—it’s a chance to widen the circle of hospitality. As we prepare for a season of deeper community and broader welcome, we’re strengthening and expanding our seasonal housing so every camper, staff member, and retreat guest has a place to rest, recharge, and belong.

Along the path, that vision becomes beautifully tangible: new canvas wall tents, sturdy bunks and new mattresses, a welcoming yurt, and updated cabin lighting for families and campers. Each improvement helps bring the Christikon experience to life. You’re invited to be part of this moment—give in any amount or sponsor a full piece of this tiered project—and help us prepare the way for summer on the mountain. Click on the giant button above to learn more, and stay tuned as this campaign develops this spring!

Let’s build what’s next—together. Thank you!

Pastor Heather Wigdahl

Executive Director

## 2026 Camp Rates

### Choice Pricing

Highlands Camp uses a pricing model that allows you to choose which fee you pay. Each camp is broken up into 3 different fees: the Subsidized fee, the Standard fee, and the Supporter fee.

#### Standard

The Standard fee more closely reflects the actual cost of a camper attending summer camp, including expenses people don’t think about such as meals, utilities, counselor and other staff pay, building and grounds maintenance, program supplies, accreditation and permits, etc.

#### Subsidized

The Subsidized fee is our commitment to keeping summer camp affordable for every camper, although it does not accurately reflect the actual financial cost of a camper. This fee is made possible through the generous support of our donors and those paying the Supporter fee.

#### Supporter

The Supporter fee will cover the cost of camp, plus allow Highlands Camp to continue to provide summer camp for everyone. By choosing this fee, families would help further the mission of Highlands Camp by covering the cost of camp for their child and helping us make the subsidized fee available to those who need it.

##### Traditional Camps (4th-6th, 7th-9th)

- Standard: $650
- Subsidized: $550
- Supporter: $750

##### Adventure Camp (9th-12th)

- Standard: $850
- Subsidized: $750
- Supporter: $950

##### Mini Camp (3rd-5th)

- Standard: $500
- Subsidized: $400
- Supporter: $600

##### Family Camp

- Standard: $500
- Subsidized: $400
- Supporter: $600
- Children aged 5 and under attend for free!

### Scholarships

The purpose of Highlands Camp is to inspire the next generation of thinkers, doers, dreamers, believers, and difference makers by fostering a sense of belonging, curiosity, adventure, and connection with nature through a safe and fun camp experience. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to go to camp.

If financial hardship is standing in the way of your camper participating in the Highlands Camp experience, scholarships are available. You may apply for a scholarship on the summer camp application form. Through the support of generous donors, scholarship funds are available to ensure that all campers have the opportunity to experience the magic of camp.

In an effort to help scholarship funds reach as many campers as possible, we do ask families requesting assistance to pay as much as they can towards their camp fee.

Want to share the love of Summer Camp? Donations for camp scholarship can be made [here](https://hpcrc.campintouch.com/ui/forms/donor/Form)

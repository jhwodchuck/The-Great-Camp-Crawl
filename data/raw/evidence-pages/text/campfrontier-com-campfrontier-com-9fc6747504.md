Camp Frontier is Florida’s OverNight Summer Camp.

Camp Frontier is Florida’s premier overnight summer camp—a true sleep away camp experience in the heart of Central Florida. With over 50 activities, Camp Frontier is an exclusive overnight adventure camp for boys and girls ages 7–17, welcoming campers from across the United States and around the world.

Campers enjoy outstanding sleepaway camp [facilities](https://www.campfrontier.com/overnight-summer-camp-facilities) designed for comfort, safety, and nonstop fun, including air-conditioned boys and girls lodge bunk rooms, an indoor gymnasium, and multiple indoor activity spaces. Outdoors, campers can explore a lake, swim in a half-Olympic pool, and take on exciting challenges like our challenge course. Creative and athletic campers love our Performing Arts Center, tennis courts, and fitness center—with even more adventures available every day.

## Sleepaway Summer Camp

There’s nothing like summer camp—and there’s nothing like Camp Frontier, Florida’s OverNight Adventure Summer Camp and sleep away camp experience designed for friendship, fun, and personal growth.

As a private residential (overnight-only) summer camp, Camp Frontier gives campers the chance to unplug, build confidence, and make lifelong friends in a safe, encouraging community. We offer up to 8 weeks of overnight camp each summer, with an action-packed schedule and supportive routines that help campers thrive.

Our [programs](https://www.campfrontier.com/camp-activities) are built on decades of summer camp tradition while intentionally meeting the needs of today’s youth—promoting kindness, responsibility, and healthy independence. Campers come from across the United States and around the world, representing many faiths and backgrounds, and many return year after year.

Camp Frontier is rooted in traditional Judeo-Christian values, a love of country, and a commitment to honor, respect, and faith—while welcoming every camper with care and belonging.

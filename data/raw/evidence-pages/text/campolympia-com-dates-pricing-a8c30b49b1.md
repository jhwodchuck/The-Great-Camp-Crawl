**DEPOSIT INFORMATION**

A $500 DEPOSIT is required to secure your camper a space. Half of the deposit, $250, is NON-REFUNDABLE and NON-TRANSFERABLE at registration. The remaining $250 of the deposit is refundable or transferable up to January 1, 2026. After January 1, 2026, the remaining $250 of the deposit is NON-REFUNDABLE or NON-TRANSFERABLE.

Note that enrollment is based upon availability. Camp will process the deposit once availability is confirmed and camper is enrolled. Deposits will not be processed for campers placed on the waiting list.

**TUITION PAYMENT OPTIONS**

Tuition balance is due by April 1st, 2026 (if enrolled before this date).  Registration after April 1st, 2026 will require full payment at time of enrollment once availability is confirmed.

Camp accepts tuition payments via credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express), check, cash and wire transfer.  Please contact our office for wire transfer instructions at 936-594-2541.

Camp offers several tuition billing preference options:
One-time Payment on April 1, 2026
3 Installments – December 1, February 1 and April 1, 2026
5 Installments – December 1, January 2, February 1, March 1 and April 1, 2026

You may choose to have your tuition payment setup to be automatically billed based on your tuition billing preference or have an invoice emailed to you in March prior to the April 1, 2026 tuition due date.  Also, tuition payments may be made online via your parent account at any time or by contacting camp at 936-594-2541.

## 2026 Cancellation Policy

In the event of a cancellation, tuition fees will be refunded based on the schedule below.

31 or more days prior to the first day of the enrolled camp term: 100% of tuition is refunded, less the non-refundable deposit.

14-30 days prior to the first day of the enrolled camp term: 50% of tuition is refunded, less the non-refundable deposit.

Less than 14 days prior to the first day of the enrolled camp term: No tuition will be refunded.

No deductions will be made for late arrivals or early departures. In cases where a camper is withdrawn upon doctor’s orders, a refund of $100 for each remaining day will be refunded upon request.

Camp Olympia reserves the right to dismiss any camper whose behavior is detrimental to other members of the camp organization. Any camper with weapons, illegal drugs, alcohol, cigarettes, vapes/e-cigarettes or smokeless tobacco while at camp will be subject to dismissal. In the case of misconduct or rules violation that results in dismissal, there will be no refund of camp fees and the camper will not be invited back to camp.

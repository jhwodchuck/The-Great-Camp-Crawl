# FAMILY CAMP

At Family Camp, nestled in the breathtaking Beartooth Mountains, families come together to embark on a transformative journey of faith and connection, creating cherished memories as they worship, learn, and play side by side amidst nature’s splendor. Picture your family engaging in uplifting activities that foster spiritual growth and bonding, from inspiring worship sessions led by passionate speakers to adventurous outings that invite laughter and togetherness. Whether you’re navigating serene hiking trails or gathering around a cozy campfire for s’mores and stories, each moment at Beartooth Christian Camp is crafted to enrich your faith and strengthen family ties in a setting that feels like a slice of heaven on Earth. Join us this August for an unforgettable week of love, laughter, and heartfelt connections with God and each other.

Camp is a place for **life changing relationships and lasting eternal impact**, and of course… it’s a lot of fun!  Ths week is a chance for you to make an investment in your family that is truly priceless!

## Webb Junior Scholars Summer Program

Address:  1175 Base Line Road
 Claremont California

[Send Message](#)

[Visit website](https://www.webb.org/summer "Webb Junior Scholars Summer Program")

Calling all junior scholars! Join us for a summer of discovery at Webb’s Junior Scholars Program for rising 7th-, 8th- and 9th-graders. Immerse yourself in a world of endless possibilities through our boarding-only experience. Choose from five program tracks: Paleontology, Science and Engineering, Digital Arts, Medical Science, and Global Changemaker.

Nestled at the foothills of the majestic San Gabriel Mountains, our campus provides the perfect backdrop for your journey of exploration and growth. Bask in the camaraderie of like-minded peers, challenge yourself academically, and let your passions soar in a safe and inspiring environment. Experience the magic of boarding school life while guided by our dedicated faculty and staff.

Participate in afternoon and evening activities, from adrenaline-pumping sports to exciting outings, ensuring every moment is packed with excitement and adventure. Embark on field trips to Southern California’s most iconic destinations, enjoy the ocean breeze on class beach trips, make a splash in our state-of-the-art aquatics center, and conquer the foothills on exhilarating hikes. Relish the warmth of cozy fire pits outside the dorms and savor the delights of our healthy dining hall feasts.

At Webb, summer isn’t just an escape—it’s a vibrant tapestry of unforgettable memories and endless possibilities. It’s your home away from home, where dreams come alive and the spirit of adventure never fades!

Session 1 is July 8–17; Bridge Session is July 17–19; Session 2 is July 19–28. Tuition per session: $3,225 for domestic students; $3,425 for international students; $450 for Bridge Session.

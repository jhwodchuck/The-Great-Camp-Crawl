## Retreats

### Curate Your Experience — Stress-Free

Cho-Yeh has been providing incredible experiences for youth, college students, and adults since 1947! Our premier retreat facilities are located just over an hour north of Houston, Texas, directly off US-59. With our customizable options for lodging, meetings spaces, and activities, Cho-Yeh is the perfect place to get away. We can help you design a meaningful experience for groups of all sizes!

[Retreat to Cho-Yeh](https://cho-yeh.org/retreats/)

## Our Location

Camp Cho-Yeh is a camp and conference center nestled in the serene piney woods of East Texas, just an hour’s drive from Houston and near stunning Lake Livingston.

[Step Into Cho-Yeh](https://cho-yeh.org/our-location/)

### **Spread the cost of an unforgettable summer; start planning today with Camp Ondessonk’s flexible payment options.**

Your child’s summer adventure at Camp Ondessonk shouldn’t come with financial stress. That’s why we’ve designed payment plans that work with your family’s budget, making it easier than ever to secure your camper’s spot for summer 2026.

### **Budget-Friendly Flexible Payment Options That Work for Your Family**

Instead of one large upfront payment, you can spread the cost of Camp from January through June. Choose the payment schedule that fits your lifestyle:

#### **Monthly Payment Plans:**

- Rate A: $123.17/month
- Rate B: $114.83/month
- Rate C: $106.50/month

#### **Bi-Weekly Payment Plans:**

- Rate A: $61.58 every two weeks
- Rate B: $57.42 every two weeks
- Rate C: $53.25 every two weeks

### **Why Families Love Our Payment Plans**

Breaking Camp tuition into manageable installments means you can prioritize what matters most, giving your child an incredible summer filled with outdoor adventure, lifelong friendships, and personal growth. No more choosing between Summer Camp and your family budget.

### **Reserve Your Camper’s Spot Today**

Sessions fill quickly, and our flexible payment plans make it simple to lock in your preferred dates without delay.

**Ready to get started?** Contact Lisa Bloodworth, our Director of Finance, at [lisa.bloodworth@ondessonk.com](mailto:lisa.bloodworth@ondessonk.com) to set up your payment plan and secure your camper’s summer 2026 session.

Check out Camp Ondessonk’s [Dates & Rates](https://ondessonk.com/summer-camp/dates-rates/) for 2026 Summer Camp.

# Camp Spring Creek is a residential summer camp in the North Carolina mountains offering dyslexic children one-on-one reading instruction plus arts, friendship and fun in the outdoors.

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/572e2bdcd51cd4708373efa6/1566323604760-ZBQUXNHJK3SIQC53KR5X/banner6JPG.jpg)

# Adventure

The beautiful mountains of Western North Carolina offer unforgettable opportunities to connect with nature. Fun and teamwork in the outdoors leads to greater self-confidence for our campers.

[Learn More](/activities-program)

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/572e2bdcd51cd4708373efa6/6359a7a8-f4ab-4975-b76b-bad36befcde7/DSC_5690.jpg)

# Academic Program

One-on-one Orton-Gillingham tutoring throughout the summer helps campers become empowered, independent learners. Camp Spring Creek helps children with dyxlexia learn to love learning again.

[Learn More](/academic-program)

### Our mission

# Camp Spring Creek empowers children with dyslexia to become confident, inspired achievers.

![]()

![DSC_5765.jpg]()

**Our Philosophy**

# We accept students with dyslexia and other language-based learning differences, including ADHD. We strive to get to know each child as an individual and to understand his or her special gifts.

[Learn More](/our-philosophy)

![DSC_5950.JPG]()

**Admissions**

# We accept campers ages 7 to 15 and our camp population includes children from all over the United States and around the world.

[Check out admissions & schedule](http://campspringcreek.org/camp-admissions-sessions/)

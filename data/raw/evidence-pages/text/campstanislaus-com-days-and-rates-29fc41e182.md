## Dates and Rates

### 2026 CAMP RATES

- Resident Camp - $945 per week
- Day Camp - $470 per week
- Father and Son: $285, additional Son(s): $100
- Marine Biology Camp (Morning only): $245
- Water Sports Camp (Morning & Afternoon offered): $345
- Registration Fee - Resident: $30 Day: $30 Father & Son: $20 Water Sports: $20 Marine Biology: $20

- Marine Biology Camp: June 8 - 12
- Week 1: June 14 - 20
- Week 2: June 21 - 27
- Week 3: June 28 - July 4
- Week 4: July 5 - 11
- Water Sports Camp: July 13 - 17

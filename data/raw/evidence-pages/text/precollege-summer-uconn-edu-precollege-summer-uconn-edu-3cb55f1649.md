## Now offering courses at both our Hartford campus and Storrs campus!

#### **Storrs Campus**

A Residential Learning Experience

Students participating at our Storrs Campus experience college at our largest campus.  Students will learn how to manage college life firsthand through college level coursework and career exploration.  Students will live on campus and participate in various extracurricular activities in the evenings. With access to UConn's state-of-the-art facilities, including libraries, residence halls, dining halls, and more, students live the life of a college student within a 1-week on-campus experience.

#### **Hartford Campus**

A Commuter Learning Experience

Students participating at our Hartford Campus experience college at one of our state of the art regional campuses.  Students will learn how to manage college life firsthand through college level coursework and career exploration. With access to UConn's state-of-the-art facilities in the city they will have access to our Hartford library, dining facilities, Hartford area partners, and more.  Students live the life of a regional campus college student within a 1 week on-campus experience.

## Explore. Create. Pursue.

UConn Pre-College Summer (PCS) is a comprehensive and immersive summer experience designed for high school students seeking to gain college-level academic experience, and prepare for the next steps in their academic journey. Offering a wide-range of courses taught by distinguished faculty members and industry professionals, UConn PCS enables students to explore various academic subjects and in some cases, to earn college credits that may be transferable to future institutions. Courses are offered in a variety of fields, including STEM, humanities, social sciences, and the arts. Throughout the program, high school students have the opportunity to attend workshops, seminars, and guest speaker events focused on leadership, time management, study skills, and college readiness.

UConn PCS is open to high school students (age 14-17) from all over the country and world, creating a diverse and inclusive community of learners. Whether you are interested in exploring new academic subjects, preparing for college, or meeting like-minded peers, UConn's Pre-College Summer program is the perfect opportunity to challenge yourself and discover your passions.

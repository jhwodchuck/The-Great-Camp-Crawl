# Session Dates & Tuition

Summer 2026

### It’s Almost Time!

We’re thrilled to announce that **Summer 2026 registration opens November 3, 2025!** 🎉 Spots fill up fast, so be ready to secure your week at camp.

For details or assistance, email us at register@stoneycreekranch.org.

We can’t wait to welcome you! 🌟

#### **Dates**

Stoney Creek Ranch offers 8 five-day, four-night sessions beginning in June and ends the first weekend in August. Since waitlists are common for most of our sessions, we encourage groups to select the alternate session(s) should their preferred option be full.

#### **Tuition**

Stoney Creek Ranch’s mission is to continually expand our outreach, offering more campers a chance to experience a life-changing Christian camping experience. The full cost of the camp is $575 for five days and four nights.

We have wonderful donors and grant supporters who take the camp’s cost down to the scholarship rate of **$175.**

#### **Registration**

Stoney Creek Ranch offers its summer program through group registration rather than individual family sign-ups. We partner with various organizations and churches who bring their groups of kids to camp. Once at camp, campers are separated into cabins based on gender and age to ensure a safe and engaging experience for everyone.

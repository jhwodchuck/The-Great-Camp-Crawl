## Camp Kawartha & Outdoor Education Centre

### Douro-Dummer, Ontario

![](https://campkawartha.ca/wp-content/uploads/birchview-road-facility.jpg)

Nestled in the heart of the Kawarthas, on the south shore of Clear Lake, our main facility is **located at 1010 Birchview Road, Douro-Dummer, ON.**   With over 186 acres of beautiful land with trails, forests, wetlands and 800 feet of waterfront on Clear Lake, our centre provides our guests the opportunity to explore the natural world and learn about the environment .

**Explore our Services:**

- Day & Overnight Summer Camps for Children
- Curriculum-linked Outdoor Education Programs for Students
- Overnight Facility Rentals for Groups
- Corporate Team Building
- Outreach Camp Partnerships with Local Cottage Associations

## The Camp Kawartha Environment Centre

### Peterborough, Ontario

![](https://campkawartha.ca/wp-content/uploads/env-ctr.jpg)

Located next to Trent University’s stunning Wildlife Sanctuary lands, our Environment Centre is **located at 2505 Pioneer Road, Peterborough, ON**. Using innovative green architecture, this day-use facility provides hope for a greener future by showing our community and future generations how we can all live differently and sustainably.

**Explore Our Services:**

- Summer, Winter, & March Break Day Camps for Children
- Curriculum-linked Environmental Education Programs for Students
- Day or Evening Facility Rentals for Groups
- Homeschool Programs
- Post Secondary Eco Mentor Certificate Programs

[Map & Directions](https://campkawartha.ca/location-and-directions/)

[University Admissions](https://ashbury.ca/category/university-admissions/) / [Weekly](https://ashbury.ca/category/weekly/)

*Donna Naufal Moffatt*

- Grade 12 students were busy this week with the U.S. [Common Application](https://www.commonapp.org/) Regular Decision Workshop on Tuesday, November 9 and the new BC Education Planner Workshop for the [University of British Columbia](https://www.ubc.ca/) on Wednesday, November 10. PowerPoints are available on the student One Note Class Notebook: Guidance – Class of 2022.

- Virtual school visits this past week included: the [University of Ottawa](https://www2.uottawa.ca/en) on Monday, November 8 at lunch. Next week, [Huron University College](https://huronatwestern.ca/) at Western will be at Ashbury for a virtual visit on **Thursday, November 18 at 11:50**.

- Reminder: [University of California](https://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/) deadline is on **November 30.**

- [SEDS – Saturday Engineering and Design Sessions:](https://carleton.ca/engineering-design/seds/) Students who take Computer Science and/or Technology classes are encouraged to sign up on this link to attend a Saturday Engineering/Design Session at [Carleton University.](https://carleton.ca/) Students looking at engineering or technology design programs are also encouraged to attend. Day students can make their way to [Carleton University](https://carleton.ca/) campus on their own. Boarding students will be organized with a bus. **Please contact Ms. Bettencourt for more information.**  SEDS dates are **November 13** (Mechanical, Aerospace, Electronics), **November 20** (Systems, Computer, Civil, Environmental), **November 27** (Information Technology).

- All students are in the midst of their applications. Students are reminded to check their Outlook Calendar and emails for meeting invitations.

- I will be visiting the Grade 10 Career Studies Class next week to share early information about university planning.

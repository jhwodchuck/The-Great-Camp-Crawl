## Summer Camp

One-week sessions include an opportunity to try most camp activities. Activities are scheduled by cabin group and camp is focused on the cabin group. One-week sessions are an introduction to resident camp and are open to campers currently in 1st through 9th grades.

Two-week sessions are our most popular session length. In addition to the group centered activities of a one-week session, two-week sessions give campers more choices in their second-week activities. All campers have a special overnight. All campers choose their daily activities during the second week. Each two-week session concludes with the camp dance, awards banquet, and closing campfire. Two-week sessions are open to campers currently in 2nd through 9th grades.

Session Three highlights the spectacular Fourth of July Fireworks in Two Harbors which we can watch from camp. Session Four has our special Chapomania Dance Night with line dancing on the courts to many camper favorites.  Session Five features Color Clash, a multi day event where campers and staff compete on one of four teams for the Color Clash Championship.

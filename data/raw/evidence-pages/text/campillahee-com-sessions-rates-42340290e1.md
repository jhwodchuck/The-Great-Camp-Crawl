Illahee sessions range from five days to four weeks.  Girls come from **thirty different states and several foreign countries** to attend camp.

## junior **camp**

The Junior Session is five exciting days for campers to sample 20 activities with their cabin group.  They choose their own favorites on the last day.  Lots of nurturing and time with the counselor make this a great first or second camp experience for girls entering 2nd to 6th grades.

## two week **and** mini sessions

The Two Week Session for 2nd to 8th graders and the Mini Sessions (two weeks within the four week for 2nd to 6th graders) are popular sessions filled with program highlights, camp spirit and enthusiasm.  They offer a chance to build self-confidence while experiencing a variety of activities.

## three **and** four week

The Three and Four Week Sessions are our culminating camp experiences, serving girls from 3rd to 11th grades.  The community feels like home during the longer sessions as girls have more time to build skills, grow in independence and foster relationships. Camp traditions and inspiring leaders encourage bonding between the older and younger campers.

\*Applicants are accepted for a full session only. Applications require a total of $1000.00 when submitted ($700 deposit and $300 non-refundable processing fee). The second payment of half the balance is due January 15, 2026 and the remaining balance is due on or before April 1, 2026. If a cancellation is necessary, the deposit of $700 is refundable if written notice is received prior to January 15, 2026. The deposit is non-refundable after January 15, 2026. There is no refund made for cancellations after April 1, 2026. Payments are not processed unless we can offer enrollment.

Current campers have until September 1st to re-enroll in their same session.  Returning campers who are changing sessions will be enrolled, as space allows, after the September 1st deadline.  Siblings and campers who have been on the previous year’s waiting list have priority after returning campers.

Balancing camper age groups and geographic representation within sessions are considered along with the date the original application was submitted.  [Contact us](https://www.campillahee.com/parents/contact-us/) with questions about the enrollment process.

Because camp tuition is non-refundable for any withdrawals made after April 1, 2026, you may want to consider [Travmark’s Activity Coverage](https://www.campillahee.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/TravMark-Flyer_ActivityInsurance-Illahee.pdf) which may offer some protection in the event of a withdrawal after the refund deadline. Travmark is a 3rd party provider. **Per their policy, “cancel for any reason” coverage must be purchased within 21 days of the initial deposit.**  [Follow this link](https://www.activityinsurance.com/) for coverage details and answers to commonly asked questions. Click here to [get a quote](https://quote.activityinsurance.com/your-trip/?agentCode=TRA14&campaignId=13). **It is important to read the “fine print” if you elect to sign up for the plan to understand any limitations and how to document in the event of a claim. Questions can be answered by contacting the program administrator at (877)500-1556 and referencing the Travmark Activity Coverage plan.**

When comparing prices between summer camps, it’s important to research the additional costs that some camps charge for special activities (equestrian programs, etc.) in addition to the actual number of days in a session. At Camp Illahee there are no extra charges for activities, laundry or transportation.

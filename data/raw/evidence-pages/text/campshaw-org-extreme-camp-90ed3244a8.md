# Extreme Camp – Ages 7-17

---

Our Extreme Camp program runs for 6 nights and 7 days. Your camper will spend their days participating in exciting Camp Shaw activities, making friends, memories and magic. Campers select from a wide range of camptivities during the session including archery, crafts, music, theatre, photography, nature, sports, zipline and many more. The pool and creek provide a chance to splash around and cool off and are available to all campers throughout the day.

During the July Extreme Camp, we will be offering tent camping for a limited number of campers. Please select “Extreme Camp – Tents” if you would like to register for this option. Campers will attend some camptivities with other campers and some just with other Tent Campers and some meals will be cooked outdoors.

Registration for 2026 is now open [here](https://www.ultracamp.com/clientlogin.aspx?idCamp=274&campCode=swn)!

# Summer Piano Camp

#### June 3-7, 2026


##### About the Camp

Discover inspiration and world-class instruction at the UNT Summer Piano Camp! Join
us for an unforgettable experience at the UNT Summer Piano camp where you will receive
exceptional instruction from concert pianists and esteemed pedagogues. Not only will
you refine and elevate your piano skills through hands-on instruction and real-world performance
experience, but you will also enhance your resume, setting yourself apart for college admissions and job opportunities
all while studying under world-class artists at the prestigious University of North
Texas.

The 2026 camp will feature distinguished concert pianists and teaching artists Adam
Wodnicki, Steven Harlos, Oscar Macchioni, Brad Beckman, Liudmila Georgievskaya, and
Thomas Schwan.

The camp is open to middle and high school students (ages 11 and up) as well as college-age
students of all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced music major,
this camp is designed for you!

Our camp offers daily public masterclasses, private piano lessons, and ensemble lessons
focused on the vast four-hand piano repertoire. Whether you bring performance-ready
pieces or new works you’ve just begun, you will receive personalized feedback tailored
to your current level. You will gain invaluable advice and encouragement and have
the opportunity to work in a supportive, friendly environment all while making new
friends and connecting with fellow musicians.

In addition to lessons, you will participate in workshops, discussions, and lectures
on the essential topics of technique, memorization, and more. You will also hear our
distinguished faculty perform for you! All participants will have the opportunity
to study privately and perform publicly. Public performances will take place in the
beautiful UNT concert halls.

All participants will perform in a recital featuring solo repertoire as well as ensemble
pieces. Friends and family are invited to attend!

With dedicated practice spaces available for all participants, housing and meal packages
are included for added convenience. The camp will conclude with the solo student recital
on Saturday evening and ensemble student recital on Sunday morning.\*

*\*The camp will conclude by 12:00pm on Sunday.*

## Sessions, Rates & Dates

- Our full length Sessions are designed to give each camper a complete Tawingo experience: Cabin and Circle themed programs, All Camp programs, Outtrips, campfires, special meals, Crest work and Crest achievements, the Musical, Library and Sports tournaments, all culminating in our final Banquet/Sing Song/Slide Show.
- Introductory Sessions are designed to give new, hesitant campers a ‘taste of Tawingo’, and spaces in our Introductory sessions are limited. When ‘new’ campers become ‘return’ campers they are required to choose longer or full-length sessions.

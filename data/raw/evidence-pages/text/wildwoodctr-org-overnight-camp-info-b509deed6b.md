# Overnight and Leadership Camp Info

[**S**ign Up For Our Newsletter](https://wildwoodctr.org/wildwood-camp-updates/)

[**Parent Login**](https://wildwood.campmanagement.com/p/campers/login_m.php)

**Overnight Camp** is an exciting Monday-to-Friday adventure for kids **entering grades 3-8**! Over five days and four nights, campers dive into activities like swimming, fishing, archery, makerspace, arts, and nature exploration. They’ll stay in cabins with other kids their age and gender, all while being guided by awesome counselors. Meals and snacks are provided, and **bus transportation is available** with camp staff riding along.

[**Leadership Camp**](https://wildwoodctr.org/the-wildwood-leadership-program/) is a two-week program for youth **entering grades 9-12**. This two-week Monday-to-Friday overnight camp includes lodging, meals, and snacks, and **bus transportation is available** with camp staff riding along. Campers will go home for the weekend on Friday and return to camp on Monday. Participants learn durable job-readiness skills like critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration while they work with younger campers. This is a program that can be repeated multiple times. Campers who successfully complete leadership are eligible to apply for Camp Apprentice (CA) sessions after they turn 15. For more information, visit the [**Leadership Camp**](https://wildwoodctr.org/the-wildwood-leadership-program/) page.

****Overnight Camp costs $850 for one week (5 days). Leadership Camp costs $850 for two weeks (10 days).**
**The week of June 22 (Session 3) is shorter (4 days) and is offered at a reduced rate of $740.****

**Please note that the week of June 29 there will be no camp.**

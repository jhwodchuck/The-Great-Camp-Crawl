## WELCOME TO KAMP!

Kanakuk operates as a fun-filled summer camp that specializes in developing both Biblical knowledge and athletics, empowering kids to greater courage, confidence, and character. Kanakuk Kamps range from 1-week sessions to 4-week programs, offering flexibility to meet the needs of families. We believe that enrolling your kid in Kamp means more than sending them off for the summer—it’s an investment in their future. By developing Christ-like character, learning the value of team, and taking opportunities to try new things, kids make unforgettable memories and build lifelong friendships at Kanakuk. There’s truly no other summer camp experience like it.

# Future Global Leaders on-campus

**Spend an unforgettable summer at UBC**

UBC Future Global Leaders is a pre-university program for high school students ages 15-18 that attracts ambitious, motivated students from across Canada and around the world. Explore your academic passions and interests at a top university, make lifelong friends, and prepare for your future beyond high school.

Our two-week on-campus program run in July and August. It features academic courses and mini schools taught by UBC professors and affiliated instructors at a first-year university level – but without the exams!

Get to know other students as you learn together in small classes and enjoy social and recreational activities on the beautiful UBC campus. Choose to live in a student residence on campus or commute daily to UBC Vancouver.

**Session dates:**

- Session 1: July 6-17, 2026
- Session 2: July 20-31, 2026
- Session 3: August 3-14, 2026

##### What’s included

**Students who do not choose the residence add-on (commuter students)** are provided weekday lunch at the residence dining hall and receive a UBC Recreation Card, which includes free swimming and drop-in sports.

- **Course students** attend morning classes and afternoon university preparation lectures followed by student life activities.
- **Mini school students** attend morning and afternoon classes followed by student life activities.

Students who choose the **residence add-on** receive all of the above, plus:

- Accommodation in a UBC campus residence (single occupancy)
- Breakfast, lunch and dinner daily (weekdays and weekends)
- Airport pick-up and drop-off services
- Weeknight and weekend activities
- Local medical insurance (international students only)

##### Payment

- A $500 CAD deposit is required at registration, or full payment if registering after May 1, 2026
- Full payment is due on or before May 1, 2026 for all students

### How to register

A Family Account is required to register a high school student in a UBC Future Global Leaders on-campus course or other pre-university on-campus program. You can set up your Family Account before you register to save time during the registration process. For support, read step-by-step instructions on [setting up a Family Account](https://lifelonglearning.ubc.ca/howCreateFamilyAccount.jsp?method=load&parentSite=EXL) and [registering for a course](https://lifelonglearning.ubc.ca/howRegisterFamilyAccount.jsp?method=load&parentSite=EXL).

**How to register:**

1. Make sure you meet the eligibility requirements. Please read carefully our [refund and cancellation policies](https://extendedlearning.ubc.ca/registration-info/refunds-cancellations-transfers#future-global-leaders).
2. Add a Future Global Leaders course or mini school to your cart and select the relevant options. If you plan to commute to campus, select the Course or Mini School Only option. You will have the option to pay in full or pay a $500 CAD deposit. **Note: Full payment is due by May 1, 2026. Registrations on or after May 1, 2026 must be paid in full at the time of registration.**
3. When you check out, you’re asked to create a new [Family Account](https://lifelonglearning.ubc.ca/portal/logonFamily.do?method=load&parentSite=EXL) or log in if you already have a Family Account. A parent or guardian needs to set up the Family Account with their primary email address. After the account is created, the parent/guardian adds the student as a family member to their Family Account using the student’s primary email address, which cannot be the same as the parent/guardian email address.
4. In the cart, add the student to the course and complete the checkout process. The parent/guardian will receive a confirmation email with the receipt. The student will receive emails to set up their student account and UBC Campus-wide Login (CWL).
5. After completing the checkout process, and while logged into your Family Account, select New Application to access the UBC Future Global Leaders information package. Please do not fill out the information package until you have registered for a course or mini school. **The information package must be completed within 48 hours of registration.**

**You will not be able to attend the program if you do not submit your completed information package.** You may partially complete the information package and return to it within your Family Account.

**After you submit your information package**

Once you submit your information package, we will send the parent/guardian **a confirmation of registration** that includes:

- Your course selection
- Accommodation details (if you choose the residence option)
- Information you need to book travel and plan your summer

If you are applying for a visa, the confirmation of registration can be used to support your application.

**Student account and Campus-wide Login (CWL)**

Finally, make sure the student sets up their student account and UBC Campus-wide Login (CWL) as instructed in the email. They will need the CWL to access important materials prior to and during the program.

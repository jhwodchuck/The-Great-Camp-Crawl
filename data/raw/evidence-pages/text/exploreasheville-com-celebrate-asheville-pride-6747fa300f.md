# Celebrate Asheville Pride 2025

Join us September 26–28 for Pride in the Blue Ridge—featuring Pattie Gonia’s SAVE HER! show, the Blue Ridge Pride Festival, and outdoor adventures.

Asheville Pride Weekend

## A Pride-Filled Fall Weekend in Asheville

###

Pack your bags and head to Asheville this September for a weekend of Pride, culture, and mountain air. Celebrate resilience one year after Hurricane Helene with unforgettable performances, festivals, and outdoor gatherings. From drag icon Pattie Gonia's must-see *SAVE HER!* show to the Blue Ridge Pride Festival and a community hike, Asheville invites you to honor the season, the spirit, and the people who make this place shine.

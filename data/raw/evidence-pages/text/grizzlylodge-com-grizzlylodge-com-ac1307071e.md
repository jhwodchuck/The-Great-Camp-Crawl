## A Family Tradition Since 1926

Walton’s Grizzly Lodge provides the ideal overnight summer camp experience for children ages 7–15. Located in the Sierras of Northern California, camp life centers around our private lake. Campers choose from over 40 activities including water sports, horseback riding, arts and crafts, athletics, archery and our extensive ropes course which features a 500 foot zip line across the lake.

Operated by fourth and fifth generation owners and directors, the rich history and tradition of Walton’s make it the premier residential summer camp on the West Coast. Since 1926, Walton’s Grizzly Lodge has been providing a quality summer camp experience where memories are made to last a lifetime.

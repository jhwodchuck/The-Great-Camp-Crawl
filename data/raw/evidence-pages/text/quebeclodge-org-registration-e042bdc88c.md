For financial assistance: Quebec Lodge - a registered charity - is committed to ensuring that all children, regardless of financial means, are able to attend camp. Quebec Lodge's Mitchell Fund bursaries are made possible through the financial support of our friends and donors. Bursaries are awarded strictly on financial need, up to a maximum of 80% of camp fees.  Please fill out the .PDF and send to us.

## Raising Strong and Powerful Girls Since 1953

![Blue Skies](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/11062b_f43b98062efe46e183f16141a1a26a2c~mv2.jpeg/v1/fill/w_135,h_90,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,blur_3,enc_avif,quality_auto/11062b_f43b98062efe46e183f16141a1a26a2c~mv2.jpeg)

For over 70 years, Heart O' the Hills has been more than just a summer camp — it’s a community where girls come to grow, challenge themselves, and discover their true potential. Nestled in the heart of the Texas Hill Country, our camp provides a safe, supportive environment that fosters leadership, resilience, and lifelong friendships.

###### We challenge and empower girls to be confident, compassionate, and courageous

![Laughing young women in kayaks and canoes on the river. They are wearing red life jackets and using bright yellow paddles.](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/8a01ea_8ae619551153455d85477c720e50a8e9~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_147,h_105,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,blur_2,enc_avif,quality_auto/Carolines-blog_edited.jpg)

At The Heart, we believe in empowering girls to be confident, compassionate, and courageous. Our diverse programs are designed to inspire adventure, creativity, and personal growth, all while embracing the rich traditions that make our camp a second home to generations of girls.

###### Our facilities are designed for safety, rooted in tradition, and surrounded by beauty

![Facilities_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/e22729_71b7cb2a62ee4f0d9aebee9af4f00ea3~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_147,h_83,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,blur_2,enc_avif,quality_auto/Facilities_edited.jpg)

Our newly constructed cabins, dining hall, and activity spaces were designed with safety front of mind. Campers will experience a summer retreat with spaces that take in exceptional hill country views, far above the floodplain. With office and activity structures dating back to the 1940s, the property offers a rare combination of natural beauty and heritage.

###### We're located in a beautiful hill country setting along the Guadalupe River

![Guadalupe Riverfront_edited.jpg](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/e22729_915a768950c74b62a9d78e4e576edd72~mv2.jpg/v1/fill/w_147,h_83,al_c,q_80,usm_0.66_1.00_0.01,blur_2,enc_avif,quality_auto/Guadalupe%20Riverfront_edited.jpg)

Heart’s new home is along the North Fork of the Guadalupe River, located between Camp Stewart and Camp Waldemar. With 1,800+ feet of riverfront, and 100+ acres, the site offers a unique combination of riverfront access and hilltop beauty. The riverfront lined with Cypress trees embodies the magic that Hunt is known for, while cabins above enjoy long-range views.

## A new chapter begins

Heart O’ the Hills Camp for Girls is opening for Summer 2026 in a new location, under new ownership, and with a renewed commitment to the traditions and spirit that make Heart so special. While the setting will be new, the essence of Heart—the songs, traditions, friendships, and spirit—remains unchanged. Work on infrastructure and cabins is underway with an exceptional construction team, and check out our Updates Page to follow along on construction, see our upcoming events, and find out ways you can contribute.

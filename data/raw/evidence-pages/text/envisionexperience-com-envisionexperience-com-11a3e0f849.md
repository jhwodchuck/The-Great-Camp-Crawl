For over 40 years, Envision—part of the WorldStrides family—has helped students from elementary through high school explore their interests and uncover what excites them most. Our immersive programs are built with educators and industry experts to support career exploration, leadership growth, and college prep. Along the way, students build real-world skills, boost their confidence, and start to see a clearer path toward their future.

Innovative Education. Established Support.

For more than 40 years, Envision has helped students discover what they’re capable of through hands-on, research-driven learning. As part of WorldStrides, the nation’s largest provider of educational travel and experiences, we offer a level of innovation and global support that few others can match. Each year, over 50,000 educators partner with WorldStrides to help more than half a million students explore new places, new ideas, and new sides of themselves.

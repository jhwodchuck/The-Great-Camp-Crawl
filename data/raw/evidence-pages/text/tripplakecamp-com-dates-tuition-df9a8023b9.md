# Dates & Tuition

Since 1911, Tripp Lake Camp has provided girls ages 7-16 a traditional and well-rounded overnight summer camp experience they take with them throughout their lives. Tripp Lake Camp is situated on a sparkling lake, with a mile-long waterfront, surrounded by majestic evergreens. Tripp Lake fosters friendships and fun, the old-fashioned way, in a close-knit atmosphere that truly creates a safe, nurturing haven for our campers.

Tripp Lake campers enjoy a full-summer program that provides every girl an opportunity to expand her horizons, explore new interests, and extend a hand of friendship to other campers. Tripp Lake is not only a summer camp, it is an institution built on teaching fundamental values such as teamwork, integrity, a sense of fair play and respect for one’s self and others.

### Summer 2026 Dates

#### Campers Arrive: Saturday, June 27

#### Campers Depart: Thursday, August 13

#### Tuition: $17,500

#### Visiting Day: Friday, July 24

#### Rookie Day:  Thursday, July 23

We are committed to full-season camping, but in rare cases, we can accommodate families who share our values even when their schedules don’t align with our dates.

If you would like to explore this option, **[click here](/contact)** to contact us. Katie will call you to discuss whether Tripp Lake Camp is the right fit for your daughter and for your family.

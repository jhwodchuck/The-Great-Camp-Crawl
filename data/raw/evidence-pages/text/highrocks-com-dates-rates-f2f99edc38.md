# Dates & Rates

## Learn About Our Sessions

Each summer camp session has unique advantages and attributes, ranging from one to four weeks, and tailored to fit your family’s summer schedule.

[Junior Camp

A one-week program specifically designed as a “best of” introduction to camp. A great place to start for a first-time experience.

Learn More](/dates-rates/junior-camp/)

[Mini Sessions

Two weeks nestled inside our longer sessions filled with program highlights, enthusiasm, and fun. Great for families looking to transition to a longer camp session.

Read More](/dates-rates/mini-sessions/)

[August Two Week

A full camp experience with all activities, trips, and evening programs on a slightly smaller scale.  A great stepping stone. Ideal for families looking to get more out of camp than a one-week session can provide.

Learn More](/dates-rates/august-session/)

[June Session

The best way to start your summer. The June 3-week session is jam-packed with fun, building skills, special events, and a variety of trips. A great next step from our shorter sessions.

Read More](/dates-rates/june-session/)

[July Session

The ultimate camp experience. Longer trips, special activities, and advanced lessons. Four weeks to create meaningful lifelong friendships in our tight-knit community.

Learn More](/dates-rates/july-session/)

[Father-Son Weekends

Weekends in the spring for those thinking about camp and those coming for the first time. A great "first taste" of camp.

See The Options](/future-families/father-son-weekends/)

[Family Camp

Enjoy a fun-filled Labor Day Weekend with your family at High Rocks. Join us for an all-inclusive, family vacation in the Blue Ridge Mountains.

VIew Weekend Schedule](/future-families/family-camp/)

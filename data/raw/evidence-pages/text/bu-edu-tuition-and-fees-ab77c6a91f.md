## Metropolitan College Tuition Rates

In general, Metropolitan College tuition is set according to the number of units being taken, plus any additional fees or course costs. The tuition rates listed below are applicable to courses and programs on campus and online. Note that tuition is different for MET courses taken on campus during [Summer Term.](https://www.bu.edu/summer/summer-sessions/tuition-payment/)

***Please note:*** *Metropolitan College students who register for courses offered by other Boston University schools and colleges will be charged the [*tuition and fees of that school or college*](https://www.bu.edu/reg/registration/tuition-fees/).*

### Part-time Status Tuition Rates (Spring 2026)

Graduate students are charged the part-time tuition rate if taking fewer than 12 units in a semester.

Undergraduate students are charged the part-time tuition rate if taking 12 or fewer units in a semester. (Undergraduates taking exactly 12 units in a semester are considered full-time status, but pay the part-time tuition rate.)

#### Tuition

- **Courses Numbered 100–599:** $567 per unit for 1–12 units
  *unless otherwise noted*
- **Courses Numbered 600–999:** $1,005 per unit for 1–11.5 units
  *unless otherwise noted*

#### Fees

- **Student Services Fee:** $75 per semester
- **Undergraduate Lab Fee:** $200 per course, per semester (non-computer science)
- **Late Fees:** Visit [Student Financials](https://www.bu.edu/studentfinancials/your-bill/payment-deadlines/late-fees/) for late fee information

***Please note:*** *Non-degree students registered for 12 units will be considered full-time if one or more courses are numbered 600 or higher.*

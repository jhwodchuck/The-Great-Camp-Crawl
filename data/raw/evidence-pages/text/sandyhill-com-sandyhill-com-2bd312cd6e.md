### Nestled on 643 acres of unspoiled forests, fields, and beaches, Sandy Hill Camp and Retreat Center overlooks the headwaters of the Chesapeake Bay near the quaint town of North East, Maryland. Conveniently located halfway between Baltimore and Philadelphia, it is well-known for its outstanding [summer camp](/about-summer-camp), its beautiful facility for hosting [large group retreats](/about-retreats), the hospitality of its staff, and now its newest addition - [Eagle Point - for outdoor events and weddings](/eagle-point). Family-owned and operated since 1995, its serene natural setting is ideal for outdoor adventure and relaxation.

There are a variety of overnight summer camps for older kids and teens in Western North Carolina. Many of these camps are offered in sessions ranging from three days up to eight weeks, depending on the camper's age. Overnight summer camps (also called residential camps) offer various activities ranging from whitewater rafting and zipline tours to creative writing, painting, or science and technology workshops. *(Feature image: Camp Pinnacle)*

![Overnight Summer Camps Asheville](/sites/default/files/u377756/CampCedarCliff-body-2.jpg)
*Water balloon toss at Camp Pinnacle, Flat Rock.*

While some parents may hesitate to send their kids to an overnight summer camp, the benefits can be wonderful! Experienced camp counselors can help your child be responsible, make decisions, and engage in their surroundings. The balance of structured activities, adventure, community, responsibility, and fun work together to enrich your child, creating memories that will last a lifetime. The unique beauty of Western North Carolina itself can also make a lasting positive impact on children. For instance, some overnight campers with Greenflash Watersports use a boat to reach an island in Lake James. They overnight there, complete with a campfire.

![Greenflash Watersports offers camping on an island for its overnight campers](/sites/default/files/inline-images/Greenflash-Watersports-boat-and-hammock_0.png)

### Benefits of Overnight Summer Camps

- Independence: Campers manage their own space, chores, and choices
- Self-Confidence: Campers are encouraged to overcome self-doubts and rise to the challenge
- Friendships: Campers develop relationships and find common interests with other campers
- New Skills: Campers develop skills from a variety of activities and experiences
- Gratitude: Time away from home often leads to an appreciation for parents and siblings
- Team Player: Campers work together toward a common goal
- Responsibility: All campers have responsibilities, including leadership roles
- Tolerance: Campers learn to work through conflict with others and allow

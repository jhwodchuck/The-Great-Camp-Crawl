# Methodology

The Great Camp Crawl is a **breadth-first research system**.

## Stages

### 1. Discovery
Use country, region, specialty, directory, and language-specific discovery agents to surface candidate programs.

### 2. Validation
Confirm that each candidate is:
- overnight or residential
- tied to a real physical venue
- active within the last 24 months

### 3. Enrichment
Fill in pricing, duration, ages, grades, language, contact details, operator identity, and taxonomy.

### 4. Rendering
Write one final Markdown dossier per venue.

## Discovery posture

This project prefers over-inclusion at the **candidate** stage and stricter filtering at the **final dossier** stage.

## Language posture

Discovery should preserve English, Spanish, and French source material when relevant. Filtering to English-only can happen later.

## Auditability

Raw evidence pages and discovery notes are intentionally retained so records can be re-checked year to year.

## Current focus

- college-run pre-college residential programs
- programs lasting one week or longer
- records suitable for later filtering around one child’s needs

# Scope

The Great Camp Crawl tracks **overnight and residential youth programs** across the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

## Included

- traditional overnight camps
- specialty camps
- sports camps
- arts camps
- music and band camps
- academic and STEM camps
- family camps
- faith-based camps and retreats
- college-run pre-college residential programs

## Current inclusion thresholds

A final record should only be created when there is evidence for:
- overnight or residential participation
- a specific physical venue or session location
- activity in the last 24 months

## Biases by design

This repository intentionally gives higher attention to:
- college-run pre-college residential programs
- programs lasting one week or longer

## Current exclusion examples

- day camps without overnight evidence
- online-only programs
- venue rentals without a youth program
- stale or purely historical programs without recent activity evidence

# Grounding Rules

These rules apply to every discovery, validation, enrichment, and rendering agent in this repository.

## Mandatory rules

1. Do not mark a program as qualifying unless there is evidence of **overnight** or **residential** participation.
2. Do not infer overnight status from photos, cabins, dorms, rustic branding, or the word camp alone.
3. Each final record must map to **one physical venue or session location**.
4. A record must have evidence of activity in the **last 24 months**.
5. Prefer official sites as the primary source of truth.
6. Directory sites may be used for discovery, but should not be the only source when official evidence is available.
7. Preserve non-English source material when found.
8. Do not exclude a camp at discovery time just because the source is Spanish or French.
9. Record uncertainty explicitly. Do not invent missing facts.
10. Pricing can be blank during early-stage enrichment, but missing pricing must be flagged.
11. College-run pre-college residential programs deserve extra recall effort.
12. Programs lasting one week or longer should be tagged and prioritized.

## Rejection rules

Reject a candidate from final-record status when:
- it is only a day camp with no overnight evidence
- it is only a venue rental, retreat center listing, or campground without a child or teen program
- it has no evidence of recent activity within the last 24 months
- the location cannot be tied to a specific physical venue or session site

## Evidence expectations

For every final record, capture source URLs for:
- overnight evidence
- recent-activity evidence
- duration evidence when available
- pricing evidence when available

## Auditability

Store raw evidence pages and discovery notes whenever practical so the record can be rechecked in later years.
